Confidential Information Sharing Agreement

This Confidential Information Sharing Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Dimše Molerov Velizar ("Recipient") and Duča Grabowsky Peco ("Discloser") on the date of acceptance by the Recipient.

1. Purpose of Agreement. The purpose of this Agreement is to protect the confidential information of the Discloser, which will be shared with the Recipient for the sole purpose of evaluating a potential business relationship.
2. Definition of Confidential Information. Confidential Information shall include, but is not limited to:
* Any non-public information regarding the business operations, financial information, or trade secrets of the Discloser.
* Any other information that is marked or otherwise designated as confidential.
3. Obligations of the Recipient. The Recipient agrees to hold and maintain the Confidential Information in strict confidence and to take all reasonable precautions to prevent unauthorized use or disclosure of the Confidential Information. The Recipient shall use the Confidential Information only for the purpose of evaluating a potential business relationship with the Discloser and shall not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Discloser, except to the extent that such disclosure is required by law or to the Recipient's professional advisors under a duty of confidentiality.
4. Obligations of the Discloser. The Discloser agrees to hold and maintain the Recipient's confidential information in strict confidence and to take all reasonable precautions to prevent unauthorized use or disclosure of the Confidential Information. The Discloser shall use the Confidential Information only for the purpose of evaluating a potential business relationship with the Recipient and shall not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Recipient, except to the extent that such disclosure is required by law or to the Discloser's professional advisors under a duty of confidentiality.
5. Term and Termination. This Agreement shall remain in effect until the Recipient notifies the Discloser in writing of its termination. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any reason, by providing written notice to the other party.
6. Governing Law. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the state of Huesca.
7. Entire Agreement. This Agreement contains the entire understanding of the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior and contemporaneous agreements and understandings, whether written or oral, relating to the same subject matter.
8. Amendments. This Agreement may be amended or modified only by an instrument in writing executed by both parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first above written.
