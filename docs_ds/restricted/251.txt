Proposal for Partnership between Biscot Pharmacy and Elly's Vac Services, LLC

To Whom It May Concern,

This proposal is submitted to explore the possibility of establishing a partnership between Biscot Pharmacy and Elly's Vac Services, LLC. Both parties agree that this partnership has the potential to provide a mutually beneficial opportunity and we believe that the resources and expertise each party brings to the table can be leveraged for a stronger business presence in the industry.

Objectives of the Partnership:

1. To expand the customer base by offering complementary services to existing customers.
2. To provide a competitive advantage by sharing resources and expertise.
3. To increase revenue and profitability through increased sales and efficient operations.

Scope of the Partnership:

1. Biscot Pharmacy will provide access to their existing customer base and distribution channels to promote and sell the vacuum services offered by Elly's Vac Services, LLC.
2. Elly's Vac Services, LLC will offer vacuum cleaning services to Biscot Pharmacy's customers and provide vacuum cleaning services to Biscot Pharmacy for their stores.
3. Both parties will work together to develop marketing and advertising strategies to promote the partnership and its offerings.

Terms and Conditions:

1. The partnership will be governed by a formal agreement that outlines the terms and conditions of the relationship.
2. Each party will contribute resources and expertise as agreed upon in the partnership agreement.
3. The partnership will operate under the name "Biscot-Elly's Vac Services Partnership" or a similar name agreed upon by both parties.

Organizational Structure:

1. A joint steering committee will oversee the day-to-day operations of the partnership.
2. Each party will appoint a representative to the steering committee.
3. A budget will be established and agreed upon by both parties for the operation of the partnership.

Conclusion:

We believe that the partnership between Biscot Pharmacy and Elly's Vac Services, LLC will bring value to both parties and provide a competitive advantage in the industry. This proposal sets forth the objectives, scope, and terms and conditions of the partnership and we look forward to discussing this opportunity further.
