Job Description: Confidential and Restricted

Job Title: Senior Data Analyst

Location: North Pleasant Street, Amherst, Massachusetts

Company: Allen-Bryan

Reports to: Director of Data Analysis

Job Summary:
The Senior Data Analyst will be responsible for analyzing and interpreting complex data sets to support strategic decision-making for the company. This role requires a high level of attention to detail, strong analytical skills, and the ability to communicate complex information to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Responsibilities:

* Analyze large and complex data sets to identify trends, patterns, and insights
* Develop and maintain statistical models to forecast future trends and inform business decisions
* Create reports and visualizations to effectively communicate findings to stakeholders
* Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business needs and identify opportunities for data-driven decision-making
* Develop and maintain data quality standards to ensure accuracy and completeness of data sets
* Keep up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in data analysis and visualization

Qualifications:

* Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field such as statistics, mathematics, or computer science
* 5+ years of experience in data analysis, with a focus on statistical modeling and forecasting
* Proficiency in statistical software such as R or Python
* Strong communication and presentation skills, with the ability to explain complex data to non-technical stakeholders
* Experience working with large and complex data sets
* Familiarity with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI

Clearance:
This position requires a background check and the ability to obtain a security clearance.
