**1. Project Purpose and Justification**
The project involves the establishment of a Maxsimos Techsoft Pvt Ltd satellite office at 30 Jeździecka, Wrocław, 53-032 województwo dolnośląskie. The purpose is to expand services to our growing client base in Eastern European markets, ensuring local support for our products and facilitating quicker response times.

**2. Objectives**
- To provide localized support and sales services to the Eastern European market
- To increase market presence and penetration in designated industries by 35% within the next fiscal year
- To establish a fully operational office by Q3 2023 with a team of 15 employees

**3. Scope of Work**
- Refurbishing and setting up the designated office space
- Recruiting local talent, including five engineers, five sales executives, and five support staff
- Establishing secure IT infrastructure and integration with Maxsimos Techsoft Pvt Ltd's central network

**4. Project Milestones**
- Lease agreement finalization: May 10, 2023
- Office ready for equipment setup: June 20, 2023
- Staff recruitment completion: July 30, 2023
- Official office opening: September 1, 2023

**5. Constraints and Assumptions**
- All project activities are subject to regional regulatory compliance
- Assumption that recruitment for key positions will not face unexpected delays
- The COVID-19 pandemic may pose health and safety concerns, affecting project timelines

**6. Risk Management**
- Risks related to regulatory approvals will be managed by the Legal Department
- A contingency plan will be in place for remote setup of office equipment in case of lockdowns
- Regular risk assessment meetings will be scheduled to navigate unforeseen challenges

**7. Budget Summary**
An initial budget of €1.5 million has been allocated, covering setup costs, staffing, and a six-month operational buffer. Full breakdown available on request.
