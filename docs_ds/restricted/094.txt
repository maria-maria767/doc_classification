Product Specification

I. Product Overview
The Evr Media Spa is proud to present its latest innovation – the EvrSmart Home Hub 1.0. Designed for ultimate compatibility and user-friendliness, the EvrSmart Home Hub 1.0 serves as the central command unit for all smart-enabled devices within modern homes and offices.

II. Product Features and Capabilities

- Wireless Connectivity: Equipped with the latest Wi-Fi and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) technology for seamless integration with a broad range of devices.
- Voice Command Integration: Compatible with leading voice assistant technologies, including those from major market players.
- Security: Advanced encryption and security protocols to ensure privacy and protection of user data.
- User Interface: Intuitive touch screen display offering easy navigation and control.
- Compatibility: Works with numerous smart devices, including thermostats, lights, locks, and cameras.
- Energy Efficiency: Designed with power-saving features to minimize electricity usage.
- Firmware Updates: Over-the-air updates for firmware enhancements and new feature integrations.
- Customizable Settings: User profiles can be created for personalized experiences and controls.

III. Product Specifications

- Dimensions: 120mm x 120mm x 30mm
- Power Supply: AC 100-240V, 50/60Hz
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi IEEE 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, Bluetooth 5.0
- Display: 7-inch capacitive touch screen, 1280x800 resolution
- Operating Temperature: 0°C to 40°C (32°F to 104°F)
- Storage Temperature: -20°C to 60°C (-4°F to 140°F)
- Certifications: Complies with FCC, CE, and RoHS standards.
