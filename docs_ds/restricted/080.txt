Job Description

Position Title: Senior Research Analyst

Department: Market Research

Reports to: Director of Research and Development

Location: Via Carso, Crandola Valsassina, Lombardia

Position Summary:
The Senior Research Analyst is responsible for providing advanced analytical support for market research projects within the organization. This role involves a high-level of proficiency in data analysis, working closely with the research and product development teams to assist in the formation of business strategies and decision-making processes. The Senior Research Analyst will play a vital role in the interpretation and communication of research findings to key stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and execute qualitative and quantitative research studies.
- Analyze complex data sets to identify market trends, patterns, and insights.
- Develop comprehensive reports to clearly communicate research findings and recommendations to senior management.
- Stay up-to-date with latest industry developments and competitive landscape.
- Assist with the preparation of presentations for internal and external stakeholders.
- Work with cross-functional teams to integrate research findings into business processes.
- Ensure all market research activities adhere to ethical standards and data protection laws.

- Bachelor's degree in market research, statistics, economics, or a related field. A Master's degree is preferred.
- Minimum of 5 yearsâ€™ experience in a market research or data analysis role.
- Strong analytical skills with proficiency in statistical software.
- Knowledge of data visualization tools and techniques.
- Excellent communication and presentation abilities.
- Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.

Sensitive Information:
- The Senior Research Analyst will be exposed to sensitive project details and proprietary company data that are critical to the competitive position of the organization.

Security Clearance:
- All candidates must pass a comprehensive background check and sign a non-disclosure agreement due to the sensitive nature of the work and data involved.
