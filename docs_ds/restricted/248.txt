Marketing Plan for Evco Building Solutions

Introduction:
Kiana Flanagan, CEO of Evco Building Solutions, has proposed a new marketing plan to increase brand awareness and sales. The plan includes a focus on social media advertising, trade shows, and targeted email campaigns.

Goals:

* Increase brand awareness by 25% within the next year
* Increase website traffic by 50% within the next year
* Generate a 20% increase in sales leads from trade shows within the next year
* Increase email open rates by 15% within the next quarter

Strategies:

* Social media advertising: We will create and run social media ads targeting our ideal customer demographic. We will also engage with potential customers through social media platforms such as LinkedIn and Facebook.
* Trade shows: We will participate in relevant trade shows to showcase our products and services. We will also offer special promotions and discounts to attendees.
* Targeted email campaigns: We will create targeted email campaigns to reach potential customers and stay in touch with existing ones. We will also use email marketing software to track and analyze the effectiveness of our campaigns.

Budget:
The marketing budget for this plan is $100,000 per year. This budget will be used for social media advertising, trade show expenses, and email marketing software.

Conclusion:
We believe that this marketing plan will help Evco Building Solutions achieve its goals and increase brand awareness and sales. We will closely monitor the effectiveness of each strategy and make adjustments as needed.
