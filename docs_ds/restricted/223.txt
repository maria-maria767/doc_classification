**1. Project Overview**

The Broad Run Veterinary Clinic Expansion Plan aims to increase the veterinary service capabilities in Huesca by establishing a new facility. This Project Plan outlines the approach that will be used to manage the project and ensure that the objectives are met.

**2. Project Justification**

The need for an expanded veterinary service has been identified based on the growing pet population in Huesca and the surrounding areas. This project will provide enhanced facilities, additional services, and improve the overall customer experience.

**3. Project Goals**

- To construct a state-of-the-art veterinary clinic at 98 Ronda d'Amèrica, Vilanova i la Geltrú, 08800 Catalunya by Q2 2024.
- To equip the facility with modern veterinary medical equipment.
- To hire and train a highly skilled team of veterinarians and support staff.
- To expand services to include 24-hour emergency care.

**4. Scope of Work**

- Site selection and acquisition.
- Obtaining the necessary construction permits and approvals.
- Designing the clinic layout and facility plan.
- Construction of the clinic.
- Procurement and installation of medical equipment.
- Staff recruitment and training.

**5. Project Milestones**

- Q3 2023: Completion of site acquisition.
- Q4 2023: Finalization of facility plans.
- Q1 2024: Commencement of construction work.
- Q3 2024: Completion of construction and beginning of equipment installation.
- Q4 2024: Start of staff onboarding and training.
- Q1 2025: Grand opening of Broad Run Veterinary Clinic.
