1. Executive Summary
The marketing plan for Evco Building Solutions has been developed to ensure that the strategic objectives for the fiscal year [Year] are met. 2. Situational Analysis
Evco Building Solutions has gained a significant market share in the [Geographic Location]. However, with increasing competition, Evco must reinforce its brand positioning to remain competitive. The target customer segment for the upcoming fiscal year comprises environmentally conscious businesses looking to reduce their carbon footprint.

3. Target Market Analysis
Primary Target Market: Commercial enterprises located within the Brescia and Forlì-Cesena regions.
Demographics: Mid to large-scale businesses looking for eco-friendly building solutions.
Behavioral Factors: Businesses are increasingly seeking greener alternatives as part of their CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) initiatives.

4. Marketing Goals and Objectives
- Increase brand awareness in the target market by 30%.
- Launch two new sustainable products by Q2.
- Forge partnerships with three industry influencers in the region.

5. Marketing Strategies
- Product Strategy: Develop and promote high-quality, sustainable products that meet the evolving needs of our customers.
- Pricing Strategy: Implement competitive pricing while maintaining a strong value proposition.
- Place (Distribution) Strategy: Expand our sales channels by adding new distributors in the Forlì-Cesena and Brescia regions.
- Promotion Strategy: Execute a comprehensive digital marketing campaign, including social media, targeted emails, and search engine marketing, to reach our target demographic.

6. Tactical Plans
- Design and host a product launch event for local businesses and media in Forlì-Cesena.
- Collaborate with industry influencers, such as Gianni Marty Whalen and Pere Caracoti Coaxum, for product endorsment.
- Create and distribute a series of case studies showcasing the successful implementation of our products.
