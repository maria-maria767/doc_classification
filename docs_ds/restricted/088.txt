Job Title: IT Security Analyst

Department: Information Technology Security Department

Reports to: Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

Location: Crandola Valsassina, Lombardia

Purpose of the Position:
The IT Security Analyst is responsible for the implementation, maintenance, and auditing of the company's information security systems to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of electronic information assets.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:
- Monitor the company's networks for security breaches and investigate violations when they occur
- Install and use software, such as firewalls and data encryption programs, to protect sensitive information
- Prepare reports that document security breaches and the extent of the damage caused
- Conduct risk assessments and security audits, and recommend enhancements to IT management
- Design and implement company-wide best security practices and policies
- Offer technical support and guidance to staff in securing their data and information

Knowledge and Skills Required:
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related field, or equivalent work experience
- Professional certification such as Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP) or Certified Information Security Manager (CISM)
- In-depth understanding of various operating systems, databases, and networks
- Ability to manage, diagnose, and mitigate network vulnerabilities
- Familiar with advanced persistent threats and cyber crime tactics
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills
- Strong attention to detail with an ability to work under pressure

Supervisory Responsibilities:
- No direct reports

Work Environment:
- Office setting in Crandola Valsassina, Lombardiawith occasional visits to satellite locations within the region
- Standard office hours with occasional need for after-hours or weekend work

Physical Requirements:
- Must be able to sit for prolonged periods of time in front of a computer

Direct Reports:
- None

Confidentiality:
This position will have access to highly sensitive company information and is expected to maintain the highest level of confidentiality and discretion at all times.
