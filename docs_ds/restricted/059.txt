JL SEARCH GROUP
INTERNAL AUDIT REPORT
SUMMARY OF FINDINGS AND RECOMMENDATIONS

Prepared by: Grayson Beattie
Date: April 25, 2023

1. Executive Summary:
This report summarizes the findings and recommendations from the internal audit conducted on the financial processes of JL Search Group, located at 98 Alma Avenue Bristol 02809 Rhode Island. The audit was performed with an aim to assess the efficiency and accuracy of our financial reporting, compliance with applicable laws and regulations, and to evaluate internal controls to detect or prevent errors and frauds.

2. Scope:
The audit focused on transactions recorded between January 1, 2023, and March 31, 2023. All financial records, bank statements, invoices, and contracts were examined.

3. Approach and Methodology:
The audit team employed a combination of techniques including analytical procedures, test of details, and observations. Transactions were selected based on materiality and risk.

4. Key Findings:
- The bookkeeping process lacks a periodic review by a senior finance officer resulting in several minor discrepancies that could aggregate into significant errors over time.
- Two incidences of non-compliance with procurement procedures were detected.
- The asset register has not been updated for the last quarter, raising concerns about the tracking of the company's fixed assets.

5. Recommendations:
- An immediate review and reconciliation of the bookkeeping records should be undertaken by Grayson Beattie.
- A compliance refresher training, focusing on procurement processes, should be organized for the relevant staff members by April end.
- Update the asset register to reflect all the changes in fixed assets since the last recorded entry. Pere Caracoti Veronika, Head of the Accounting Department, is tasked to complete this by May 15, 2023.

6. Conclusion:
The findings of this audit require prompt attention to ensure the reliability of financial information and adherence to operational policies. The recommendations should be implemented without delay to improve financial discipline and accountability within JL Search Group.
