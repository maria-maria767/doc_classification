REPORT

Title: Evaluation of Location Suitability for New Branch Expansion

Author: Marley Novačkicoff
Date: 04/07/2023
Prepared for: Paywise Executive Committee

1. Executive Summary
This report provides an analysis of the two potential locations for Paywise's new branch expansion. After considering various factors such as regional economic indicators, potential customer base, accessibility, and security concerns, the final recommendation is presented for further consideration and approval by the Executive Committee.

2. Introduction
Paywise is exploring the opportunity to expand operations by opening a new branch. The expansion aims to increase market presence, enhance customer service, and drive revenue growth. Two locations have been shortlisted:
- 1 Kings Court, Fairmount, 13031 New York
- Division Road rd. on number 636 in Westport, Massachusetts state

3. Methodology
Both qualitative and quantitative analyses were used to assess the suitability of the locations. Sources include proprietary market research, financial projections, and security evaluations. Site visits were conducted, and consultations with local business associations, such as The Armory Show and Mechanical & Electrical Fixings Ltd, were undertaken.

4. Location Analysis
4.1. 1 Kings Court, Fairmount, New York
Economic Indicators: The Fairmount area displays a robust economic profile with a growing middle-class population. Proximity to major commercial hubs offers substantial customer footfall. Real estate trends indicate an upward trajectory in property values, suggesting a viable long-term investment.
Accessibility: Situated near main transport links, the location offers excellent accessibility for customers and employees. Public transport facilities are within walking distance, promoting ease of access.
Security Concerns: The area is monitored by local law enforcement, and preliminary checks suggest low crime rates. Additional private security measures are recommended for overnight asset protection.

4.2. Division Road rd. on number 636, Westport, Massachusetts
Economic Indicators: Westport is an emerging market with a considerable number of small and medium enterprises that could benefit from Paywise services. Economic growth in the region is slower compared to Fairmount, with a more gradual increase in property values.
Accessibility: This location provides customer accessibility via major highways and has ample parking space, favoring clients who prefer to travel by personal vehicles.
Security Concerns: A recent increase in petty crime rates has been noted. Collaboration with local authorities and investment in a robust in-house security system will be necessary.

5. Recommendations
After evaluating both options, 1 Kings Court, Fairmount, New York is recommended as the preferred location for the new branch. The economic vibrancy of the area, coupled with better accessibility and a more favorable security profile, aligns with Paywise's strategic goals for expansion.

6. Conclusion
A decision to proceed with the establishment of a new branch at the recommended location should consider the initial investment costs, potential long-term benefits, and the alignment with Paywise's business objectives. The full commitment to additional security measures and community engagement is necessary for the success of the new branch.

7. Next Steps
Upon approval, a detailed project plan will be developed, including timelines, budget allocation, staffing requirements, and security protocol implementation. A follow-up report will be prepared to review progress and address any arising concerns during the project execution phase.
