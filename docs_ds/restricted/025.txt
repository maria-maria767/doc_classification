Efforts to penetrate the following markets will be prioritized:

- A series of sales training sessions will be conducted to enhance team capabilities in negotiation and closing deals.
- Product knowledge workshops to be completed by all sales representatives by May 15, 2023.

- Monthly progress reports are due on the last business day of the month.
