Marketing Plan for Jump Rewards

Introduction:
Jump Rewards is a company that offers loyalty rewards to customers for shopping at participating businesses. Our goal is to increase customer engagement and drive sales for our partner businesses. The following marketing plan outlines the strategies and tactics we will use to achieve this goal.

Target Audience:
The target audience for Jump Rewards is consumers aged 18-55 who are active shoppers at participating businesses. We will also target small and medium-sized businesses that are interested in participating in our program.

Marketing Strategies:

1. Influencer Marketing: We will partner with social media influencers to promote Jump Rewards to their followers. Petra Manuel Tenney will be our main influencer, with Ander Stamper as a secondary influencer.
2. Email Marketing: L. Curtis will oversee email marketing campaigns to inform subscribers about Jump Rewards and its benefits.
3. Paid Advertising: We will place targeted ads on social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram. Our primary contact for advertising is J.S.
4. Content Marketing: We will create a blog on our website to provide tips and information about shopping at participating businesses.
5. Events: We will host events at participating businesses to promote Jump Rewards and drive sales.
6. Referral Marketing: Customers will be rewarded for referring friends and family to sign up for Jump Rewards.
7. SMS Marketing: Customers will receive SMS messages about rewards, promotions, and events.

Partnerships:
We will partner with businesses such as Floramiata S.P.A to encourage participation in our program. Belgian company bvba will also be a key partner in expanding Jump Rewards to Europe.

Conclusion:
The marketing plan outlined above is designed to increase customer engagement and drive sales for participating businesses. By implementing these strategies and partnerships, we can effectively promote Jump Rewards and create a successful loyalty program.
