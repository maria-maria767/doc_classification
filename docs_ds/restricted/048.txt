MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING (MOU)

Between: Safeway Tyres
And: AVOM Consultants

Date: [Current Date]

I. Purpose

The purpose of this Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) is to establish a framework for collaboration between Safeway Tyres and AVOM Consultants on the development and implementation of sustainable transport solutions. The MOU outlines the roles and responsibilities of both parties and sets forth the terms under which they will cooperate.

II. Scope of Collaboration

Safeway Tyres and AVOM Consultants agree to collaborate on research, development, and deployment of innovative tyre technologies aiming to enhance fuel efficiency and reduce emissions for a variety of vehicle classes. The collaboration will extend to joint applications for funding opportunities, sharing of knowledge and expertise, and the co-development of market strategies.

III. Confidentiality

Both parties acknowledge that during the course of this collaboration, they may have access to and become acquainted with various trade secrets, inventions, innovations, processes, information, records, and specifications owned or licensed by the other party and/or other business information considered to be proprietary and confidential to the other party ("Confidential Information"). As such, both parties will:

1. Maintain the confidentiality of all Confidential Information received from the other party.
2. Not disclose the Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent from the disclosing party.
3. Use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of this MOU and not for any other purpose.
