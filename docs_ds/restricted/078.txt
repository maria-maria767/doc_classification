Project Plan: Expansion of Broad Run Veterinary Service

I. Executive Summary

The Broad Run Veterinary Service (BRVS) is launching a project for the expansion of services in the Sondrio region. This project plan outlines the approach towards increasing the infrastructure and resources of the BRVS to cater to the growing demand for veterinary services in the area. The plan will detail the timeline, budget, key personnel, and risk mitigation strategies involved in the project.

II. Project Justification

With a noticeable increase in the pet population and a concurrent rise in the demand for veterinary services in Sondrio, the expansion project is expected to enhance BRVS’s capacity to serve our clients efficiently while sustaining our growth trajectory.

III. Project Scope

The scope of the project includes:
- The acquisition of new property at an undisclosed location within the Sondrio area.
- Recruitment of additional staff, including veterinarians, vet technicians, and support staff.
- Purchasing new equipment and updating current facilities to meet higher operational standards.
- Implementation of new services such as emergency care and specialty surgeries.
