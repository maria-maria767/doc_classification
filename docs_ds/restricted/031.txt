**Chapter 1: Introduction to Information Sensitivity**
Welcome to Magnum Enforcement Limited's training on handling sensitive information. This manual outlines the standard procedures and protocols for dealing with information labeled as "RESTRICTED." All employees are required to strictly adhere to these guidelines to ensure the continued protection of sensitive organizational data.

**Chapter 2: Definitions of Classification Levels**
At Magnum Enforcement Limited, information is classified into four categories:
- Public: Information that can be freely shared with anyone.
- Internal: Information that is meant to be shared within the organization.
- Confidential: Information that should only be shared with individuals who have the proper authorization.
- Restricted: Information that is highly sensitive and should only be shared with a specific group of individuals who have a need-to-know basis.

**Chapter 4: Procedures for Accessing Restricted Documents**
- To gain access to RESTRICTED documents, authorized personnel must present their security badge and sign a logbook provided by the Security Department.
- Documents must be stored in a secure location when not in use and never left unattended.
- Electronic documents must be accessed through the company's secure network, and two-factor authentication is required.

**Chapter 5: Responsibilities When Handling Restricted Information**
Employees must:
- Maintain the confidentiality of restricted information.
- Share restricted documents only with authorized personnel.
- Properly dispose of restricted documents using shredders or other approved methods.
- Ensure that unauthorized individuals do not view or access the restricted content.
