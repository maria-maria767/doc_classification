**Project Purpose and Justification:**
The purpose of this project is to upgrade the current technological infrastructure at the Atlantgatan office in Helsingborg, Sverige, to enhance productivity and ensure secure operations. Upgrades will include but aren't limited to improved network capabilities, modernized workstations, and the integration of advanced security measures.

**Project Scope:**
The scope of the Atlantgatan Technology Upgrade Initiative will encompass the following objectives:
- Replacement of outdated hardware and software systems.
- Installation of state-of-the-art security protocols.
- Training for staff on new technologies and security practices.
- Minimal disruption to ongoing operations during the upgrade process.
