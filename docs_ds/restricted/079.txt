**EXECUTIVE SUMMARY**

The purpose of this Marketing Plan is to outline the strategies, tactics, and activities that will be pursued by Evco Building Solutions to achieve its marketing objectives in the fiscal year 2023. The plan is dedicated to expanding our market share in the competitive building solutions sector, enhancing our brand recognition, and driving sustainable revenue growth.

**OBJECTIVES**

1. Increase brand awareness by 25% by the end of Q4.
2. Generate a 15% uplift in sales leads via online marketing channels.
3. Strengthen customer engagement and retention by 20% year-on-year.
4. Launch two new groundbreaking products by the end of Q3.
5. Penetrate two new regional markets: Forlì-Cesena and Brescia.

**TARGET MARKET**

Evco Building Solutions' primary targets are large-scale construction companies, architects, and real estate developers, mainly located in urban areas where the demand for innovative building solutions is high. Secondary targets include independent contractors and small-scale builders who value quality and efficiency.

**PRODUCT STRATEGY**

- Introduce Eco-Friendly Insulation Panels and Smart Energy Conservation Systems.
- Emphasize the durability, cost-effectiveness, and technological superiority of our products.
- Tailor product offerings to meet regional standards and preferences in new markets (Forlì-Cesena and Brescia).

**PRICING STRATEGY**

- Adopt competitive pricing matched with value-added services to differentiate from competitors.
- Implement flexible pricing models for long-term contracts to improve customer relations.

**PROMOTIONAL STRATEGY**

- Conduct a series of webinars hosted by Gianni Marty Whalen, our Chief Product Engineer, showcasing product benefits and installation techniques.
- Develop a social media campaign, leveraging platforms favored by industry professionals.
- Allocate resources to Search Engine Marketing (SEM) to increase online visibility.
- Partner with industry influencers for product promotion at key trade events.

**DISTRIBUTION STRATEGY**

- Strengthen relationships with current distributors and identify potential ones in Forlì-Cesena and Brescia.
- Explore direct-to-consumer sales for smaller scale projects via our enhanced e-commerce platform.

**BUDGET**

- Total marketing budget allocated for 2023 is $1.2M, with quarterly assessments for reallocation based on performance metrics.

**MONITORING AND EVALUATION**

- KPIs will include lead generation, conversion rates, sales numbers, and brand awareness levels.
- Monthly reports from the Marketing Director Pere Caracoti Coaxum will assess campaign performance against objectives.

**RISKS & CONTINGENCIES**

- Identify potential risks such as supply chain disruptions or changes in regulatory frameworks.
- Develop contingency plans, including alternative supply chain strategies and compliance adjustments.
