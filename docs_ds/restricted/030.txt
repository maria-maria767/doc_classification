This contract shall commence on the date of the last signature hereto and conclude twelve (12) months thereafter unless extended by mutual written agreement by both parties.

Either party may terminate this agreement with a sixty (60) days prior written notice if there is a breach of contract terms that is not rectified within thirty (30) days after the non-breaching party provides notice of the breach.
