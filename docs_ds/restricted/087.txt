Product Overview:
The SmartSecure Home Lock System v2.3 (hereafter referred to as "the Product") is a state-of-the-art electronic locking mechanism designed for residential properties, offering advanced security through biometric verification, mobile app connectivity, and encrypted access control.

Key Features:
- Biometric Fingerprint Recognition
- Remote Access via Secure Mobile Application
- Intrusion Alert System with Real-Time Notification
- Backup Mechanical Key Access
- Tamper-Resistant Casing and Lock Design

Specifications:
- Dimensions: 150mm x 75mm x 35mm
- Weight: 1.2 kg
- Material Composition: Zinc Alloy, ABS Plastic
- Power Supply: 4 AA Batteries
- Battery Life: Approximately 12 Months (Depending on Usage)
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi IEEE 802.11 b/g/n (2.4 GHz)
- User Capacity: Up to 100 fingerprints and 5 Admin-level Mobile Accounts

Environmental Conditions:
- Operating Temperature: -10°C to 55°C
- Storage Temperature: -20°C to 70°C
- Operating Humidity: 20% to 80% RH, non-condensing

Compliance Standards:
- Electrical Safety: EN 60950-1
- Electromagnetic Compatibility: EN 301 489-1, EN 301 489-17
- Wireless Compliance: EN 300 328
- Biometric Device Performance: ISO/IEC 19794

Installation Requirements:
- Door Thickness Compatibility: 35mm to 50mm
- Latch Type: Single Latch (Deadbolt Replacement Kit Available Separately)
