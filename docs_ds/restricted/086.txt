MARKETING PLAN

Executive Summary:
This marketing plan outlines the strategy for the remainder of the fiscal year for Evco Building Solutions, focusing on expanding our presence in the Brescia and Forlì-Cesena regions. Developed by Kiana Flanagan and Drahos Slutskiy, this actionable plan is designed to increase market share and brand recognition within the construction and home renovation sectors. It includes a mix of traditional and digital marketing tactics tailored for maximum impact in our target geographic areas.

I. Strategic Overview
- Objective: To increase Evco's market share in the Brescia and Forlì-Cesena regions by 25% in the upcoming year.
- Goals: To enhance brand visibility, establish strong relationships with local suppliers, and increase customer engagement through targeted marketing efforts.

II. Target Market Analysis
- Primary Audience: Homeowners planning renovations and small to medium-sized construction firms in Brescia and Forlì-Cesena.
- Secondary Audience: Local hardware stores and suppliers for potential partnerships.

III. Positioning Strategy
- Brand Message: Evco Building Solutions: "Innovative Solutions, Durable Construction"
- Unique Selling Proposition (USP): Offering cutting-edge, sustainable building materials at competitive prices with unparalleled customer service.

IV. Marketing Mix (4Ps)
- Product: High-quality, eco-friendly building materials backed by reliable support services.
- Price: Competitive pricing strategy with flexible payment options for long-term projects.
- Placement: Strengthening current distribution channels and establishing new retail partnerships within the target cities.
- Promotion: Implementing an integrated marketing campaign, including online advertising, direct mail, partnerships, and workshops for professionals.

V. Tactical Plan
1. Digital Marketing
- Launch targeted Google AdWords and Facebook ad campaigns.
- Improve SEO for local search terms related to the construction and renovation industry.
- Develop a content marketing strategy with a focus on DIY projects and sustainable building trends.

2. Traditional Marketing
- Host informational booths at local trade shows and community events.
- Run a direct mail campaign with offers targeting homeowners in the higher-income brackets in Brescia and Forlì-Cesena.

3. Partnerships & Collaborations
- Collaborate with local influencers in the home improvement and green building spaces.
- Establish a preferred supplier relationship with Beltz Lawn & Gardens for cross-promotion.

VI. Budget Overview
- Total Marketing Budget Allocation: €50,000
- Projected allocation: 40% Digital Marketing, 30% Traditional Marketing, 30% Partnerships & Collaborations.

VII. Success Metrics
- Track increases in website traffic, engagement on social media, and conversion rate.
- Monitor sales growth in the targeted regions.
- Evaluate partner relationships' effectiveness through shared business gains.

VIII. Implementation Timeline
- Immediate Action: Begin partnership meetings and digital campaign set-up.
- 1-3 Months: Launch digital campaigns and direct mail efforts.
- 3-6 Months: Evaluate intermediate results and make necessary adjustments.
- 6-12 Months: Expand successful strategies and explore additional market opportunities.
