**1. Introduction**
This document specifies the technical and operational characteristics of the AlphaStream Router 3000 designed and manufactured by Evr Media Spa. The purpose of these specifications is to detail the product's design for compliance, manufacturing, and product validation.

**2. Product Overview**
The AlphaStream Router 3000 (ASR-3000) is a high-performance routing device intended for medium to large enterprise networks. It supports advanced features such as gigabit Ethernet connectivity, Virtual Private Network (VPN) capabilities, firewall protection, and quality of service (QoS) management. 

**3. Technical Specifications**

- **Physical Dimensions:** 
  - Height: 44 mm (1U rack unit)
  - Width: 423 mm
  - Depth: 300 mm
  - Weight: 4.2 kg

- **Hardware Features:** 
  - CPU: Quad-core 1.5 GHz
  - Memory: 8 GB DDR4 RAM
  - Storage: 128 GB SSD

- **Connectivity:**
  - 12 x 1 GbE RJ-45 ports
  - 4 x 10 GbE SFP+ ports
  - 2 x USB 3.0 ports for external connectivity

- **Power Supply:** 
  - AC Input: 100–240V AC, 50–60 Hz
  - Power Consumption: Maximum 150 W

- **Environmental Requirements:**
  - Operating Temperature: 0°C to 40°C
  - Non-operating Temperature: -20°C to 70°C 
  - Humidity: 10% to 90% non-condensing

**4. Features and Capabilities**

- High-capacity, high-performance routing for scalable enterprise networks
- Advanced QoS for traffic prioritization and optimized bandwidth usage
- Comprehensive security features including an integrated firewall, intrusion prevention, and VPN support
- User-friendly management interface with both CLI and GUI options
- Support for IPv4/IPv6, OSPF, BGP, and MPLS networking standards

**5. Compliance and Standards**

The ASR-3000 complies with industry standards including IEEE 802.3, IEEE 802.1Q, and safety standards such as UL, CE, and FCC part 15.
