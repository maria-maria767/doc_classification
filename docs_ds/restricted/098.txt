**1. Introduction**

We are dedicated to creating a productive, respectful, and safe work environment. This handbook is designed to acquaint you with our company culture, values, policies, and benefits. Please take the time to read and understand the contents.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**2. Employment Policies**

_Equal Opportunity Employer_
Anastasia Corporation is an equal opportunity employer and does not discriminate against employees or applicants based on age, race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability, or any other characteristic protected by law.

_Open Door Policy_
Employees are encouraged to discuss any issues they face with their supervisors or our Human Resources department at 4 Boucher Street West Warwick Rhode Island 02893, without fear of retaliation.

_Confidentiality Agreement_
All employees are required to sign confidentiality agreements as a condition of employment. Breaching confidentiality may lead to disciplinary action, including termination.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**3. Workplace Conduct & Ethics**

_Code of Conduct_
Employees are expected to conduct themselves in a professional manner at all times, adhere to our ethical standards, and comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

_Harassment_
We maintain a strict no-tolerance policy towards harassment of any kind. Incidents should be reported immediately to HR at 5281 22 3798.

_Social Media Use_
Employees should use discretion when posting about Anastasia Corporation on social media. Sensitive or proprietary information must not be shared.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**4. Compensation and Benefits**

_Payroll Information_
All salary-related inquiries should be directed to our payroll department via email at n.baric@klanac.biz.

_Benefits_
Eligible employees are provided with a range of benefits, including health insurance, retirement plans, and paid leave. Details are provided upon hiring and can be reviewed with our benefits coordinator at 6543 77 2125.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

**5. Health and Safety**

_Workplace Safety_
The safety of our employees is paramount. Please report any unsafe conditions to the safety officer at Earle Drive rd. on number 174 in North Kingstown, Rhode Island state.

_Emergency Procedures_
All employees must familiarize themselves with emergency exit routes and procedures.
