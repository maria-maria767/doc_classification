1. Executive Summary

This document details the developmental progress of Autoequation Inc.'s new automotive diagnostics tool, codenamed 'Project Viper'. Due to the sensitive and proprietary nature of the projects at Autoequation Inc., the contents of this report are restricted strictly to personnel who are directly involved in 'Project Viper'. Unauthorized dissemination of any information contained within this report is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.

2. Project Overview

'Project Viper' is designed to enhance the diagnostic capabilities of automotive repair facilities by integrating with existing vehicle's onboard systems to provide comprehensive analysis through advanced AI algorithms. The project is currently in the prototype phase and has demonstrated promising results in initial testing. Key components include a user-friendly interface and compatibility with a wide range of vehicle makes and models.

3. Developmental Milestones

- UI/UX Prototype Completion: Successfully achieved as of March 5, 2023.
- AI Algorithm Initial Trainings: Ongoing, with significant progress reported by the Data Science team led by Iliana Chuchkova.
- Hardware Integration Testing: Scheduled to commence on May 10, 2023.

4. Technical Concerns

Several technical issues have been identified by the engineering team that require immediate attention:
- Power Consumption: The prototype currently exceeds the expected wattage, potentially impacting its usability in mobile operations.
- Data Security: Measures to protect sensitive vehicle data during diagnostics are still under development.

5. Financial Report

6. Next Steps

- Complete AI Algorithm Optimization by June 15, 2023.
