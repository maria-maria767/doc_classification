Meeting Minutes

Date: February 6, 2023
Attendees: Belevski, Borčkie, Konečkini, Kirce, Francis

Location: The armory show
Address: 271a Via Bologna, Ferrara, Emilia-Romagna 44122, IT

Agenda:

1. Review of recent sales performance
2. Discussion on new marketing strategies
3. Update on product development progress
4. Q&A session

Minutes:
The meeting began with a review of recent sales performance. Belevski reported that sales had been steady, but there was room for improvement. Borčkie suggested targeting specific demographics through social media advertising. Konečkini agreed, adding that email marketing campaigns could also be more effective. Kirce agreed, but noted that the content of the emails needed to be more engaging. Francis suggested working with influencers to promote the products.

The group then discussed new marketing strategies. Belevski suggested partnering with other companies in the industry to cross-promote products. Borčkie agreed, but noted that the partnership needed to be strategic to avoid cannibalizing sales. Konečkini suggested targeting specific regions through local advertising. Kirce agreed, but noted that the messaging needed to be tailored to each region. Francis suggested hosting events to showcase the products.

The group then discussed product development progress. Belevski reported that the new product line was on track to be released in the next quarter. Borčkie asked about the pricing strategy for the new products. Konečkini suggested conducting market research to determine the optimal pricing. Kirce agreed, but noted that the pricing needed to be competitive. Francis asked about the packaging design for the new products.

The meeting concluded with a Q&A session. Belevski answered questions about upcoming trade shows and industry events. Borčkie asked about the status of the company's online presence. Konečkini asked about the status of the company's partnerships. Kirce asked about the status of the company's sustainability initiatives. Francis asked about the status of the company's charitable giving.

Adjournment: The meeting was adjourned at 4:30 PM. The next meeting will be held on March 6, 2023.
