1. Introduction
Welcome to the preliminary design review (PDR) documentation for Project Falcon. This document provides a comprehensive overview of the design approaches taken in the development of our upcoming product, scheduled for unveiling Q3 2023. The purpose of this review is to ensure all technical plans are feasible, risks are assessed, design criteria are met, and project schedules are upheld.

2. Project Objectives
Project Falcon aims to introduce a breakthrough product that will significantly enhance our market position. Specific goals include improved functionality, 20% cost reduction compared to current models, and regulatory compliance with the latest industry standards.

3. Design Overview
The design of Project Falcon incorporates the following key elements:
   - A new lightweight composite material for the chassis
   - Advanced power-saving features without compromising performance
   - Modular design for easy customization and upgrades
   - Redesigned user interface for enhanced customer experience

4. Technical Specifications
Item  | Description  | Specification | Compliance Notes
---------------------------------------------------------
Chassis | Frame and body | Composite Materials | Meets weight reduction goals
Battery | Power storage | Li-Ion 4,000mAh | Exceeds industry standards
UI | User Interface | Touch Screen 4k | Pending final tests

5. Risk Analysis
Key risks have been identified as follows:
   - Delay in the supply of composite materials could impact production timelines.
   - The current battery prototype is not meeting expected life spans, and continued R&D may be required.
   - Regulatory approval is pending for new power-saving features which might affect launch schedules.
