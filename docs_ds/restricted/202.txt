PROJECT PLAN: "Developing a New Pet Health App"

Project Lead: Dušan Knezevic Jovan
Project Manager: Hristovski

Project Goals and Objectives:

1. Develop a new pet health app that allows pet owners to easily track and manage their pet's health information, including vaccination records, medical history, and care instructions.
2. Create a user-friendly interface that makes it easy for pet owners to input and access their pet's health information.
3. Integrate features that allow pet owners to schedule appointments with their veterinarian, order prescription medication, and get reminders about upcoming vet visits and vaccinations.
4. Ensure the app is secure and compliant with all medical privacy regulations.

Project Timeline:

1. Planning and design phase (2 months)
2. Development and testing phase (4 months)
3. Launch and marketing phase (1 month)

Communication and Collaboration:

1. Team members will communicate and collaborate through a project management platform, such as Asana or Trello.

Risk Management:

1. Possible data breaches or cybersecurity threats will be addressed through regular security audits and compliance checks.
2. Unforeseen delays in development or testing will be managed through regular project status updates and contingency planning.

Approval and Review:

This project plan will be reviewed and approved by the project stakeholders in a restricted meeting to ensure sensitive information is kept confidential. Any changes to the project plan will be communicated to all stakeholders in a timely manner and reviewed for approval before implementation.
