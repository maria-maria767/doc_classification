Purpose:

The purpose of this document is to outline the health and safety procedures and policies implemented at Compagnie D'Exploitation Agricole Ltd. The policies are mandatory for all employees and are put in place to minimize the risk of work-related accidents and ensure a safe working environment.

Scope:

This document applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors of Compagnie D'Exploitation Agricole Ltd situated at Via C. Colombo, Premana, Lombardia.

Policy Statement:

Compagnie D'Exploitation Agricole Ltd is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all employees and to comply with all applicable health and safety laws and regulations. 

General Health and Safety Rules:

1. Employees must adhere to all safety instructions and signage within the workplace.
2. The use of personal protective equipment (PPE) is required in designated areas.
3. Work areas must be kept clean and free from hazards at all times.
4. Report any unsafe conditions or accidents to the immediate supervisor or the health and safety officer.
5. Smoking is not permitted within the facilities or within 15 meters of the entrance.

Emergency Procedures:

1. Fire – Employees should familiarize themselves with the nearest fire exits and the location of fire extinguishers. In the event of a fire, follow the evacuation plan.
2. Medical Emergency – First aid kits are located throughout the premises. Contact the emergency services at 317/163.37.17 and inform a first aider immediately.
3. Evacuation – Follow the instructions of the emergency wardens and proceed to the nearest safe assembly point.

Training:

- All new employees undergo health and safety training upon induction.
- Specific task-related safety training will be provided as necessary.
- Refresher training will be organized annually or when procedures are updated.

Incident Reporting:

- All incidents must be reported immediately to a supervisor or designated health and safety representative.
- An incident report must be completed within 24 hours of the event.
