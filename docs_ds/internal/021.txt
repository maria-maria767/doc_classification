**Subject:** Implementation of New Project Management Software

**Purpose of Memorandum**
The purpose of this memorandum is to inform all departments of the forthcoming implementation of our new project management software, ProManSys, which is scheduled to commence on May 15, 2023.

**Background**
As part of our ongoing commitment to enhance operational efficiency, the decision has been made to transition from our current project management tools to ProManSys. **Implementation Plan**
- **Training Sessions**: Starting April 20th, training sessions will be held for all team members. Attendance is mandatory to ensure a smooth transition.
- **Data Migration**: Department leads are required to oversee data migration from the old system to ProManSys starting May 1st. Support will be provided by the IT Department.
- **Systems Integration**: Any existing tools or software that need to be integrated with ProManSys must be reported to the IT Department by April 10th.

**Expectations from Departments**
- Provide a list of all active projects with detailed descriptions by April 15th.
- Attend the ProManSys feature briefing on April 10th.
- Submit any specific needs or concerns regarding the implementation to the project management office (PMO) by April 13th.
