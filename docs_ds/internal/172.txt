Policy and Procedure: Social Media Usage

Introduction:

This policy and procedure outlines the guidelines for employees of Zidarska, James Orr Smail, Traiko Kocpjan Elisie, and their respective organizations (bms auto haus, Vehicleservicepros.Com, komarek gmbh) regarding the use of social media. The purpose of this policy is to ensure that employees use social media in a responsible and professional manner that aligns with the company's values and does not compromise its reputation.

Policy:

1. Employees are prohibited from using social media during working hours unless it is part of their job duties.
2. Employees must not share any confidential or sensitive information about the company or its clients on social media.
3. Employees must not use social media to make negative comments about the company, its clients, or its competitors.
4. Employees must not use social media to harass, discriminate, or bully others.
5. Employees must not use social media to promote their personal interests or businesses, unless approved by the company.
6. Employees must use their company email address and not their personal email address for all company-related communications on social media.

Procedure:

1. All employees must read and sign this policy and procedure to indicate their understanding and agreement to abide by its terms.
2. Any violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
3. Employees who have questions about this policy or need guidance on social media usage should contact their supervisor or the human resources department.
4. The company reserves the right to monitor social media usage by employees, including their personal accounts, if there is reason to believe that company policies or confidential information have been compromised.
