Policy and Procedure: Social Media Usage for Employees

Purpose:
This policy sets out the guidelines for employees' social media usage during work hours and while representing the company. The purpose of this policy is to maintain the company's reputation, protect confidential information, and ensure that employees' social media activities align with the company's values and interests.

Scope:
This policy applies to all employees of Odysseycz, regardless of their position or level within the organization. It covers all social media platforms, including but not limited to Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, and any other platform used by employees for work-related purposes.

Guidelines:

1. Employees are prohibited from making any defamatory, offensive, or discriminatory comments about the company, its clients, or its competitors on social media platforms.
2. Employees must not use the company's logo, trademarks, or any other intellectual property without prior authorization.
3. Employees must not disclose any confidential or sensitive information about the company or its clients on social media platforms.
4. Employees are encouraged to use social media platforms to promote the company's products and services, share industry news and insights, and engage with clients and customers.
5. Employees must ensure that their social media activities do not interfere with their work responsibilities or commitments.
6. Employees must not use social media during work hours unless it is necessary for work-related purposes.

Consequences of Non-Compliance:
Any violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

Approval:
This policy has been approved by the Executive Management Team and takes effect immediately.
