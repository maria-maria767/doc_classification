Policy and Procedure: Social Media Usage

Introduction:

This policy and procedure outlines the guidelines for employees of Zidarska, James Orr Smail, Traiko Kocpjan Elisie, and their respective organizations (bms auto haus, Vehicleservicepros.Com, komarek gmbh) regarding the use of social media. The purpose of this policy is to ensure that employees use social media in a responsible and professional manner that aligns with the company's values and does not compromise its reputation.

Policy:

1. Employees are prohibited from using social media during working hours unless it is part of their job duties.
2. Employees must not share any confidential or sensitive information about the company, its clients, or its employees on social media.
3. Employees must not use social media to criticize or disparage the company, its clients, or its employees.
4. Employees must not use social media to promote any competing products or services.
5. Employees must not use social media to engage in any illegal or unethical activities.
6. Employees must not use social media to harass or discriminate against any individual.

Procedure:

1. All employees must read and understand this policy and procedure and sign a confirmation of understanding.
2. Any violation of this policy and procedure may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
3. The company reserves the right to monitor employee use of social media to ensure compliance with this policy and procedure.
4. Employees who have any questions or concerns regarding this policy and procedure should contact their supervisor or the HR department.
