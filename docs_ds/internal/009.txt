This memo is to inform you of the upcoming implementation of a new time tracking system designed to replace our existing manual timesheet process. This new system aims to streamline our operations regarding time recording and enhance the overall efficiency of payroll processing. The purpose of this memorandum is to outline key dates and responsibilities for each department as we make this important transition.

**Key Features of the New Time Tracking System:**
1. **Automatic Time Recording:** The system will automatically record time when employees log into their workstations.
2. **Mobile Access:** Employees will be able to clock in and out through a mobile application when working remotely.
3. **Overtime Tracking:** The system will alert managers when employees approach overtime thresholds.
4. **Leave Management:** Employees can apply for leave directly through the system, and managers can approve these requests online.
5. **Reports:** The system will generate real-time reports to assist with project management and budgeting.
