Subject: Quarterly Sales Report

Date: April 10, 2023

To: All Sales Team

From: Radivoje Rybakov Zharko, Sales Director

Hello,

I am pleased to present the quarterly sales report for the period of January 1, 2023, to March 31, 2023. This report summarizes the sales performance of our products and services in the window automation industry.

Total sales for the quarter amounted to $1,250,000, which is a 10% increase from the previous quarter. The top-selling products were the Smart Window System and the Motorized Shade System, accounting for 35% and 25% of total sales, respectively.

In terms of regions, the United States contributed the most to our sales, accounting for 50% of the total. The top-performing states were California, Texas, and New York.

We also saw significant growth in the European market, with the United Kingdom, Germany, and France being our top-performing countries.

In terms of customer segments, our commercial segment generated the most revenue, accounting for 60% of total sales. The hospitality and healthcare industries were the top-performing sectors within this segment.

Moving forward, we will continue to focus on expanding our presence in the US and European markets and developing new products to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

Thank you for your hard work and dedication to the sales team.
