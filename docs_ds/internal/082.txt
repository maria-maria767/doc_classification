**1. Introduction**

This document outlines the specifications for the Bell EcoStream Water Filter (BEWF-3245), designed and manufactured by Peyton Industries. This document is intended for internal use and should not be distributed outside of the organization without proper authorization.

**2. Product Description**

The Bell EcoStream Water Filter is an advanced water purification system designed to provide clean and safe drinking water. It’s equipped with a multi-stage filtration process to remove contaminants, including sediment, chlorine, and heavy metals. The system is designed for easy installation under the kitchen sink and provides a convenient, eco-friendly solution for high-quality drinking water.

**3. Specifications**

- Filtration Stages: 5-stage process, including sediment filter, activated carbon filter, reverse osmosis membrane, UV sterilization, and a post-carbon filter for taste improvement.
- Flow Rate: Up to 1.5 gallons per minute (GPM)
- Filter Lifespan: 
   - Sediment filter: 6 months
   - Activated carbon filter: 6 months
   - Reverse osmosis membrane: 2 years
   - UV sterilization lamp: 1 year
   - Post-carbon filter: 6 months
- System Capacity: 600 gallons before filter replacement is necessary
- Operating Pressure: 40-80 psi
- Operating Temperature: 41°F to 100°F (5°C to 38°C)
- Dimensions: 15” x 7” x 17” (Width x Depth x Height)
- Weight: 27 lbs (12.2 kg)

**4. Compliance and Standards**

The BEWF-3245 complies with all applicable standards and regulations including NSF/ANSI standards for the reduction of specific health-related contaminants.

**5. Installation Requirements**

- Under-sink space minimum: 18” x 12” x 20”
- Standard electrical outlet (120V) required for UV lamp
- Water supply with shut-off valve
- Non-proprietary tools for installation

**6. Warranty**

The Bell EcoStream Water Filter comes with a 2-year limited warranty covering parts and labor under normal use conditions.
