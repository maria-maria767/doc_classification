INTERNAL POLICY UPDATE MEMO

To: All Staff
From: Human Resources Department
Date: April 10, 2023
Subject: Changes to Remote Work Policy

Dear Team Members,

We would like to inform you about the recent updates to our remote work policy, effective May 1, 2023. These changes are aimed at increasing flexibility while ensuring that we maintain a high standard of productivity and collaboration across our teams.

1. Eligibility:
Employees that have been with the company for at least six months and have consistently met their performance goals may be eligible for remote work, subject to manager approval.

2. Application Process:
Interested employees should submit a remote work application to their direct supervisor, outlining the proposed remote work schedule and a plan for maintaining productivity and communication with the team.

3. Equipment and Security:
The company shall provide necessary hardware and software to enable remote work; however, employees are responsible for maintaining a secure and productive home office environment. This includes adhering to the company's IT security policies and ensuring a stable internet connection.

4. Communication Expectations:
Remote employees must be available during core business hours (9:00 AM to 5:00 PM) and are expected to communicate proactively with their team and supervisors. Regular check-ins via video conference are mandatory to ensure ongoing alignment with team goals.

5. Performance Monitoring:
Remote work arrangements will not impact the standard performance review process. However, supervisors will closely monitor productivity and quality of work to ensure that remote work is not adversely affecting performance.

6. Workplace Health and Safety:
Remote employees must comply with workplace health and safety guidelines while working from home. It is the responsibility of the employee to ensure that their home office meets ergonomic standards to prevent workplace injuries.

7. Policy Review:
This policy will be reviewed annually to ensure it continues to meet the needs of our organization and our team members.

Your supervisor will provide further details on the application process and answer any questions you may have regarding this update. We believe these changes will offer additional flexibility while maintaining our commitment to excellence and teamwork.

Thank you for your continued hard work and dedication.

Sincerely,

[Human Resources Manager Name]
Human Resources Manager

Please ensure you understand and comply with the above policy changes. Do not distribute this document outside the company. If you have received this document in error, please notify the Human Resources department immediately.
