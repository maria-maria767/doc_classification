**Job Description**

*Position Title:* Senior Research Analyst

*Department:* Market Research and Analytics

*Location:* Merlan Scientific Ltd., 1 Kings Court, Fairmount, 13031, New York

*Reports To:* Director of Market Research and Analytics

*Effective Date:* April 1, 2023

*Summary:*
Merlan Scientific Ltd. is seeking a diligent and adept Senior Research Analyst to join our Market Research and Analytics Department. The successful candidate will be responsible for providing strategic insights and actionable recommendations through comprehensive market analysis.

*Key Responsibilities:*

1. Conduct research to gather data on market trends, customer behavior, and competitor activity.
2. Analyze complex data sets to identify insights and support decision-making across the organization.
3. Prepare detailed reports and presentations highlighting findings and recommendations for internal stakeholders.
4. Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including marketing, product development, and sales to support business objectives.
5. Utilize various analytical tools and methodologies to improve the efficiency and accuracy of research studies.
6. Mentor and provide guidance to junior research analysts within the team.
7. Stay abreast of industry changes and adjust research strategies accordingly.
8. Manage multiple research projects, ensuring timely completion and adherence to budget constraints.

*Qualifications:*

- Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business Administration, Statistics, or a related field; a Master's degree is preferred.
- A minimum of 5 years of experience in a market research or analytical role.
- Proven expertise in statistical analysis, survey design, and data interpretation.
- Strong knowledge of research methodologies and industry-standard software tools.
- Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to think strategically and provide insight into complex data sets.
