Introduction
This legal brief is intended to provide a comprehensive outline of the contractual obligations and confidentiality standards required of all personnel within Ediciones Urano. 1. Contractual Obligations of Personnel
Every employee at Ediciones Urano, from entry-level to executive, is bound by the terms set out in their employment contract upon the commencement of their employment. As stipulated, employees are obligated to perform their duties to the best of their ability and in a manner that maintains and upholds the integrity and reputation of the company.

2. Confidentiality Agreement
As part of the contractual obligations, employees have signed a Confidentiality Agreement, which rigorously prohibits the disclosure of proprietary information and trade secrets to third parties. This includes but is not limited to unpublished manuscripts, internal strategies, client details, and unpublished financial reports.

Sensitivity Label: "DIFFUSION INTERNAL"
Information designated as "DIFFUSION INTERNAL" is classified for internal sharing within the company only. This classification aims to prevent the circulation of sensitive information outside the organizational structure.
