Job Description: Customer Service Representative

Position Title: Customer Service Representative

Reports to: Customer Service Manager

Location: 1 Kings Court, Fairmount, 13031 New York

Purpose:
The Customer Service Representative will be responsible for providing exceptional customer service to our clients and ensuring customer satisfaction. The role involves responding to customer inquiries, resolving issues, and maintaining accurate records of customer interactions.

Responsibilities:

* Respond to customer inquiries via phone, email, and chat in a timely and professional manner
* Resolve customer complaints and issues in a friendly and efficient manner
* Maintain accurate records of customer interactions and keep detailed notes of complaints and resolutions
* Follow up with customers to ensure their issues have been resolved to their satisfaction
* Collaborate with other departments to resolve complex customer issues
* Keep up-to-date with product and service information to provide informed assistance to customers
* Participate in training and development activities to enhance customer service skills

Requirements:

* High school diploma or equivalent
* 1-2 years of experience in a customer service role
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and handle multiple tasks simultaneously
* Strong problem-solving and decision-making skills
* Familiarity with customer service software and tools
* Availability to work a flexible schedule, including evenings and weekends

Salary: $35,000 - $40,000 per year, depending on experience
