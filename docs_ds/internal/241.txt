Product Specification:

Product Name: EcoFly Hybrid Air Purifier
Product Description:

EcoFly Hybrid Air Purifier is an innovative product designed to improve the air quality in your home or office. With cutting-edge technology, it combines the benefits of both mechanical and natural air purification methods to provide you with a healthier living or working environment.

Product Features:

1. Three-Stage Filtration System: The EcoFly Hybrid Air Purifier is equipped with a pre-filter, an activated carbon filter, and a washable filter. These three stages work together to remove allergens, pollutants, and odors from the air.
2. Energy-Efficient: The air purifier runs on an electric motor that consumes minimal energy, making it an eco-friendly choice for your home or office. It also has an energy-saving mode that allows you to regulate the fan speed based on your room's air quality.
3. Smart Sensor: The built-in smart sensor detects the air quality in your room and automatically adjusts the fan speed to maintain optimal air purification. This sensor also indicates when the filters need to be replaced, ensuring maximum efficiency.
4. Quiet Operation: The EcoFly Hybrid Air Purifier operates quietly, allowing you to enjoy a peaceful environment while still benefiting from clean air.
5. Portable Design: The compact and lightweight design of the air purifier makes it easy to move from room to room, or even take with you when you travel.
6. Manufacturer's Warranty: EcoFly offers a 2-year warranty on all Hybrid Air Purifiers, ensuring you get a reliable product and excellent customer service.

Maintenance and Safety Instructions:

To ensure optimal performance and safety, please follow these instructions:
a. Allow at least 1-2 hours for the filters to activate after unpacking.
b. Never use the air purifier in temperatures above 41°C (106°F) or in environments with direct sunlight.
c. Regularly check the filters for blockage and clean or replace them as needed.
d. Do not use any cleaning materials or solvents on the air purifier's exterior.
e. Always unplug the air purifier when not in use.
