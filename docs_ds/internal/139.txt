Policy

Equal Opportunity Employer
Exittolive is an equal opportunity employer and we welcome applicants from all backgrounds. We believe that a diverse work force is essential to our success, and we do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, physical or mental disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law.

Workplace Conduct

At Exittolive, we expect all employees to conduct themselves in a professional and respectful manner at all times. This includes treating your fellow employees, customers, and vendors with courtesy and respect.

Harassment

Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated at Exittolive. This includes sexual harassment, racial harassment, and any other form of harassment based on an individual's protected characteristics.

Discrimination

Exittolive maintains a workplace free from discrimination, and we prohibit discrimination on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, physical or mental disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by law.

Health and Safety

The health and safety of our employees is of the utmost importance to us. We will provide training on all relevant safety procedures and expect all employees to comply with these procedures.

Leave of Absence

If you need to take a leave of absence for any reason, please contact your supervisor and the human resources department. We will work with you to ensure that your leave is handled in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

Compensation and Benefits

Compensation and benefits for our employees are determined based on a variety of factors, including job responsibilities, experience, and performance. We offer a competitive benefits package to eligible employees, including health insurance, 401(k) matching, and other perks.

Employee Dispute Resolution
If any employee has any concern or dispute with the company, they are encouraged to bring it to the attention of their supervisor or the human resources department. We will do our best to resolve the issue in a fair and timely manner.
