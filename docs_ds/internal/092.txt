Policy and Procedure: Social Media Usage

Introduction:

This policy and procedure outlines the guidelines for employees of Odysseycz regarding the use of social media. It is important to maintain a positive image of the company and its employees on social media platforms.

Policy:

1. Employees must refrain from making negative comments about the company, its policies, or its employees on any social media platform.
2. Employees must not share confidential information about the company on any social media platform.
3. Employees must not use social media during working hours unless it is for work-related purposes.
4. Employees must not use the company's logo or any other branding materials on their personal social media accounts without prior approval from the marketing team.
5. Employees must inform the marketing team if they are tagged in a post that relates to the company or its employees.

Procedure:

1. Employees must review and adhere to the guidelines set forth in this policy.
2. The marketing team will monitor social media platforms for any mentions of the company or its employees and will respond accordingly.
3. Any violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
