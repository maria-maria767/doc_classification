Policy and Procedure: Social Media Usage

Introduction:

This policy and procedure outlines the guidelines for employees using social media while representing the company. Social media can be a powerful tool to connect with our customers, promote our brand and build relationships. However, it is important to ensure that social media usage aligns with the company's values and does not harm our reputation.

Scope:

This policy applies to all employees of the company, regardless of their position or level of seniority. It covers personal social media accounts as well as company-owned social media accounts.

Guidelines:

1. Employees must only use social media in a way that is consistent with the company's values and policies.
2. Employees must not use social media to make disparaging or offensive comments about the company, its customers or its competitors.
3. Employees must not disclose confidential or proprietary information about the company on social media.
4. Employees must not use social media to promote any personal or external business interests that conflict with the company's interests.
5. Employees must not share or forward content that is illegal, obscene or otherwise inappropriate.
6. Employees must use their best judgment when engaging with customers on social media and must always respond professionally and respectfully.
7. Employees must not use social media during work hours except for work-related purposes that have been approved by their manager.

Consequences of Non-Compliance:

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

Enquiries:

Any questions or concerns regarding this policy should be directed to the Human Resources department.
