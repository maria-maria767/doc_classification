1. Introduction
The Bell Superior Wireless Headset (BSWH-2023) is designed to provide users with premium sound quality and wireless convenience for various applications, including communication, entertainment, and professional environments. This document details the technical specifications, design, and functional characteristics of the product.

2. Specifications
2.1 Audio
- Driver Size: 40mm Neodymium
- Frequency Response: 20Hz to 20kHz
- Sensitivity: 98dB +/- 3dB
- Impedance: 16 ohm

2.2 Bluetooth
- Bluetooth Version: 5.1
- Range: 30 meters
- Audio Codec Support: SBC, AAC, and aptX

2.3 Battery
- Type: Lithium Polymer
- Capacity: 600mAh
- Playtime: Up to 22 hours
- Charging Time: Approximately 3 hours
- Standby Time: Up to 300 hours

2.4 Microphone
- Type: MEMS
- Sensitivity: -42dB +/- 3dB
- Directivity: Omnidirectional

2.5 Physical
- Weight: 235 grams
- Material: Memory foam ear cushions and aluminum frame
- Color Options: Matte Black, Silver Gray

2.6 Controls and Connectivity
- Multi-function button for power, play, pause, and call handling
- Volume control buttons
- Active Noise Cancellation control
- Voice assistant compatibility
- USB Type-C charging port
- 3.5mm audio jack for optional wired use

3. Compliance and Certification
- FCC, CE, RoHS
- IPX4 rating for sweat and water resistance

4. Packaging
- Packaging Dimensions: 210mm x 185mm x 80mm
- Package Contents: BSWH-2023 Headset, USB Type-C Cable, 3.5mm Audio Cable, Carry Pouch, User Manual

5. Target Market
The product is targeted towards the high-end consumer electronics market with a focus on sound quality, durability, and feature-rich wireless technology, suitable for both casual listeners and audiophiles.

6. Distribution
The BSWH-2023 will be available through select retail partners of Gravesande Electronics Division and on the company's e-commerce platform. Distribution channels include outlets located in urban centers and online sales with direct-to-consumer shipping options.
