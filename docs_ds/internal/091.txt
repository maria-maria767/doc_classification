Product Description:
The BellMax Pro 3000 is an advanced communication device designed to meet the diverse needs of corporate environments. With an emphasis on versatility, the product integrates seamlessly with multiple telecommunication platforms, offering superior sound quality and user-friendly functionality.

Key Features:
- High definition voice clarity with noise-canceling technology
- 10-inch touchscreen display for easy navigation
- Wireless connectivity with Bluetooth 5.0 and dual-band Wi-Fi
- Compatible with various communication protocols, including SIP and VoIP
- Customizable user interface with multi-language support
- Advanced security features, including end-to-end encryption

Technical Specifications:
- Display: 10-inch IPS LCD, 1280x800 resolution
- Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.0, Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac (2.4G+5GHz)
- Ports: 1x RJ-45 Ethernet, 2x USB Type-C, 1x HDMI out
- Audio: Built-in dual stereo speakers, dual-microphone array with echo cancellation
- Power Supply: AC 100-240V, 50/60 Hz, PoE optional
- Operating Temperature: 0°C to 40°C

Compliance:
- Meets FCC, CE, and RoHS standards
