Introduction
--------------------------------------------------------
The purpose of this Health and Safety Document is to establish a comprehensive health and safety plan for all employees at Compagnie D'Exploitation Agricole Ltd. to ensure a safe and healthy work environment. This document outlines the company's policies, procedures, and responsibilities for health and safety management, risk assessments, incident reporting, and emergency response.

--------------------------------------------------------
Health and Safety Policies
--------------------------------------------------------
All employees must:
- Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment.
- Follow safe work practices and operational procedures.
- Report any health and safety hazards or incidents to their supervisor immediately.
- Attend all mandatory health and safety training sessions.

--------------------------------------------------------
Risk Assessment
--------------------------------------------------------
Risk assessments shall be conducted:
- Annually for all company operations.
- When new equipment or procedures are introduced.
- Following any significant health and safety incident.

Results and any action items must be documented and made available to all employees.

--------------------------------------------------------
Workplace Hazards
--------------------------------------------------------
Common hazards include:
- Operating heavy machinery without proper training.
- Exposure to hazardous substances without adequate protection.
- Slips, trips, and falls due to unmarked obstacles or wet floors.

Preventative measures include:
- Proper signage and labeling of hazardous areas.
- Regular maintenance checks on equipment.
- Routine cleaning and inspections to ensure clear pathways.

--------------------------------------------------------
Incident Reporting
--------------------------------------------------------
All health and safety incidents shall be immediately reported to the supervisor who will:
- Document the incident in the Health and Safety Incident Log.
- Initiate a preliminary investigation to determine cause and prevention measures.
- Notify human resources and, if necessary, appropriate health and safety authorities.

--------------------------------------------------------
Emergency Response Plan
--------------------------------------------------------
Evacuation routes and assembly points are posted in all work areas. In case of an emergency:
- Remain calm; stop work and power down equipment.
- Follow the posted evacuation routes to the nearest safe assembly point.
- Account for all team members, and do not re-enter the premises until cleared by emergency personnel.

--------------------------------------------------------
Training
--------------------------------------------------------
All employees must complete the following training modules:
- General Health and Safety Awareness
- Equipment and Machinery Operation Safety
- Hazardous Material Handling

Training records shall be maintained by HR and updated annually.

--------------------------------------------------------
Responsibilities
--------------------------------------------------------
- Management is responsible for ensuring that health and safety policies are up to date and enforced.
- Supervisors are responsible for ensuring their team's compliance with health and safety procedures.
- Employees are responsible for adhering to all health and safety guidelines and attending scheduled training.
