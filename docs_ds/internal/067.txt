**Policy and Procedure: Handling of Internal Documentation**

1. Policy Statement

1.1. This policy establishes the guidelines for handling, managing, and distributing documents with the sensitivity level "DIFFUSION INTERNAL" within [Organization Name, e.g., bms auto haus].

1.2. The purpose is to maintain the integrity and confidentiality of internal documents while ensuring they are accessible to authorized personnel as necessary for the operation of the organization.

2. Scope

2.1. This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and business partners of [Organization Name] who handle internal documentation. 

3. Definitions

3.1. "DIFFUSION INTERNAL" refers to the sensitivity classification designated to documents meant solely for circulation within the [Organization Name] and should not be shared externally.

4. Responsibilities

4.1. All employees are responsible for understanding and adhering to this policy.

4.2. The Information Security Officer (ISO), currently Traiko Kocpjan Elisie, is responsible for enforcement and providing guidance on the policy.

5. Procedure for Handling "DIFFUSION INTERNAL" Documents

5.1. Creation

5.1.1. All documents marked as "DIFFUSION INTERNAL" must be created using the companyâ€™s official templates.

5.1.2. Documents must be clearly marked with "DIFFUSION INTERNAL" in the header and footer.

5.2. Storage

5.2.1. Electronically stored documents must be saved in designated secure folders accessible only to employees with the necessary permissions.

5.2.2. Physical documents are to be stored in secured filing cabinets in locked offices, with access limited to authorized personnel.

5.3. Distribution

5.3.1. Internal documents may be distributed via company email or the internal document management system.

5.3.2. Email distribution must be limited to within [Organization Name] email domains. Emails should not be forwarded to external parties.

5.4. Access

5.4.1. Access to documents is restricted based on job function and the necessity-to-know principle.

5.4.2. Permissions for access should be regularly reviewed and updated by the department heads.

5.5. Disposal

5.5.1. Documents should be disposed of securely when no longer needed, according to the data retention policy.

6. Breach of Policy

6.1. Any unauthorized distribution of "DIFFUSION INTERNAL" documents may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.

6.2. Suspected breaches of this policy should be reported immediately to the ISO.

7. Training

7.1. Employees will receive training on this policy upon hire and at least annually thereafter.

7.2. Refresher sessions will be provided as necessary to ensure continued understanding and compliance.

8. Policy Review

8.1. This policy will be reviewed annually by the ISO to adapt to any changes in legal, regulatory, or business requirements.
