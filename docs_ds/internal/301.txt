Policy and Procedure for Handling Confidential Information

Introduction:

Zidarska, James Orr Smail, and Traiko Kocpjan Elisie, on behalf of bms auto haus, Vehicleservicepros.Com, and komarek gmbh, hereby establish the following policy and procedure for handling confidential information within the organization.

Policy:

1. All employees must handle confidential information with the highest level of care and professionalism.
2. Confidential information must be protected from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.
3. Confidential information must be shared only with authorized employees who require access to perform their job duties.
4. Employees must sign a confidentiality agreement upon hire and annually thereafter to ensure compliance with this policy.

Procedure:

1. All confidential information must be stored in a secure location with limited access.
2. Access to confidential information must be restricted to authorized employees who require access to perform their job duties.
3. Employees must use strong passwords and multi-factor authentication to access confidential information.
4. Confidential information must be transmitted securely, such as through encrypted email or secure file transfer protocols.
5. All employees must complete annual training on handling confidential information to ensure compliance with this policy.
6. Any suspected breaches of confidential information must be reported immediately to the designated privacy officer.
