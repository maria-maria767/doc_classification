**Introduction**

As part of our ongoing commitment to ensuring the safety and security of our workplace, we are implementing new office security procedures effective immediately. Please ensure the dissemination of this information to all staff within your respective departments. This memorandum should not be shared outside the company.

**Access Control**

1. Updated Badge System:
   - All employees are required to use their security badges to access the building. Any badge not working must be reported to the Security Department immediately.
   - Guests must be registered in advance through the online portal and will be issued a temporary badge upon arrival.

2. Office Lock-up Times:
   - The main office doors will now automatically lock at 8:00 PM on weekdays and will remain locked over the weekend. Please plan your activities accordingly.

**Surveillance Enhancements**

- Additional CCTV cameras have been installed at key points throughout the building to cover blind spots in the corridors and near emergency exits.
- Surveillance footage will be stored for a longer period (90 days), in compliance with privacy regulations.

**Incident Reporting Protocol**

- In the case of any security incident, including unauthorized access or suspicious behavior, employees are to immediately contact the Security Department at phone number: 2500233769.
- A written report via e-mail to n-m@davis-shannon.ly should follow the verbal report.

**Cybersecurity Updates**

- The IT department, led by Stoyan Tsarknias Kirce, will conduct a mandatory cybersecurity training session for all staff on April 19, 2023. Attendance is crucial for enhancing our digital data safeguarding.

**Conclusion**

The well-being of our personnel and the security of company property are paramount. We trust that these updated security measures will contribute to a safer working environment for everyone. Please adhere strictly to these guidelines and ensure your teams are fully informed.

For any questions or clarifications, do not hesitate to contact the COO's office at iris.buric@hotmail.com or via direct line at 1293414713.
