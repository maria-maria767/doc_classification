**Memo**

To: All Staff
From: Letha Andreev, Office Manager
Date: April 3, 2023
Subject: Spring Cleaning Schedule & Instructions

---

**Purpose**:
The purpose of this memo is to inform all employees about the upcoming office spring cleaning scheduled for April 14, 2023. We will be conducting a thorough cleaning of the office premises to ensure a healthy and productive working environment.

**Schedule**:
The cleaning will commence at 9:00 AM and is expected to continue until 5:00 PM. During this time, certain areas of the office will be temporarily inaccessible.

**Areas To Be Addressed**:
- All personal workstations
- Common areas such as the kitchen and lounge
- Conference rooms
- File storage rooms
- Restrooms

**Staff Responsibilities**:
- Please back up all electronic data and ensure that any sensitive information is secured before the cleaning staff access your workstation.
- Remove all personal items from desks and lockers that you do not want to risk being displaced or damaged during the cleaning.
- Label any items that are fragile or require special care.
- Dispose of any unnecessary paper or debris in your workspace ahead of time to facilitate a speedy cleaning process.

**Supplies**:
Cleaning supplies will be provided by Enviro-stewards Inc., our contracted cleaning service provider. However, if anyone has a preference for certain cleaning products due to allergies or sensitivities, please inform the office manager by April 7.

**Special Notes**:
- Letha Andreev will be overseeing the cleaning operations. Any concerns or questions should be directed to her.
- North Arm Transportation is offering discounted services if you need to transport large items home or need assistance rearranging your workspace post-cleaning.
- Please ensure that all sensitive documents are securely stored in filing cabinets. The file room on Concord Street, Gloucester, Massachusetts, will be the last area cleaned to minimize disruption to ongoing work.

**Contact Information**:
For any immediate assistance on the day of cleaning, please contact Panayot Karanov Richard via phone at (07700)900315 or via email at n.baric@klanac.biz.

Thank you in advance for your cooperation and efforts to maintain a clean and organized workplace.
