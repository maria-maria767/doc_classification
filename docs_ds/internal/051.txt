**Summary of Changes**

Effective immediately, the company's remote work policy has been updated to reflect ongoing operational needs and to align with our strategic goals. These changes are crucial to maintaining the balance between flexibility for our employees and ensuring peak operational performance.

**Key Policy Updates**

1. **Eligibility Criteria:**
   - All employees with a tenure of 6 months or more are eligible for remote work, subject to managerial approval.
   - Eligibility will now also include part-time employees who meet performance criteria.

2. **Remote Work Schedule:**
   - Approved employees may work remotely for a maximum of three days per week.
   - Remote work schedules must be consistent week-to-week and agreed upon with your direct supervisor.

3. **Responsibilities While Working Remotely:**
   - Employees must be reachable by phone or email during standard working hours.
   - Attendance in all required meetings, virtual or otherwise, is mandatory.
   - Performance standards and output must remain consistent with in-office work.

4. **Workspace Requirements:**
   - A designated home office or workspace must be maintained.
   - The company will provide a stipend for necessary equipment upgrades to ensure a productive home office setup.

5. **Security Protocols:**
   - Employees must comply with all IT security protocols, including the use of VPNs and secure passwords when accessing company files remotely.
   - The confidentiality of all company information must be preserved in the home office setting as it would be within the company's physical premises.

**Implementation & Compliance**

- Immediate implementation is expected for all employees currently engaged in remote work.
- Training on security compliance will be provided, and attendance is mandatory.
- Supervisors will be responsible for monitoring adherence and addressing any non-compliance.

**Questions & Clarifications**

Employees seeking clarification or having questions regarding this policy update should contact their direct supervisor or the Human Resources department by April 19, 2023.
