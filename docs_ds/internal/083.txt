**2. Purpose**

This Health and Safety document has been created to outline the company’s commitment to ensuring a safe and healthy work environment for all employees, contractors, and visitors at Compagnie D'Exploitation Agricole Ltd. The policies and procedures contained herein are to be followed rigorously and reviewed annually or as necessitated by changes in legislation, operational procedures, or after a health and safety incident.

**3. Scope**

This document applies to all operations at Compagnie D'Exploitation Agricole Ltd, including but not limited to offices located at 57 Avenue Colonel Picquart - Kolonel Picquartlaan, Schaerbeek - Schaarbeek, 1030 Région de Bruxelles-Capitale - Brussels Hoofdstedelijk Gewest.

**4. Health and Safety Policies**

a. Compliance with Legislation
Compagnie D'Exploitation Agricole Ltd will adhere to all local and national health and safety laws and regulations.

b. Employee Responsibilities
All employees are responsible for maintaining a safe work environment. This includes following all safety procedures, reporting hazards, and participating in safety training sessions.

c. Incident Reporting
Any health and safety incident must be reported immediately to the direct supervisor or the designated health and safety officer, Toma Katja Puljak.

d. First Aid and Emergency Response
Designated first aid responders are Jakovčić and Orta. In case of an emergency, contact the internal emergency line at 317/163.37.17.

**5. Training and Awareness**

All new hires will undergo a health and safety orientation within their first week of employment. refresher training will be conducted annually or whenever significant changes to safety protocols are made.

**6. Equipment and Work Environment Safety**

a. Equipment Use
Only trained and authorized personnel may operate machinery and equipment.

b. PPE (Personal Protective Equipment)
Employees must wear required PPE at all times in designated areas.

**7. Fire Safety and Evacuation Procedures**

a. Fire Safety Measures
Compagnie D'Exploitation Agricole Ltd has implemented fire safety measures, including regular equipment checks and emergency drills.

b. Evacuation Plan
Evacuation routes are clearly marked, and evacuation procedures are posted in all work areas.

**8. Risk Assessment and Management**

Regular risk assessments will be conducted by Orta Turundzhov to identify potential hazards and determine appropriate control measures.

**9. Health and Well-being Program**

A well-being program has been established to support the mental and physical health of all employees, with resources and confidential support available via email at n.baric@klanac.biz.

**10. Monitoring and Review**

This document and all related health and safety practices will be monitored and reviewed by the health and safety committee quarterly. Feedback can be directed to natalie-soto@gmail.com.
