1. PURPOSE

The purpose of this Policy and Procedure document is to establish the guidelines for the acceptable use of Odysseycz's company resources by all employees to ensure consistent and responsible behavior within our work environment. Company resources include, but are not limited to, equipment, computer systems, software, electronic data, and communication networks.

2. SCOPE

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and temporary personnel at Odysseycz who have access to company resources.

3. POLICY DETAILS

3.1. Acceptable Use
Employees must:
- Use company resources only for legitimate business purposes.
- Ensure that their use does not compromise company standards or security.
- Avoid the disclosure of sensitive information outside the internal environment.
- Obtain proper authorization before extending access to internal resources to outsiders.

3.2. Unacceptable Use
The following actions are strictly prohibited:
- Sharing sensitive or internal information with unauthorized persons or entities.
- Using resources for personal gain or illegal activities.
- Installing unauthorized software or hardware on company systems.

4. PROCEDURES

4.1. Resource Allocation
- Department heads will authorize the allocation of company resources to employees.
- Inventory control measures will be in place to track asset usage.

4.2. Email and Communication
- Email accounts provided by the company should be used primarily for business-related correspondence.
- Emails containing sensitive information should be marked as 'DIFFUSION INTERNAL'.

4.3. Data Security
- Employees must follow established protocols for data protection, including password management and data encryption.
- Hard copies of sensitive documents must be stored securely and shredded when no longer needed.

4.4. Reporting Violations
- Any misuse of company resources should be reported to the immediate supervisor or the Compliance Department.

5. ENFORCEMENT

Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

6. POLICY REVIEW AND MODIFICATION

This policy will be reviewed annually and modified as needed to ensure its relevance and efficiency.
