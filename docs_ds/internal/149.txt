Please find below the sales report for the first quarter of the year. This report covers the period from January 1 to March 31, 2023.

Sales Performance:

Total sales for the quarter were $5,750,000. This is an increase of 10% compared to the same period last year. Our top-selling products were eco-friendly cleaning solutions, which accounted for 35% of total sales, followed by organic food products at 25%. We also saw a significant increase in sales of solar-powered devices, which generated 15% of total revenue.

Sales by Region:

The West Coast was the best-performing region, generating 40% of total sales. The South followed closely with 35% of total sales, and the Midwest contributed 25%.

Sales by Channel:

Online sales channels generated 60% of total revenue, while offline sales channels, such as retail stores and markets, accounted for the remaining 40%.

Going forward, we will focus on expanding our online sales channels and increasing marketing efforts in the Midwest region.
