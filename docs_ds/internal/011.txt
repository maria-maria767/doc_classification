TO: All Departments
FROM: Kotsios, Operations Manager

SUBJECT: Implementation of New Inventory Management System

ATTENTION: All Department Heads and Inventory Supervisors

As part of our ongoing commitment to improve operational efficiency within Del buono gazerwitz limited, we are implementing a new inventory management system designed to streamline our current processes and enhance accuracy in stock level maintenance. This initiative is expected to significantly reduce human error and save time in inventory tracking and management.

I. Implementation Schedule:
The rollout of the new system will commence on May 1, 2023, beginning with training sessions for the Inventory Supervisors from each department. The schedule for training sessions will be communicated to you by Spas Hospod Ricardo, our IT Manager, by April 15, 2023.

II. Training:
Comprehensive training will be provided to all users of the system to ensure a smooth transition. Please ensure that your department's Inventory Supervisors are available to attend these mandatory training sessions:

1. Technical Training – Understanding system infrastructure and backend operations.
2. Operational Training – Hands-on instructions on daily use, reporting, and troubleshooting.

III. System Features:
The system will include features such as:

1. Real-time inventory tracking
2. Automated reorder alerts
3. Data analytics for inventory forecasts
4. Integration with current database systems of Umaqia agency

IV. Responsibilities:
Department Heads are responsible for ensuring that their team complies with the training schedule and post-implementation procedures. Inventory Supervisors will be expected to cascade the relevant information and oversee the adoption of the system within their respective areas.
