**JOB DESCRIPTION**

**1. Position Title:** 
Junior Research Analyst

**2. Department:**
Research and Development

**3. Reports to:** 
Senior Research Analyst

**4. Position Overview:**
The Junior Research Analyst is responsible for providing support to the Research and Development team by conducting data compilation, analysis, and contributing to innovative solutions. The role requires strong analytical skills and the ability to work collaboratively on several projects within specified deadlines.

**5. Key Responsibilities:**
- Assist in the collection and analysis of data for ongoing research projects.
- Support team members in the development of project frameworks and methodologies.
- Prepare reports and presentations on research findings for internal use.
- Contribute to the formulation of research-based recommendations for the improvement of products or processes.
- Ensure compliance with internal protocols and regulations during research activities.
- Participate in regular department meetings to provide updates on assigned tasks.
- Engage with relevant stakeholders to gather necessary information for research assignments.
- Maintain an organized record of all research activities in alignment with company's data management policies.
- Conduct literature reviews and keep abreast with latest industry trends and developments.
