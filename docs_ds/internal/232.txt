Policies and Procedures
As an employee of Exittolive, you are expected to uphold the highest standards of professionalism and ethical conduct. This includes following company policies and procedures, as well as complying with all applicable laws and regulations.

Paid Time Off (PTO)
Exittolive offers a generous PTO policy to all employees. PTO can be used for vacation, sick leave, or personal days. The amount of PTO you are eligible for is based on your length of service with the company.

Health and Safety
The health and safety of our employees is of utmost importance to Exittolive. We are committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all employees. All employees are required to follow safety guidelines and procedures, and to report any safety hazards or concerns to their supervisor immediately.

Diversity and Inclusion
Exittolive is an equal opportunity employer and is committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace. We value the unique backgrounds and perspectives that our employees bring to the table, and we believe that a diverse workforce leads to greater innovation and success.
