**EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK**
-----------------

**Welcome to Mining Safer**

Welcome! We're thrilled to have you as part of the Mining Safer team. This handbook is designed to provide you with an overview of our company policies, work ethics, and culture. Please read it carefully to understand our shared duties and responsibilities.

**Our Vision**

Mining Safer strives to be an industry leader in safety, efficiency, and innovation in mining operations across the globe. We are committed to providing an environment that fosters personal growth and mutual respect.

**Code of Conduct**

- Respect for all: Treat colleagues and clients with respect, regardless of their role or background.
- Integrity: Always act with honesty and integrity.
- Confidentiality: Safeguard the company's confidential information zealously.
- Compliance: Follow all laws, regulations, and company policies.

**Employment Policies**

- Equal Opportunity Employer: We are committed to providing a non-discriminatory work environment.
- Harassment-Free Workplace: Harassment of any kind is unacceptable and will be dealt with sternly.
- Drug and Alcohol Policy: All employees must adhere to our strict no drug or alcohol policy while on duty.

**Work Hours and Time Off**

- Work Schedule: Standard work hours are from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM with an hour lunch break.
- Overtime: Must be approved in advance by your supervisor.
- Vacation: Employees are eligible for paid vacation time, accruing at a rate that corresponds with tenure.

**Compensation and Benefits**

- Salary: Salaries are competitive and commensurate with experience and position.
- Benefits: Benefits package includes healthcare, dental, vision, and retirement plans.
- Bonus System: Performance-based bonuses are awarded annually.

**Health and Safety**

- Safety Training: All employees receive regular safety training relevant to their position.
- Emergency Procedures: Familiarize yourself with the emergency exits and procedures provided during orientation.
- Reporting Incidents: All incidents must be reported to the safety officer immediately.

**Technology and Data Security**

- Acceptable Use: Company technology and devices should be used for business purposes only.
- Data Security: Employees are responsible for ensuring that sensitive company data is kept secure at all times.

**Dress Code**

- Professional Attire: Employees should dress appropriately for their role within the company.
- Safety Gear: Personal protective equipment must be worn on-site without exception.

- Notice Period: Employees must provide a minimum of two weeksâ€™ notice when voluntarily resigning.
- Exit Interview: Departing employees will be asked to participate in an exit interview to provide feedback on their experience at Mining Safer.

**Acknowledgment**

By remaining in our employment, you acknowledge that you have received, read, and understand these policies. You accept to abide by the handbook's guidelines as a condition of your continued employment.

Thank you for joining Mining Safer. Together, let's work towards continued excellence and safety in all our endeavors.
