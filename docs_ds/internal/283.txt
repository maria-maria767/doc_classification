Subject: New Sales Strategy for Window Automation Industry Srl

Dear Employees,

As we continue to expand our business in the window automation industry, it is important that we adapt our sales strategy to meet the changing needs of our customers. After careful consideration and analysis, we have developed a new sales strategy that will help us stay ahead of the competition and increase our market share.

The new sales strategy includes the following key components:

1. Focus on customer needs: We will work closely with our customers to understand their specific needs and tailor our products and services to meet those needs.
2. Increase our online presence: We will invest in digital marketing and social media to increase our visibility and reach a wider audience.
3. Develop strategic partnerships: We will seek out partnerships with other companies in the industry to expand our offerings and reach new customers.
4. Enhance our customer service: We will provide exceptional customer service to build strong relationships with our customers and ensure their satisfaction.

It is important that all employees are familiar with this new sales strategy and work to implement it in their daily activities. We will be providing training and support to ensure that everyone is equipped to succeed.

Thank you for your dedication to our company and for your commitment to helping us achieve our goals.

Sincerely,

Radivoje Rybakov Zharko
CEO, Window Automation Industry Srl
