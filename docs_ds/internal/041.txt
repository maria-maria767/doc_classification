**MEMORANDUM**

To: All Department Heads  
From: Jane Doe, Chief Operating Officer  
Date: April 1, 2023  
Subject: Policy Update on Remote Work Equipment Reimbursements

-----------------------------------------------------------------------

**Introduction**

The purpose of this memorandum is to inform you of the updates made to our company's remote work equipment reimbursement policy. These amendments have been made to ensure that our team members can work efficiently from home while maintaining clear guidelines for company reimbursements.

**Policy Updates**

- **Eligibility**: Only full-time employees who have been with the company for more than six months and have received approval from their direct supervisor are eligible for remote work equipment reimbursements.

- **Allowable Equipment**: Reimbursable items include ergonomic office chairs, desks, computer monitors, keyboards, mice, and necessary computer peripherals. All equipment must be essential for job performance and must be pre-approved by the IT department.

- **Reimbursement Limit**: The company will reimburse up to $500 for eligible equipment. Please note that this is a lifetime limit per employee.

- **Receipt Submission**: Employees are required to submit itemized receipts along with a completed reimbursement form to the Finance Department within 30 days of purchase.

- **Non-Eligible Items**: Equipment that is not directly related to work activities, including but not limited to gaming accessories, personal laptops, tablets (unless otherwise approved for mobile work), and software not pre-approved by IT.

- **Purchase of Tech Gadgets**: Tech gadgets considered non-essential, such as smartwatches, fitness trackers, or similar devices, will not be reimbursed under any circumstances.

**Procedure**

Employees seeking reimbursement should follow these steps:  
1. Obtain pre-approval for the equipment from your direct supervisor and the IT department.  
2. Purchase the item(s) using personal funds.  
3. Submit itemized receipts and the reimbursement form to the Finance Department.  

**Compliance**

Failure to adhere to the above guidelines may result in a denial of reimbursement and/or disciplinary action. It's crucial that all purchases are compliant with company policy and are made in the interest of professional necessity.

**Action**

Department heads are requested to communicate this policy update to their team members and ensure that all remote employees understand the process to claim reimbursement for remote work equipment.

Please direct any questions or concerns regarding this policy or its implementation to the Human Resources Department.

**Conclusion**

We appreciate your cooperation in implementing this updated policy and believe that it will help to streamline processes and provide clarity for remote work equipment purchases. Our goal is to support our employees while maintaining responsible financial practices.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.
