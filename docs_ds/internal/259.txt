Dear Team,

As part of our ongoing commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility, we are implementing a new recycling protocol effective immediately. This document outlines the changes and your responsibilities in ensuring compliance within your respective departments.

1. Paper Waste Management:
   - All used paper is to be deposited in the blue recycling bins.
   - Confidential documents must be shredded before recycling.

2. Plastic and Metal Recycling:
   - Rinse any plastic or metal item before placing it in the green recycling bins.
   - No plastic bags or non-recyclable plastics in the green bins.

3. Electronic Waste:
   - Deposit used batteries, ink cartridges, and small electronics in the designated e-waste storage area located at East Shore Road rd. on number 199 in Swanzey, New Hampshire state.
   - Arrange for proper disposal through our approved e-waste recycling partner, leepson bounds entertainment inc.

4. Organics:
   - Organic waste, including food scraps and compostable materials, should be placed in the brown compost bins.
   - Avoid contamination with non-organic material.
