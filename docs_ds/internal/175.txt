Policies and Procedures

At Klara Nora Brooks Ingram and Borče Konečni Joao Farrow Norman, we are dedicated to maintaining a safe, respectful, and inclusive workplace for all our employees. The following policies and procedures are in place to ensure that everyone has a positive experience while working with us:

* Anti-Discrimination Policy: We are committed to providing equal opportunities to all employees and applicants for employment without regard to race, color, religion, national origin, age, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability, veteran status, or any other protected characteristic.
* Harassment Policy: We do not tolerate any form of harassment in the workplace. If you experience or witness harassment, please report it to your supervisor or HR representative immediately.
* Health and Safety Policy: We take the health and safety of our employees very seriously. All employees are required to follow safety procedures and wear appropriate personal protective equipment.
* Time Off Policy: We offer a variety of time off options, including vacation, sick leave, and personal days. Please review the policy for specific details on eligibility and how to request time off.
* Dress Code Policy: We have a professional dress code that all employees are expected to follow. The dress code is designed to promote a professional and respectful work environment.
