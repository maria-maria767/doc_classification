**Introduction:**

The Flack Wireless Earbuds Model XB90 are designed to deliver superior audio quality and cutting-edge wireless connectivity to the consumer electronics market. This document specifies the technical and aesthetic characteristics of the product that must be adhered to for manufacturing and QC processes.

**Technical Specifications:**

- **Product Code:** XB90-475
- **Bluetooth Version:** 5.1
- **Effective Range:** Up to 15 meters without obstacles
- **Battery Life:** 
  - Talk time: Up to 7 hours
  - Music play time: Up to 6 hours
  - Standby time: Up to 150 hours
- **Charging Time:** Approximately 1.5 hours for earbuds, 2 hours for charging case
- **Driver Size:** 10mm
- **Frequency Response:** 20Hz - 20kHz
- **Water Resistance:** IPX4 rated (splash-resistant)
- **Noise Cancellation:** Passive noise isolation with optional active noise cancellation (ANC) at additional cost
- **Weight:** Earbuds: 5g each, Charging case: 50g

**Materials:**

- **Earbud Caps:** Medical-grade silicone
- **Housing:** High-density polymer with matte finish
- **Charge Case:** Matching high-density polymer with soft-touch coating

**Color Options:**

- **Default:** Matte Black
- **Other Options:** Pearl White, Midnight Blue, Rose Gold (custom colors available for bulk orders over 5,000 units)

**Packaging:**

- **Box Dimensions:** 100mm x 100mm x 45mm
- **Contents:** Earbuds, Charging Case, USB-C Charging Cable, User Manual, Warranty Card

**Regulatory Compliance:**

- CE Certified
- FCC ID: QO3-XB9050
- Bluetooth SIG Certified
- RoHS Compliant

**Quality Assurance:**

- **Burn-In Test Duration:** 48 hours for each unit
- **Drop Test:** From 1.5 meters onto concrete surface, three times each axis
- **Water Resistance Test:** 5 minutes under low-pressure water jets
- **Sound Quality Inspection:** Every unit tested to match preset EQ curves

**Warranty Period:** 

- 24 months, covering manufacturer defects. Does not cover user-inflicted damage or normal wear and tear.

**Target Markets:**

- Aimed at mid to high-end consumer electronics market
- Primary focus on users aged 18-35 who prioritize audio quality and the latest wireless technology
