Employment Policies

At Jill Crawford, Lcsw, Llc, we strive to provide a fair and equal employment opportunity for all employees. All employment decisions, including hiring, promotions, and terminations, are made based on job-related factors.

All employees are expected to:

* Perform their job duties to the best of their abilities and in a timely manner
* Follow company policies and procedures
