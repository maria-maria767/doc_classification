**OurCompany Inc. Announces Expansion to New Regional Headquarters**

March 15, 2023 – [City, State] – OurCompany Inc., a leader in innovative technology solutions, is proud to announce the expansion of its operations with a new regional headquarters slated to open in [City, State] in the fourth quarter of this year. This expansion is part of OurCompany's ongoing commitment to support the growth of the tech industry in the region and to provide high-quality jobs to the local community.

The new headquarters will house a state-of-the-art Research & Development Center, as well as sales, marketing, and customer support teams. The facility is expected to bring over 250 new employment opportunities to the area, with roles ranging from software engineering to business administration.

OurCompany’s CEO, John Smith, said, "The decision to expand our presence into [City, State] was a logical step in our business growth strategy. The area is rich with diverse talent from prestigious schools and a vibrant tech sector. We are excited to provide new opportunities for jobs and collaboration within the community."

The company will host a Job Fair on July 1, 2023, at the [City Convention Center] to fill the new positions. Prospective candidates are encouraged to attend and bring their resumes for on-the-spot interviews.

In addition, OurCompany is committed to partnering with local educational institutions to develop programs that will equip students with the necessary skills to join the future workforce.

The community and press are invited to attend the groundbreaking ceremony scheduled for April 20, 2023. The event will include a keynote address by the CEO, a guided tour of the future site, and a reception where attendees can meet with OurCompany leaders and team members.

For more information about OurCompany Inc., employment opportunities, or to RSVP for the groundbreaking ceremony, please visit our website at www.ourcompany.com or contact our media relations department at the contact information provided above.
