Job Description: Customer Service Representative

Position Overview:
We are seeking a highly motivated and customer-focused individual to join our team as a Customer Service Representative. The successful candidate will be responsible for providing excellent customer service through phone, email and live chat support. They will also be responsible for resolving customer complaints and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Responsibilities:

* Answer customer inquiries via phone, email and live chat
* Provide accurate and timely information to customers
* Resolve customer complaints and issues in a professional and efficient manner
* Maintain accurate records of customer interactions and complaints
* Escalate complex issues to higher-level support when necessary
* Contribute to the development and improvement of customer service processes and procedures
* Stay up-to-date with product and service information to provide accurate and helpful information to customers

Requirements:

* High school diploma or equivalent
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Strong problem-solving and decision-making abilities
* Ability to work in a fast-paced and dynamic environment
* Proficiency in Microsoft Office and customer service software
* Availability to work rotating shifts, including weekends and holidays

We offer a competitive salary and benefits package, as well as opportunities for growth and development within the company. If you are passionate about providing excellent customer service, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.
