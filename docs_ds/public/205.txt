Proceedings:

I. Call to Order
The meeting was called to order by the Chair, Zelislav Kapa Silvana, at 10:00 AM.

II. Review and Approval of Previous Meeting Minutes
The minutes of the previous meeting held on March 3, 2023, were reviewed. Jur Lebedev Karolina proposed a motion to approve the minutes, seconded by Vena Miocic Vlado. Minutes were approved as read.

III. Financial Report and Budget Discussion for Q2 2023
Vena Miocic Vlado presented the financial report. The report highlighted the current budget status and projections for the second quarter. Discussion ensued regarding potential cost-saving measures and reallocation of funds for high-priority projects. The committee agreed to a revised budget plan for Q2, which includes a partnership with the ALcides & Rosaura Diniz (ARD) Foundation for a joint funding venture.

IV. Project Collaboration with OC STEM Initiative
Jur Lebedev Karolina presented the proposal for collaboration with the OC STEM Initiative. The focus will be on creating educational programs that target underserved communities in Sondrio. The committee expressed unanimous support for the project. Contact will be made with the OC STEM Initiative to formalize the partnership.

V. Upcoming Community Outreach Program in Collaboration with HappyBrains.eu
Zelislav Kapa Silvana briefed the committee on the upcoming community outreach program, designed to promote mental health awareness and support in the Lugo area. The program, scheduled for June 2023, will be conducted in collaboration with HappyBrains.eu. Members suggested various strategies to involve local schools and community centers.

VI. Miscellaneous & New Business
- The chair addressed an email received from as@williams-thompson.xyz suggesting a new environmental sustainability initiative. After a brief discussion, the committee decided to pursue further information and possibly include it in the agenda for the next meeting.
- Vena Miocic Vlado mentioned a contact at the ARD Foundation who offered potential sponsorship opportunities. It was agreed to follow up on this lead.

VII. Setting Next Meeting Date and Adjournment
The next meeting was scheduled for May 17, 2023, at 10:00 AM at the same location. There being no further business to address, the meeting was adjourned at 11:30 AM.
