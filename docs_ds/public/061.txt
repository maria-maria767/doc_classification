### PRESS RELEASE

**For Immediate Release**

**Dive Otago Expansion Plan Announcement**

We are thrilled to announce that Dive Otago will significantly expand its operational capacity and offerings starting June 2023. This expansion includes the inauguration of a new dive shop located at 3 Via Pontasso, Casella, Liguria 16015, Italy. This location will provide local and visiting divers with easy access to some of the most exquisite underwater adventures in the Mediterranean.

**A New Era for Dive Otago in Liguria**

With the opening of our new dive shop, we are committed to providing unparalleled service and unforgettable diving experiences to enthusiasts and professionals alike. Liguria's crystal-clear waters and rich marine biodiversity offer a perfect playground for divers of all levels.

**Grand Opening Ceremony**

We cordially invite the public to our grand opening ceremony scheduled for June 15, 2023. The event will begin at 10:00 AM with a ribbon-cutting ceremony, followed by an open house where guests can tour our new facility, meet our certified instructors, and learn about our courses and dive excursions.

**Special Offers**

To celebrate the launch, Dive Otago will offer a 20% discount on all diving courses and gear rentals during the first month of operations at the new location. In addition, we will conduct a raffle for a chance to win a free Discover Scuba Diving experience for two.

**About Dive Otago**

Established in 1998, Dive Otago is a leading dive center known for its commitment to safety, environmental conservation, and providing top-notch diving education. Our mission is to create a community of responsible divers who respect and enjoy the ocean's natural wonders.

We look forward to welcoming you to our new location in Liguria.
