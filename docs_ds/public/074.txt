1. Purpose

The purpose of this Health and Safety Policy document is to outline the health and safety measures and procedures in place at Triview UK Ltd., to ensure the wellbeing of all employees, visitors, and contractors involved in company operations.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees of Triview UK Ltd., contractors, and visitors at the facility located at 24 Apter Drive, Torrington, 06790 Connecticut.

3. Policy Statement

Triview UK Ltd. is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all its employees and ensuring that operations are conducted in a manner that is environmentally responsible and compliant with health and safety laws and best practices.

4. Roles and Responsibilities

- Management: Ensure that all health and safety policies are implemented, provide necessary resources for effective health and safety management, and promote a culture of safety within the organization.

- Employees: Adhere to all safety policies and procedures, report any workplace hazards or injuries, and participate in health and safety training provided by the company.

- Health and Safety Officer (Jody Novačkicoff): Develop and review health and safety programs, conduct risk assessments, and provide guidance and education to employees on health and safety matters.

5. General Workplace Safety

All personnel must follow the safety protocols set forth which include but are not limited to:
- Using personal protective equipment (PPE) as required.
- Following safe handling procedures for equipment and materials.
- Reporting any unsafe conditions or incidents to a supervisor immediately.

6. Emergency Procedures

In the event of an emergency (fire, medical, natural disaster), employees should:
- Alert other staff and help to evacuate the premises if necessary.
- Follow the instructions given by the emergency response team or public safety officers.
- Assemble at the designated emergency assembly point for headcount.

7. Training

Employees will receive health and safety training including, but not limited to:
- Orientation training for new hires.
- Continuous education on workplace-specific risks.
- Emergency response training.

8. Health and Safety Communication

Triview UK Ltd. will keep all employees informed about health and safety issues through:
- Regular team meetings.
- The company intranet.
- Health and safety bulletin boards located throughout the workplace.
