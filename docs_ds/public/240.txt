Job Description: Sales Representative

Job Title: Sales Representative

Job Summary:
The Sales Representative will be responsible for promoting and selling the company's products to potential customers. They will be required to build relationships with clients, negotiate contracts, and ensure that sales targets are met.

Key Responsibilities:

* Identify potential customers and assess their needs
* Develop and maintain relationships with customers
* Provide product demonstrations and presentations
* Negotiate contracts and close sales deals
* Maintain accurate sales records and report results to management
* Develop and execute sales plans to meet or exceed sales targets
* Stay up-to-date with market trends and competitor activities
* Participate in trade shows and other sales events
* Provide customer support and resolve any issues that may arise

Qualifications:

* Bachelor's degree in business, marketing, or a related field
* Minimum of 3 years of sales experience
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* Strong negotiation and closing skills
* Familiarity with sales software and CRM systems
* Ability to travel as needed

The Sales Representative will be based in Huesca, Lugo, or Como and will report to the Sales Manager.
