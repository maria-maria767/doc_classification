Miami, OK – March 9, 2023

**MARVELS OF MODERN ARCHITECTURE: EXHIBITION OPENING**

The City of Miami, in association with Maram, the innovative architectural firm, is excited to announce the opening of the "Marvels of Modern Architecture" exhibition. This event will be open to the public and will showcase groundbreaking architectural designs and concepts.

**Event Details:**

- **Date:** April 21, 2023
- **Time:** 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM
- **Location:** The Grand Gallery, Via Leonardo da Vinci, Umbertide, Umbria
- **Featured Speaker:** Simeon Toshev Vierka, renowned architect and design visionary
- **Organizations Involved:** Maram, Law Office of Josh Kendrick, Esq., Dive Otago

**Exhibition Highlights:**

- Interactive displays of modern sustainable building techniques
- Scale models of cutting-edge architectural projects from around the world
- Guest lectures by prominent architects including Argir Dajo Ognyan and Kralevski 
- Networking opportunities with industry professionals
- Hands-on workshops for students and aspiring architects

Admission is free, and guests of all ages are encouraged to attend. The exhibition is a fantastic chance for the community to engage with the individuals shaping our urban landscapes. For more information, please contact:
