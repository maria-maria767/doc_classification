# Evolving Consulting 2023 Marketing Plan

## Executive Summary 
This marketing plan has been developed for **Evolving Consulting** to outline the strategies and actions to be taken in the year 2023 to meet the company's marketing objectives. This document is Public and can be shared openly.

## Market Analysis
The competitive landscape within the consultancy sector continues to evolve with increased digitalization. Evolving Consulting must leverage current trends and technologies to assert our presence in key markets such as Forlì-Cesena, Lecco, and Cremona. 

## Marketing Objectives
- **Brand Awareness**: Increase brand recognition in the Emilia-Romagna region and specific target cities by 20%.
- **Client Acquisition**: Gain 15 new corporate clients by Q3.
- **Engagement**: Enhance online engagement by 35% through strategic content marketing.
  
## Target Market 
Our primary target market includes small to mid-sized businesses within the Emilia-Romagna region, seeking innovative and effective consulting services to optimize their operations and growth strategies.

## Marketing Strategies and Tactics
- **Digital Marketing**: 
  - SEO Optimization to increase our visibility on search engines.
  - Social Media Campaigns targeting platforms where our clients are most active.
  - Paid Advertising through Google Ads and LinkedIn Sponsored Content.

- **Content Marketing**:
  - Bi-weekly blog posts related to business growth strategies and case studies.
  - Monthly webinars with our lead consultants, including special guests from within the industry.
  
- **Networking and Partnerships**: 
  - Engage in local business events in Forlì-Cesena, Lecco, and Cremona to build partnerships.
  - Collaborate with organizations such as **Ksl Hair Ltd** and **Seman Baker** for cross-promotional opportunities.

- **Email Marketing**:
  - Monthly newsletters featuring industry insights, company updates, and special offers.
  - Personalized outreach to potential clients.

- **Public Relations**: 
  - Press releases in local news for significant company milestones or events.
  - Engage with community projects for brand visibility and corporate social responsibility.

## Budget Allocation
A budget of $100,000 is allocated to the marketing department to execute the above strategies.

## Key Performance Indicators
- Increase in website traffic.
- Growth in social media followers and engagement rates.
- Number of new clients acquired.
- ROI for each marketing channel.
