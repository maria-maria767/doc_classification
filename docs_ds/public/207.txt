Subject: New Partnership Announcement with East Staffordshire Citizens Advice Bureau

Dear Esteemed Associates,

We are thrilled to announce that our company, Vertepac | Toto Carrying Systems BV, has embarked on an exciting partnership with the East Staffordshire Citizens Advice Bureau. This collaboration is a testament to our commitment to community engagement and corporate social responsibility.

Starting from April 2023, Vertepac | Toto Carrying Systems BV will be supporting the East Staffordshire Citizens Advice Bureau in their mission to provide free, confidential advice to the people of Staffordshire, helping them resolve problems affecting their lives.

This partnership allows us to contribute to the societal well-being while extending our brand ethos of innovative solutions and community support. The Citizens Advice Bureau's dedication to offering guidance on issues such as debt, employment, housing, and more, aligns with our core values of empowerment and service.
