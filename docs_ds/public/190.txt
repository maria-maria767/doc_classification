Company Policy and Procedure Document

Policy Title: Data and Communication Usage Policy

Policy Number: PP-2023-05

Effective Date: April 15, 2023

Issuing Authority: Lipowska, Chief Compliance Officer

I. Purpose:
The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for the appropriate use of all forms of communication and the handling of data within and outside of the organization, consistent with the company's commitment to legal compliance, security, and professionalism.

II. Scope:
This policy is applicable to all employees, contractors, and representatives of Seratek Manufacturing Inc. ("The Company") in all locations, including the headquarters at 183 Main Street, Chatham, Massachusetts 02733, United States.

III. Policy:

A. Electronic Communication:
1. All company-related communication should be conducted through official company channels, such as assigned corporate email addresses. Personal email addresses, such as juliusrogers769@hotmail.com, should not be used for business purposes.
2. Employees must ensure the confidentiality and integrity of the information they send and receive.
3. Electronic files should be transmitted securely and in accordance with data protection laws.

B. Data Handling and Security:
1. Employees shall handle all company data with the utmost care and in compliance with all applicable privacy laws.
2. Public data may be disclosed to parties outside of the company. All other data classifications (internal, confidential, restricted) must be handled according to respective internal protocols.

C. Phone and Video Conferences:
1. Business-related phone calls should be made using company-approved devices and numbers, e.g., (07700)900940. Personal phone numbers should not be used for business purposes unless specifically approved in advance.
2. Video conferencing should be conducted on secure and approved platforms only.

D. Social Media Use:
1. While using social media, employees must not represent themselves as spokespeople for the company unless authorized as part of their role.
2. The company's brand and reputation must be upheld across all forms of communication.

E. Contact Information:
1. For any queries or clarification regarding this policy, employees are advised to contact the HR department at s-snyder1@carey-hernandez.website or phone 051 651 348.

IV. Compliance:
Failure to comply with this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.

V. Amendments:
The company reserves the right to amend this policy at any time. Staff will be notified through official channels of any such changes.

By adherence to these guidelines, Seratek Manufacturing Inc. aims to maintain a high standard of operational efficiency, information security, and legal compliance.
