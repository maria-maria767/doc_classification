### 1. Welcome to AVL in Sweden
Welcome to AVL in Sweden, a place where your professional growth is just as important as the contributions you make. In this manual, you will find all the necessary information to navigate your role successfully. 

### 2. Company Overview
AVL in Sweden is part of a global network that specializes in automotive development, simulation, and testing solutions. Our aim is to drive the future of mobility by enhancing efficiency, quality, and innovation.

### 3. Our Core Values
- **Innovation**: Strive for creative solutions and continuous improvement.
- **Integrity**: Conduct business with honesty and fairness.
- **Teamwork**: Collaborate effectively to achieve common goals.

### 4. General Safety and Conduct
Safety is our top priority. Always follow established procedures, and report unsafe conditions immediately to your supervisor or through the designated company channels.

### 5. Introduction to Our Products and Services
We offer a wide range of services, including vehicle testing, simulation software, and powertrain development. Familiarize yourself with our product categories and their applications.

### 6. Customer Service Excellence
Every team member represents AVL Sweden. Delivering outstanding customer service is paramount. Listen attentively, communicate clearly, and resolve customer issues with courtesy and professionalism.

### 7. Basic Troubleshooting Procedures
Should you encounter equipment or system issues, refer to the basic troubleshooting steps outlined in Chapter 7. Always escalate unresolved problems to your technical lead.

### 8. Reporting Issues and Concerns
If you face an issue or have a concern, report it to your supervisor. Alternatively, contact Human Resources at j-lazaro9419@medina.us or call 1814281282 during office hours.

### 9. Continuing Education and Training Opportunities
We encourage continuous learning. Stay updated with training sessions and workshops listed on our intranet. Take advantage of education reimbursement for approved courses.

### 10. Acknowledgment and Agreements
Upon completion of this manual, sign the acknowledgment form to confirm that you understand and agree to abide by AVL Sweden's policies and procedures.

This training manual is intended to be a starting point for new employees. It does not cover all scenarios and should be used in conjunction with ongoing training and supervision.
