Job Title: Administrative Assistant

Company: Brighton Wray

Location: 209 Wachusett Street, Boston, Massachusetts

Reports to: Office Manager

Job Summary:
Brighton Wray is seeking a highly organized and self-motivated Administrative Assistant to join our dynamic team. The successful candidate will provide administrative support to ensure efficient operation of the office. This role involves a wide range of administrative tasks including handling correspondence, scheduling appointments, and supporting staff with various office duties.

Key Responsibilities:
- Provide general support to office visitors and handle inquiries via phone and email.
- Schedule and coordinate meetings, appointments, and travel arrangements for staff.
- Prepare and distribute written communications such as memos, emails, invoices, reports, and other documents when necessary.
- Maintain a filing system for important and confidential company documents.
- Perform data entry and scan documents to ensure the accuracy and completeness of information.
- Assist in the preparation of regularly scheduled reports.
- Maintain office supplies inventory by checking stock, anticipating needed supplies, and placing orders for replenishment.
- Contribute to the overall workflow efficiency by performing additional administrative tasks as assigned by the manager.

Qualifications:
- Proven experience as an administrative assistant or office admin assistant.
- Knowledge of office management systems and procedures.
- Proficiency in MS Office, particularly MS Word and MS Excel.
- Strong organizational skills with the ability to multi-task.
- High School degree; additional qualification as an administrative assistant or secretary will be a plus.

We offer a competitive salary, comprehensive benefits, and an engaging work environment. Brighton Wray is committed to creating a diverse environment and is proud to be an equal opportunity employer.
