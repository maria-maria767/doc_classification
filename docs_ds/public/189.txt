**Uyola Environment Initiatives Newsletter - March 2023 Edition**

Welcome to the March edition of Uyola's Environmental Initiatives newsletter, where we share news, updates, and stories from our sustainability efforts across the globe.

**[Headline]**  
_Uyola Leads Community Cleanup in Buford, GA_

Over the weekend, the amazing people of Buford, GA, alongside volunteers from Uyola, embarked on a community cleanup event at the local park. Together, we collected over 300 pounds of trash! A big thank you to the community members and Uyola employees, Beth, Dexter, and Kennedy Annemarie Swearingen, for organizing this successful initiative.

**[Feature Article]**  
_Green Spaces for Ghana_

The expansion of our "Green Spaces" project has reached new heights in Ghana, with the planting of 5,000 new trees in urban areas. This effort is aimed at improving air quality and providing recreational spaces for local communities. We are grateful for the partnership with the local non-profit organizations, which made this possible.

**[Company Highlight]**  
_Andrew & T2 Interiors Collaboration for Sustainability_

Uyola is excited to announce a partnership with Andrew, a renowned architecture firm, and T2 Interiors, leading experts in sustainable interior solutions. This year, we will be launching a series of eco-friendly office spaces designed to minimize environmental footprint while maximizing worker satisfaction and productivity.
