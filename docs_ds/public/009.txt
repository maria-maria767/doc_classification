Press Release

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Albany, OR – March 15, 2023

Craftmatch HR, an innovative leader in the human resources sector, is excited to announce the launch of its new campaign, "Empower Your Workforce: Strategies for Success". This initiative is aimed at providing businesses in Albany and the greater Oregon region with cutting-edge insights and tools to foster productive work environments.

Join us for the kick-off event which will take place on April 1, 2023, at the downtown conference hall in Albany, OR. The event will feature prominent speakers, including Argir Dajo Matej Matea Smolković Dodik from Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda and Filimena Tunteva Dobrochna, a renowned organizational psychologist.

The themes that will be covered during the event include:

- Leveraging technology for efficient workforce management
- Enhancing employee engagement through innovative programs
- Applying data-driven strategies to HR processes
- Fostering diversity and inclusion within the workplace

This campaign underscores Craftmatch HR's commitment to empowering organizations and their employees to achieve greater productivity and satisfaction. "We believe that businesses thrive when their workers do," says Sofija, the head of Craftmatch HR’s development team. "Our Empower Your Workforce campaign will provide the knowledge and resources required for companies to harness the full potential of their human capital."

For more details on the event, or to register, please contact Addelyn Hall at addelyn.hall9550@turner-clay.biz or call +57 61 1794 0104. Media inquiries are also welcomed. 

We look forward to welcoming business owners, HR professionals, and all those interested in advancing their company’s human resources strategies to this groundbreaking event.
