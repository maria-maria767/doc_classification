Jerome Enterprises Announces Expansion into the Sondrio Market

Sondrio, Italy - Jerome Enterprises, Inc., a leading provider of innovative technology solutions, is proud to announce its latest market expansion into Sondrio, Italy as part of its ongoing effort to bring exceptional service and cutting-edge products to consumers worldwide.

This strategic move supports Jerome's mission to bridge technological divides and to provide top-tier services to more communities, ensuring that every individual has access to the resources they need to thrive in today's digital world. 

"Expanding our presence to Sondrio is an exciting step for Jerome Enterprises. The vibrant culture and growing market in this region are perfect for our next phase of growth. We are committed to becoming an integral part of the community and contributing positively to its economic development," said Zlata Okiljivic Duffy, CEO of Jerome Enterprises.

As part of the expansion, Jerome Enterprises plans to open a new office on North Pleasant Street, Amherst, Massachusetts, creating job opportunities and fostering local partnerships. Interested individuals and organizations are encouraged to contact Nexus Services, an employment and business development partner, to learn more about potential collaborations. Information sessions and job fairs will be announced in the upcoming weeks.

For further details, please contact Nexus Services at z-w60@li.website or by phone at 0800 40 62.
