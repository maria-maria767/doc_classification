**Product Overview:**

Eyes of the World Optical is proud to introduce the HelioView Smart Glasses, an innovative wearable device designed to offer a seamless integration of technology with everyday eyewear. The HelioView Smart Glasses provide a hands-free experience for users, offering functionalities such as live notifications, GPS navigation, augmented reality, and voice control.

**Key Features:**

- **Lightweight, ergonomic design** suitable for prolonged wear.
- **Adjustable transparency** smart lens technology with auto-adaptation to lighting conditions.
- **High-definition micro-display** projecting a clear and bright interface.
- **Touchpad and voice-controlled interface** enabling efficient and intuitive user interaction.
- **Full wireless connectivity** including Bluetooth 5.0 and Wi-Fi capabilities.
- **Integrated high-resolution camera** for capturing first-person photos and videos.
- **Built-in bone conduction speakers** delivering crisp and clear audio without obstructing the ear canal.
- **Augmented reality applications** compatible with navigation, information retrieval, and gaming.
- **Up to 12 hours battery life** with fast-charging support.
- **Water and dust resistance** rated IP67 for outdoor use reliability.

**Technical Specifications:**

- **Display Resolution:** 1280x720 pixels
- **Camera:** 8MP, 1080p video recording
- **Audio:** Bone conduction transducers with noise-cancellation microphones
- **Sensors:** Gyroscope, accelerometer, ambient light sensor, proximity sensor
- **Storage:** 32GB internal storage
- **Operating System:** Android based with compatibility for iOS and Android devices
- **Connectivity:** Bluetooth 5.0, Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, GPS/GLONASS
- **Battery:** Lithium-polymer 400mAh
- **Charge Time:** Approximately 1 hour for full charge
- **Port:** USB-C for charging and data
