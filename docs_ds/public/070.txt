**Galaxy-Energy Announces Expansion into Huesca**

Chicopee, MA, April 4, 2023 – Galaxy-Energy, a leader in innovative renewable energy solutions, is proud to announce its upcoming expansion into the city of Huesca. This initiative is part of our commitment to contribute to a sustainable future and to meet the increasing demand for renewable energy options in regions across the globe.

**Details of the Huesca Expansion:**

* The new operations will be housed at zones approved by the Zone franche organization, positioning the company to take advantage of strategic economic benefits.
* Aspa consultores, a renowned consulting firm, has been engaged to oversee the environmental impact assessments to ensure all activities are in line with Galaxy-Energy's environmental commitments.
* The project is set to kick off this coming summer with a job fair at 37 Dickinson Street, Chicopee 01013 Massachusetts. Galaxy-Energy plans to create up to 50 new jobs in the Huesca region over the next three years.

Galaxy-Energy's CEO, Slobodan Kovaševski Dragoslav, expressed enthusiasm about the expansion: "Huesca offers a thriving community with a dynamic workforce, and we are excited to bring new economic growth and sustainable energy solutions to the area. Our commitment to environmental stewardship and innovation in energy continues to be at the forefront of our expansion strategies."

**About Galaxy-Energy:**

Galaxy-Energy has been at the cutting edge of renewable energy since its inception in 2005. With a mission to provide clean, sustainable, and affordable energy, Galaxy-Energy is continually expanding its global footprint to make a positive impact on the environment and communities worldwide.

For more information on the Huesca expansion or to learn more about Galaxy-Energy's initiatives, please visit our website at www.galaxy-energy.com.
