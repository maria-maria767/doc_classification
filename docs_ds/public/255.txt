# Product Specification Document

### 1. Introduction
This document provides a detailed specification of the new "Eyesight Enhancement Opticals (EEO)" product, designed and distributed by eyes of the world optical. The Eyesight Enhancement Opticals are a new line of eyewear intended for widespread use, integrating the latest technology in lens clarity and frame strength for a premium vision-enhancing experience.

### 2. Product Overview
- **Product Name:** Eyesight Enhancement Opticals (EEO)
- **Manufacturer:** eyes of the world optical
- **Type:** Eyewear – Spectacles

### 3. Features and Benefits
- **Lens Technology:** Polycarbonate lenses with anti-reflective coating, UV400 protection, blue light filtration, and scratch-resistant finish.
- **Frame Material:** Bend-resistant titanium alloy with a memory form factor to retain shape.
- **Design Options:** Available in full-rim, half-rim, and rimless styles.
- **Size Variety:** Standard, petite, and broad fit to cater to all face shapes and sizes.
- **Hinge Mechanism:** Reinforced spring hinge for greater durability and comfort.
- **Nose Pads:** Hypoallergenic silicone pads with adjustable design for a customized fit.
- **Style Variants:** Modern, Classic, and Trendy to align with a wide range of customer preferences.

### 4. Product Use
The Eyesight Enhancement Opticals are suitable for individuals requiring vision correction and protection from harmful light exposure. They are versatile for use in indoor and outdoor environments.

### 5. Quality Assurance
All EEO products go through rigorous quality control checks to ensure they meet industry standards for eyewear.
