Softway Medical Announces Partnership with Maretti Lighting for Enhanced Healthcare Environments

UMBRIA, ITALY – [Current Date] – Softway Medical, a leader in the development of healthcare software solutions, is proud to announce a groundbreaking partnership with Maretti Lighting, renowned for its innovative lighting designs. This partnership is expected to revolutionize the way lighting is integrated into healthcare facilities, thereby improving patient experiences and healthcare outcomes.

Through this collaboration, Maretti Lighting will provide state-of-the-art lighting solutions designed to enhance the functionality of Softway Medical’s healthcare software systems. The tailored lighting environments will aim to support patient well-being, staff efficiency, and overall facility management. One of the first initiatives of this partnership will be the implementation of smart lighting systems in select healthcare facilities in the United States, launching at the 299 Pines Bridge Road, Town of New Castle, New York 10562, USA.

“Lighting plays a crucial role in healthcare settings, not just for the obvious functional aspects, but also for the comfort and recovery of patients,” said Andon Konstantinov Nedelcho, CEO of Softway Medical. “Maretti Lighting’s expertise will enable us to create a more holistic healthcare environment that benefits all users of our software.”

Elise Rossi, CEO of Maretti Lighting, added, “We are excited to partner with an innovative company like Softway Medical. Together, we believe we can set new standards in healthcare infrastructure by combining cutting-edge lighting technology with advanced medical software.”

For more information about the partnership between Softway Medical and Maretti Lighting, or to speak with a representative from either organization, please contact Velizar Cisek Kostya at a.r48@cooper-phillips.us or via phone at (051) 8567-7487.
