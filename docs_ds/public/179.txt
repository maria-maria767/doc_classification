Job Description: Account Manager

Location: Huesca, Spain

Job Type: Full-time

About the company:
Oasis One-Twelve is a leading provider of residential mortgage services in Spain. We are currently seeking an experienced Account Manager to join our team in Huesca. The ideal candidate will be highly motivated, proactive, and driven to succeed.

Responsibilities:

* Develop and maintain strong relationships with existing clients, ensuring a high level of customer satisfaction.
* Proactively identify new business opportunities and develop strategies to increase sales.
* Manage a portfolio of commercial loans, ensuring all loans are serviced in accordance with industry regulations and standards.
* Collaborate with internal teams to ensure timely and accurate processing of loan applications and payments.
* Provide exceptional customer service, responding to inquiries and resolving any issues that may arise.
* Maintain accurate records and reports, as required.

Qualifications:

* Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Finance or related field.
* Minimum of 5 years of experience in account management or sales.
* Highly organized and detail-oriented, with excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
* Strong analytical skills and the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment.
* Demonstrated experience in managing a portfolio of commercial loans and customer relationships.
* Knowledge of the residential mortgage industry and regulations is highly desirable.
