MEMORANDUM

Date: February 17, 2023

To: All Employees

From: Petra Manuel Tenney, CEO

Subject: Announcement of New Product Launch

Dear Employees,

I am excited to announce that our company will be launching a new product line in the coming weeks. This new product is the result of months of hard work and dedication by our research and development team, and I am confident that it will be a great success.

The new product is a revolutionary new device that will change the way we live and work. It is a compact, portable device that can be used to charge all of your electronic devices on the go. It is perfect for busy professionals who are always on the move and need to stay connected.

We are excited to bring this new product to market and believe that it will be a game-changer in the industry. We will be holding a launch event in a few weeks, and I encourage all employees to attend and learn more about this exciting new product.

Thank you for your hard work and dedication to our company. I am proud to be a part of such a talented and innovative team.

Sincerely,
Petra Manuel Tenney
CEO

cc: Veton Zibrechki Slavco, COO
Phillip Patsev Pribena, CFO
