**Bureau Sjoerdsma: A New Partnership for Sustainable Energy Solutions**

Bergamo, March 30, 2023 — Bureau Sjoerdsma, a leader in innovative environmental engineering, is thrilled to announce our new partnership with Galaxy-Energy, the prominent renewable energy company known for advocating for sustainable energy solutions. This synergistic collaboration is set to revolutionize the green energy landscape, with both parties committed to reducing carbon emissions and promoting eco-friendly energy alternatives.

**The Partnership in Detail:**

With this partnership, Galaxy-Energy will leverage Bureau Sjoerdsma's expertise in environmental analysis and project implementation to enhance the efficiency and distribution of clean energy systems across urban and rural regions. This venture underscores our shared dedication to a greener, more sustainable future and aligns with our global commitment to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

**Future Ventures:**

The collaboration plans to kick off with a series of community-based solar power initiatives in Northern Italy, particularly within the Forlì-Cesena region, with further projects already in the pipeline for expansion into other European markets.

**Involvement Opportunities:**

Residents and businesses in the Forlì-Cesena area interested in participating in our initial phase of community solar projects are encouraged to express their interest. For more information on the partnership or to learn how to get involved, please contact Olga Jankovič Ellen, our Public Relations Coordinator, via email at scann@shepard-reed.com or by phone at (051) 8567-7487.

**About Bureau Sjoerdsma:**

Based out of 189 Beech Terrace, Wayne, New Jersey 07470, Bureau Sjoerdsma has been an environmental pioneer for over a decade. Our company specializes in developing comprehensive strategies that empower communities to transition towards sustainability without compromising economic growth.

**About Galaxy-Energy:**

Galaxy-Energy, located at Concord Street, Gloucester, Massachusetts, is one of the leading names in the renewable energy sector. They are dedicated to harnessing the power of renewable resources to foster cleaner and more resilient energy systems worldwide.
