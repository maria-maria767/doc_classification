### Galaxy-Energy – Press Release

#### Announcing Our New Energy Conservation Initiative

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

**Chicopee, Massachusetts, [Release Date] –** Galaxy-Energy, a leading innovator in sustainable energy solutions, is delighted to announce the launch of our latest energy conservation initiative aimed at promoting sustainable energy use and reducing the carbon footprint within the Massachusetts region.

**Introducing GreenStride:**
Galaxy-Energy's 'GreenStride' project is a community-oriented program that partners with local businesses and residents to install energy-efficient appliances and solar panels at vastly discounted rates. This initiative is a testament to our commitment to environmental sustainability and supporting local communities in their efforts to become eco-friendlier.

**Statement from Xavier Paulo Linn Barros, Director of Public Engagement:** "We recognize the urgency of addressing climate change and we are proud to lead by example. GreenStride is more than just a program—it's our pledge to future generations that we will strive for a brighter, cleaner world. We invite everyone to join us on this journey."
