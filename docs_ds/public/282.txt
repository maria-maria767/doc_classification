Policy and Procedure: Customer Complaint Resolution

Introduction:
At Themaverde, we value our customers and strive to provide the best possible service. However, we understand that sometimes things can go wrong, and we want to make sure that we have a process in place to handle customer complaints in a timely and effective manner. This policy and procedure outlines the steps we will take to resolve customer complaints and ensure that our customers are satisfied with our service.

Policy:

1. All customer complaints will be taken seriously and treated with the utmost respect.
2. We will respond to all customer complaints within 24 hours of receipt.
3. Our customer service team will work to resolve the complaint as quickly as possible, and keep the customer informed of the progress.
4. If the complaint cannot be resolved immediately, we will provide the customer with a timeline for when they can expect a resolution.
5. If the complaint requires escalation, it will be escalated to the appropriate department or manager for further investigation.
6. We will keep detailed records of all customer complaints and the steps taken to resolve them.
7. We will use customer feedback to improve our service and prevent similar complaints from occurring in the future.

Procedure:

1. When a customer complaint is received, it will be logged in our customer service system and assigned to a customer service representative.
2. The customer service representative will contact the customer to acknowledge receipt of the complaint and gather additional information if necessary.
3. The customer service representative will work to resolve the complaint, either by providing a solution or escalating it to the appropriate department or manager.
4. If the complaint is escalated, the customer will be kept informed of the progress and the expected timeline for resolution.
5. Once the complaint has been resolved, the customer will be contacted to confirm the resolution and ensure their satisfaction.
6. The customer service representative will document the complaint and the steps taken to resolve it in our customer service system.
7. The customer feedback will be used to improve our service and prevent similar complaints from occurring in the future.

At Themaverde, we are committed to providing excellent service to our customers.
