**1. Introduction**

Triview UK Ltd. is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all of its employees, visitors, and contractors. This document outlines the company's general health and safety procedures that are to be followed to ensure a secure and risk-free workplace. This is a public document and provides guidelines applicable to the Triview UK Ltd. office located at 24 Apter Drive, Torrington, 06790 Connecticut.

**2. General Safety Rules**

- All employees and visitors must comply with all posted safety signs and instructions.
- Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) must be worn in designated areas and for specific tasks as directed by health and safety officers.
- Report any unsafe conditions or accidents to the immediate supervisor or to the health and safety committee.
- Emergency exit routes and doors must never be blocked or locked during working hours.
- Smoking is prohibited in all indoor areas and within 15 feet of building entrances, exits, and air intakes.

**3. Emergency Procedures**

- In case of fire, raise the alarm by activating the nearest fire alarm switch. Evacuate the building immediately via the nearest exit and proceed to the designated assembly point.
- For medical emergencies, call the emergency number (07700)900752 and provide the operator with the exact location and nature of the emergency.

**4. Reporting Accidents and Incidents**

- Any work-related injury, no matter how minor, should be reported to the Health and Safety Manager, Nixon Puentes, by email at v-perez2@evans.store or phone (07700)900752.
- A detailed incident report must be filled out within 24 hours of the occurrence.

**5. Training and Education**

- Employees will receive health and safety training during orientation and regularly thereafter.
- Training sessions will be scheduled annually or as required by changes in policy or law.

**6. Health and Safety Committee**

- The health and safety committee is responsible for the implementation and oversight of this document.
- The committee meets on a quarterly basis and may be contacted through the Health and Safety Manager, Nixon Puentes.

**7. Review and Revision of Guidelines**

- These health and safety guidelines will be reviewed annually by the health and safety committee.
- Any changes required will be communicated through standard company channels and updated on the company noticeboard.

**8. Acknowledgement**

All Triview UK Ltd. employees are required to acknowledge that they have read and understood these guidelines and agree to abide by them as a condition of their employment.
