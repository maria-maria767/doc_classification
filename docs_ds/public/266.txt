**NextStep for Life, Inc.**
Annual Community Engagement Report 2022

**Introduction**

On behalf of NextStep for Life, Inc., it is with great pleasure that I, Kristal Dinova, having served as the Community Outreach Coordinator, present the 2022 Annual Community Engagement Report. This document highlights the impact of our programs and initiatives throughout the year in our commitment to foster community growth and involvement.

**Programs & Initiatives**

1. **Mentorship Magic:** Our mentorship program has paired over 150 young adults with experienced professionals in their field of interest, fostering growth, learning, and professional development.

2. **Health and Wellness Fairs:** With the partnership of local health organizations, we hosted bi-monthly fairs at our office on Via Brogeda rd. on the number 24 in Como, Lombardia. These events provided free health screenings, workshops, and fitness classes to the public. 

3. **Career Pathways Expeditions:** In collaboration with Jobs&Travel Consultancy - J&T Dubai, we organized three expeditions for individuals seeking to advance their career prospects abroad with appropriate information and guidance.

**Community Impact**

- Over 2,000 community members participated in our Health and Wellness Fairs.
- Successful job placements for 75% of our Career Pathways participants.
- Notable increase in local business sponsorships by 40% compared to the previous year.

**Financial Overview**

The financial contributions and fundraising efforts have been directed towards expanding our programs and maintaining the quality of services provided. A detailed financial report is available upon request via email at b-m@gonzalez-thomas.org or by contacting our office at 456.565.2621x77600.

**Acknowledgements**

**Conclusion**

As we reflect on the accomplishments of the past year, we remain dedicated to our mission of empowering lives through active and compassionate community involvement. For further information about our programs or to learn how to get involved, please reach out to us at the above contact details.
