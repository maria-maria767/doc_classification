Albany, OR – March 16, 2023 – Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda is proud to announce the expansion of its strategic services to the vibrant business community of Albany, Oregon. This venture aims to bridge the gap between innovative chess strategies and cutting-edge business practices, fostering a unique approach to business challenges and decision-making processes.

In conjunction with the expansion, Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda will host a Grand Opening event on April 15, 2023, at 10:00 AM at our new office located at 39.3369893 Green St., Albany, OR. The event will feature keynote speaker Argir Matej Matea Smolković Dodik, renowned business strategist and author of "The King's Gambit: Applying Chess Principles to Business Success." 

Attendees will have the opportunity to network with industry professionals, explore our wide range of consulting services, and participate in an interactive chess tournament designed to highlight the importance of strategic planning and foresight in the business environment. 

Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda is also excited to announce partnerships with local businesses including Quevin, LLC, and Craftmatch HR, to further integrate into the local business ecosystem and provide comprehensive support and resources to our clients.

We look forward to welcoming members of the Albany business community, the press, and public figures to our Grand Opening event. For more information, please contact Sofija Smolković at the details provided above, or visit our website at www.chessconsulting-bs.com/albany-opening.

About Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda:
Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda is a consulting firm that blends traditional business strategy with the centuries-old wisdom of chess. Our innovative approach helps clients navigate the complexities of modern business, ensuring they stay several moves ahead of the competition. With a team of expert consultants and a track record of successful engagements, we are committed to delivering optimal solutions tailored to the unique needs of each client.
