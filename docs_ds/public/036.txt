**Maram Announces Partnership with The Law Office of Josh Kendrick, Esq. for Environmental Awareness Initiative**

Umbertide, Umbria – Maram, a prominent advocacy group for environmental conservation, is thrilled to announce a new partnership with the Law Office of Josh Kendrick, Esq., a legal firm with a strong commitment to environmental issues. This initiative is aimed at raising awareness and promoting community engagement in sustainability efforts within the Umbria region.

"Our collaboration with Josh Kendrick’s law firm marks a significant milestone in our journey towards a greener future," stated Simeon Toshev Vierka, the spokesperson for Maram. "We believe that by combining our resources and expertise, we can make a substantial impact on local environmental policies and practices."

The partnership will kick off with a series of workshops and seminars open to the public, focusing on key issues such as renewable energy adoption, waste management, and legal aspects of environmental protection. These events will be held at:

3 Via Pontasso,
Casella, Liguria 16015, Italy

Commencing on June 5, 2023, coinciding with World Environment Day, these programs aim to inspire positive change and empower residents with the knowledge to make eco-friendly choices.

"We are proud to stand alongside Maram to champion the importance of preserving our planet," said Josh Kendrick, Esq., founder of the Law Office of Josh Kendrick. "It is crucial that we engage with the community to develop practical solutions that benefit both the environment and our society."
