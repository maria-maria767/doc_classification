Job Summary:

The Sales Representative will be responsible for promoting and selling our products to potential customers in the assigned region. The ideal candidate should have excellent communication skills, be able to build and maintain relationships with customers, and have experience in sales.

Responsibilities:

* Develop and implement sales strategies to achieve company goals
* Build and maintain relationships with potential and existing customers
* Provide customers with product information and demonstrate products as needed
* Prepare and present sales proposals and presentations to potential customers
* Maintain accurate sales records and provide reports as required
* Collaborate with other departments to ensure customer satisfaction
* Attend trade shows and other marketing events as required

Requirements:

* Bachelor's degree in business, marketing or related field
* 2+ years of sales experience
* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
* Ability to work independently and as part of a team
* Strong organizational and time management skills
* Proficient in Microsoft Office and CRM software
* Valid driver's license and reliable transportation
