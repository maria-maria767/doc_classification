**Servpro of East Cobb Initiates Community Outreach Program in Saylesville**

Saylesville, Rhode Island - Servpro of East Cobb is thrilled to announce the launch of its innovative Community Outreach Program, designed to enhance the preparedness of residents and businesses in Saylesville, Rhode Island for unexpected property damage events.

**Free Educational Seminars and Workshops**
The cornerstone of the Community Outreach Program is a series of educational seminars and workshops open to the general public. These events are aimed at helping individuals and business owners understand the necessary steps to minimize damage from fire, water, and mold. The first seminar is scheduled for:

Date: May 15, 2023
Time: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Location: Saylesville Community Center, 6 Burwell Street, Saylesville, Rhode Island 02865, US
Admission: Free

**Emergency Readiness Plan (ERP)**
In addition, Servpro of East Cobb is offering a complimentary Emergency Readiness Plan (ERP) for local businesses. The ERP is a no-cost assessment of your facility that helps you understand and plan for potential emergencies.
