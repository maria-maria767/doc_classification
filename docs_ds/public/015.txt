Softway Medical Announces Grand Opening of New Regional Headquarters in Merced, California

MERCED, CA - March 2023 - Softway Medical, a leading innovator in healthcare IT solutions, is excited to announce the grand opening of its new regional headquarters situated at 299 Pines Bridge Road, Town of New Castle, New York 10562, US. The inauguration event is scheduled for May 1st, 2023, and will begin at 10:00 AM.

This expansion reflects Softway Medical's ongoing commitment to providing exceptional service and support to its clients and partners. The new office in Merced will serve as a central hub for operations across the United States, enabling the company to better serve the growing demand for advanced medical software solutions.

During the grand opening, guests will have the opportunity to tour the new facility, meet with top executives, and learn more about Softway Medical's leading-edge products and services. The event promises to bring together healthcare professionals, industry experts, and community leaders.

"We are thrilled to open our doors in the vibrant city of Merced and are looking forward to establishing strong relationships within the community," said Velizar Cisek Kostya, CEO of Softway Medical. "Our new headquarters is not just a building; it's a testament to our dedication to improving healthcare through technology."

Softway Medical's innovative product lineup includes solutions for electronic health records (EHR), practice management, and patient engagement. By streamlining clinical workflows and enhancing patient care, Softway Medical helps healthcare providers adapt to the ever-changing landscape of the medical field.
