Company Document: Service Level Agreement

This Service Level Agreement (SLA) is made between Simeon Toshev Vierka, Argir Dajo Ognyan, Kralevski (hereinafter referred to as "the Client"), and Maram (hereinafter referred to as "the Company").

Scope
--

This SLA outlines the standards of service that the Company agrees to provide to the Client.

Service Levels
------

The Company shall provide the following services to the Client:

1. Software Development
2. Technical Support
3. Project Management

Service Standards
------------

The standards of service that the Company agrees to meet are as follows:

1. Software Development: The Company shall deliver software that is free of errors, meets the Client's specifications, and is delivered on time.
2. Technical Support: The Company shall provide technical support to the Client within 24 hours of receiving a support request.
3. Project Management: The Company shall provide regular project status updates to the Client and ensure that the project is delivered on time and within budget.

Service Levels Guarantees
-------------

The Company guarantees that it shall meet the above-mentioned service levels. If the Company fails to meet these service levels, the Client shall receive a service credit equal to 5% of the Client's annual service fees for each incident where the service level is not met.

Termination
----

Either party may terminate this agreement upon 30 days' written notice to the other party.

Governing Law
-----

This SLA shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
