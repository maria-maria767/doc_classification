Subject: Health and Safety Policy for Sys softwaresysteme gmbh

Dear Employees,

At Sys softwaresysteme gmbh, we are committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment for all our employees. We believe that a safe and healthy workplace is essential for the well-being of our employees and the success of our business.

To maintain a safe and healthy work environment, we have established a Health and Safety Policy that outlines our commitment to protecting the health and safety of our employees. This policy is based on the principles of prevention, protection, and participation.

Prevention: We will take all reasonable steps to prevent accidents and injuries in the workplace. This includes providing appropriate training and safety equipment, conducting regular safety inspections, and addressing any hazards or safety concerns that are brought to our attention.

Protection: We will provide our employees with the necessary equipment and resources to protect their health and safety while at work. This includes personal protective equipment, ergonomic tools and equipment, and first aid supplies.

Participation: We encourage our employees to participate in the development and implementation of our Health and Safety Policy. We believe that our employees are an important part of our safety team, and we value their input and feedback.

We ask that all employees review and follow the guidelines outlined in this policy.
