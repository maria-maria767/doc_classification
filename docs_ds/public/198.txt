**PRESS RELEASE**

MULTISKIN Announces New Skincare Line Launch

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

Veneto, Italy – March 15, 2023 – Multiskin, a globally recognized leader in innovative skincare solutions, is excited to announce the launch of its newest product line, "Eterna Radiance," designed to cater to all skin types, providing a comprehensive solution for a radiant, youthful complexion.

This new line has been carefully formulated with natural ingredients, incorporating the latest in skincare technology. "Eterna Radiance" will be available starting April 1st, 2023, at select retailers and on our website.

Multiskin's CEO, Simeon Toshev, expressed enthusiasm about the launch: "We are proud to introduce 'Eterna Radiance' to our customers. Our team has spent years perfecting these formulas, ensuring that we continue to deliver products that meet the high standards our consumers expect from Multiskin.”

To celebrate this launch, Multiskin will be hosting a promotional event on April 5th, 2023, at 6/A Via Giovanni Amendola, Verona 37138, Veneto. The event will feature product demonstrations, free samples, and consultations with skincare experts.

For more information on the "Eterna Radiance" line or the launch event, please contact our Public Relations officer Eleanore Adam at e.adam@multiskin.com or call 0270551531.

About Multiskin:  
Multiskin has been at the forefront of skincare innovation since its inception in 1998. With a commitment to providing high-quality, effective products, Multiskin continues to revolutionize the skincare industry, catering to the diverse needs of its global customer base.
