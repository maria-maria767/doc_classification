**Job Description**

**Position Title:** Customer Service Representative  
**Department:** Client Relations  
**Organization:** Oasis One-Twelve  
**Reports To:** Client Relations Manager  
**Location:** 93 Ashton Street, Crompton, Rhode Island 02893  
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2023

**Position Purpose:**

Oasis One-Twelve is seeking a dedicated Customer Service Representative to join our Client Relations department. This role involves providing exceptional service to our customers by answering questions, resolving issues, and providing information about our products and services.

**Key Responsibilities:**

- Interact with customers via phone, email, and live chat to provide information and assistance
- Resolve customer complaints and queries in a timely and satisfactory manner
- Track and document customer interactions and transactions
- Follow-up on customer interactions when necessary
- Work closely with other departments to find solutions to customer issues
- Maintain a positive and professional demeanor to enhance customer satisfaction
- Stay updated on product and service offerings to provide accurate information to customers
- Assist in creating and updating customer service policies and procedures

**Qualifications:**

- High school diploma or equivalent; higher education or certifications in customer service are a plus
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills
- Proficiency with customer service software, databases, and tools
- Strong problem-solving and decision-making abilities
- Ability to handle stressful situations and remain calm
- Commitment to ongoing education and improvement
- Experience in a customer service role is preferred

**Work Conditions:**

- Office environment in Crompton, Rhode Island
- Standard work hours with possible shift changes based on business needs
- Interaction with a wide variety of individuals and organizations

**Physical Requirements:**

- Must be able to remain stationary for extended periods of time
- Frequently communicate with customers and colleagues in person, over the phone, and through email
