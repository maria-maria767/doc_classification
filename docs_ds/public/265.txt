**Executive Summary:**

Evolving Consulting aims to expand its market presence and brand awareness throughout the fiscal year 2023. Our marketing strategy involves leveraging new digital platforms, enhancing customer engagement, and targeting specific growth markets in telecom and finance sectors in Lecco and Cremona regions. Moreover, we plan to collaborate with prominent local influencers and businesses such as Ksl Hair Ltd and Seman Baker to boost visibility.

**Market Analysis:**

Recent research suggests that businesses in Forlì-Cesena are primed for digital transformation with a significant potential for consultative support. The growing industries in these regions present an opportunity for our consulting services to make a substantial impact.

**Marketing Objectives:**

- Increase overall brand awareness by 25% in the Lecco and Cremona regions by Q4 2023
- Develop and foster two new strategic partnerships with local industry leaders 
- Drive traffic to our newly revamped website by 40% and increase lead conversions by 15%
- Engage with the public through monthly webinars covering industry trends and insights

**Target Audience:**

Our primary target will be small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) within the telecom and finance sectors, seeking to improve their operational efficiencies and digital capabilities.

**Strategies & Tactics:**

- **Digital Marketing:**
  - Redesign and optimize the company website for user experience and lead capture
  - Implement a content marketing plan that includes SEO-optimized articles and whitepapers
  - Enhance social media presence with weekly posts and sponsored content on platforms suitable for B2B audiences
  
- **Events and Networking:**
  - Attend and sponsor local business events in selected cities to increase visibility
  - Organize quarterly networking events for local business leaders, featuring key speakers from Evolving Consulting

- **Collaborations:**
  - Partner with local influencers and businesses in the industry to promote our services
  - Launch a joint venture with Ksl Hair Ltd for a case study on operational efficiency improvements

- **Public Relations:**
  - Issue press releases highlighting new services, partnerships, and successful client case studies
  - Engage with local newspapers and business publications for feature articles

**Budget:**

An allocation of $120,000 is proposed for the marketing initiatives outlined in this plan, with quarterly evaluations to adjust spendings for optimal ROI.

**Evaluation Metrics:**

Performance will be measured against established KPIs such as website traffic, lead conversion rates, social media engagement metrics, and event attendance numbers.

**Implementation Schedule:**

- Q1 to Q4: Roll out digital marketing campaigns, commence webinars, and networking events
- Q2: Launch the new website and begin partnership discussions
- Q3 to Q4: Public relations initiatives and case study release

This marketing plan supports Evolving Consulting's strategic business goals for 2023 and sets the foundation for sustained growth in upcoming years.
