**Position Summary:**

The Customer Support Specialist is responsible for providing outstanding customer service by addressing customer inquiries and concerns with speed and professionalism. The role involves interaction with customers via phone, email, and live chat to ensure that all aspects of the customer service experience are met to the highest standards.

**Key Job Responsibilities:**

1. Respond to customer inquiries, resolve issues, and provide detailed information about products and services.
2. Maintain a high level of product knowledge to assist customers effectively.
3. Document customer interactions in the customer relationship management (CRM) system accurately.
4. Collaborate with team members to ensure a seamless customer experience.
5. Assist in creating and updating customer service procedures and policies.
6. Provide feedback on the efficiency of the customer service process and make suggestions for improvements.
7. Support customer service initiatives and promotional campaigns as needed.
8. Attend training sessions to grow knowledge base and improve customer support skills.

**Minimum Qualifications:**

- High school diploma or equivalent; college degree preferred.
- Proven customer support experience or experience in a client-facing role.
- Strong phone contact handling skills and active listening capabilities.
- Excellent communication and presentation skills.
- Ability to multitask, prioritize, and manage time effectively.
- Familiarity with CRM systems and practices.

**Physical Requirements:**

- Must be able to remain seated in a stationary position for long periods of time.
- Regular use of phone and computer systems is required.

**Work Environment:**

- Office setting with standard office equipment.
- Open-space workstations.

**Company Values:**
