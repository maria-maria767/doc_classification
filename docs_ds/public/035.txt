Autoequation Opens New Eco-Friendly Showroom in Vantaa

Vantaa, Finland – April 3, 2023 – Autoequation, a leader in the automotive industry, is excited to announce the grand opening of its latest eco-friendly car showroom located at Lumikero, Vantaa, Suomi. This new state-of-the-art facility represents Autoequation’s commitment to sustainable business practices and its dedication to providing customers with an extensive range of environmentally conscious vehicles.

The grand opening event will take place on Saturday, April 15, 2023, and will showcase some of the most innovative electric and hybrid models available on the market today. The event is open to the public, and we warmly invite the community to join us for a day of celebration, test drives, and informative sessions about green transportation solutions.

“Our new Vantaa showroom is a testament to Autoequation’s pledge to promote sustainable transportation,” said Bozan Czajkowski Tečomir, CEO of Autoequation. “We are thrilled to invite the local community to explore our newest electric and hybrid vehicles that not only deliver outstanding performance but also contribute to reducing our environmental footprint."

Event Details:
- Date: April 15, 2023
- Time: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM EEST
- Location: Autoequation Showroom, Lumikero, Vantaa, Suomi
- Activities: Vehicle exhibits, test drives, Q&A sessions with Autoequation experts

Special Offers:
Visitors can take advantage of exclusive grand opening promotions, including special financing rates, discounts on vehicle accessories, and a sweepstakes drawing to win a weekend experience with the latest electric vehicle model.

For more information about the grand opening, preview of vehicles, and directions to the showroom, please contact Stefanie at z-w60@li.website or call our customer service line at 0800 11 79.

We look forward to welcoming you to our new showroom and helping you discover the perfect sustainable vehicle for your lifestyle.

About Autoequation:
Autoequation is a global automobile manufacturer and dealer specializing in eco-friendly and innovative transportation solutions. With a presence in over 30 countries, our mission is to drive the future of mobility by making it cleaner, safer, and more accessible for everyone.
