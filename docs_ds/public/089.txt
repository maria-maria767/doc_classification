**Purpose:**

The purpose of this Health and Safety Document ("the Document") is to provide guidelines and procedures to ensure the health and safety of all employees, visitors, and contractors at Sys Softwaresysteme GmbH. This Document has been developed in compliance with the relevant health and safety regulations and is intended to minimize risks associated with workplace activities.

**Scope:**

This Document is applicable to all operations and activities at the premises of Sys Softwaresysteme GmbH located at Via Giovanni Paisiello Rd., Acerra, Campania state, and all company personnel, including full-time, part-time employees, contract workers, and visitors.

**Responsibilities:**

- All employees are responsible for understanding and adhering to the guidelines and procedures outlined in this Document.
- Supervisors and managers are responsible for enforcing health and safety protocols and providing training to their teams.
- Health and Safety Officers are responsible for periodic reviews of this document and implementing updates as required by law or internal policy changes.

**Health and Safety Guidelines:**

1. **General Workplace Safety:**
   - Keep work areas clean and free from hazards.
   - Immediately report any unsafe conditions or accidents to a supervisor.
   - Use safety equipment as required for specific tasks.
   - Follow emergency evacuation procedures posted near building exits.

2. **Personal Protective Equipment (PPE):**
   - PPE must be worn in designated areas or while performing specific tasks as per the job safety analysis.
   - Training on the correct use of PPE will be provided, and it must be properly maintained.

3. **Fire Safety:**
   - Know the location of fire extinguishers and how to use them.
   - Never block fire doors or emergency exits.
   - Participate in fire drills and be aware of the primary and secondary evacuation routes.

4. **First Aid:**
   - Familiarize yourself with the location of first aid kits and how to use their contents.
   - First aid training is provided to select employees; a list of first aid certified personnel is available at the HR department.

5. **Chemical Safety:**
   - Handle chemicals only if you have received the appropriate training.
   - Use required PPE when handling chemicals.
   - Follow proper storage and disposal procedures for hazardous materials.

6. **Ergonomics:**
   - Arrange workstations for comfort and to prevent strain or injury.
   - Take regular breaks to minimize the risk of repetitive strain injuries.

**Emergency Contacts:**

- In case of an immediate threat to health or safety: Dial local emergency number.
- Non-emergency health and safety concerns or inquiries: Contact the Health and Safety Officer at Sys Softwaresysteme GmbH or email v-perez2@evans.store.

**Training and Communication:**

All new employees will receive orientation on this Document as part of their induction training. Regular refresher training will also be provided. Updates to this Document will be communicated via email and posted on the company bulletin board.

**Document Maintenance:**

This Document will be reviewed annually or following any significant changes to workplace infrastructure or health and safety legislation. The latest version of this Document will always be accessible to employees.
