Policy and Procedure: Customer Complaint Resolution

Introduction:
At Themaverde, we value our customers and strive to provide the best possible service. However, we understand that sometimes things can go wrong, and we want to make sure that we have a process in place to handle customer complaints in a timely and effective manner. This policy and procedure outlines the steps we will take to resolve customer complaints and ensure that our customers are satisfied with our service.

Policy:

1. All customer complaints will be taken seriously and treated with the utmost respect.
2. We will respond to all customer complaints within 24 hours of receipt.
3. Our customer service team will work to resolve the complaint as quickly as possible, and keep the customer informed of the progress.
4. If the complaint cannot be resolved immediately, we will provide the customer with a timeline for when they can expect a resolution.
5. If the complaint requires escalation, it will be escalated to the appropriate department or manager for further investigation.

Procedure:

1. When a customer complaint is received, it will be logged into our customer service system with the following information:
* Customer name
* Contact information (email, phone number, address)
* Nature of the complaint
* Date and time of receipt
2. A customer service representative will contact the customer within 24 hours to acknowledge receipt of the complaint and provide an expected resolution timeline.
3. If necessary, the customer service representative will escalate the complaint to the appropriate department or manager for further investigation.
4. The customer will be kept informed of the progress of the complaint resolution, and will receive a final response from our customer service team within 72 hours of the initial complaint.
5. If the complaint cannot be resolved to the customer's satisfaction, the customer service representative will offer alternative solutions, such as a refund or replacement product.
6. All customer complaints will be reviewed on a regular basis to identify trends and areas for improvement in our service.
