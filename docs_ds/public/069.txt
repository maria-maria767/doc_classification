**PROPOSAL FOR STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIP BETWEEN SEA LAKE YACHTS, LLC & VIVAMOREM ENTERTAINMENT**

---

**Executive Summary**

Sea Lake Yachts, LLC is a company that prides itself on delivering exquisite boating experiences to our esteemed clientele. Located at 222 Goodwill Road, Montgomery, New York 12549, United States of America, we have established a reputation for excellence in the luxury boating industry. Vivamorem Entertainment, a prominent player in the entertainment industry, based in Rimini, Emilia-Romagna, stands out for creating unforgettable events and experiences.

This proposal aims to establish a strategic partnership between Sea Lake Yachts, LLC and Vivamorem Entertainment to combine our mutual strengths and enhance our market offerings.

**Objective of Partnership**

1. To provide exclusive yacht-based entertainment and event services to clientele seeking unique and luxurious experiences.
2. To expand market reach for both companies by leveraging each other's customer base and marketing channels.
3. To create synergy between Sea Lake Yachts' expertise in boating services and Vivamorem Entertainment's flair for creating extraordinary events.

**Partnership Details**

Sea Lake Yachts, LLC will provide a fleet of luxury yachts, alongside captains and crew, to serve as the venues for events organized by Vivamorem Entertainment.

Vivamorem Entertainment will cater to planning, production, and execution of entertainment events on board Sea Lake Yachts, utilizing their extensive expertise in the realm of high-profile event management.

**Proposed Terms**

- Joint marketing campaigns to promote yacht events.
- Revenue sharing from events hosted on Sea Lake Yachts, with a negotiable percentage.
- Exchange of services, wherein Sea Lake Yachts provides the venue, and Vivamorem Entertainment manages the event without direct exchange of funds.
- Commitment to a pilot period of one year, with the option to extend the agreement based on performance metrics.
