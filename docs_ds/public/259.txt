Alia Wheat Announces Community Collaboration Event

Vantaa, Suomi – Alia Wheat, a recognized leader in sustainable agricultural practices, is excited to announce its upcoming Community Collaboration Event. The event aims to engage the public in discussions around environmentally friendly farming and to showcase the latest in ecological agriculture technologies.

**Date:** Saturday, April 15, 2023  
**Time:** 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM  
**Location:** Lumikero 10, Vantaa, Suomi  
**Admission:** Free to the public

The day's activities will include guided tours of Alia Wheat’s pioneering wheat fields, interactive seminars with our expert agronomists, and a variety of informative booths hosted by Autoequation and Marilyn, our partners in sustainable agriculture. This family-friendly event will also feature hands-on activities for children, live music, and a farmers’ market where local produce will be available for purchase.

Mekhi Cain Rains, Lead Agronomist at Alia Wheat, will deliver the keynote address, discussing the importance of community involvement in sustainable farming practices. Additionally, Bozan Czajkowski Tečomir, Environmental Consultant, will be hosting a workshop on "Conservation Techniques for the Home Gardener."

We welcome all residents of Vantaa and the surrounding areas to join us in our efforts to promote a greener future. This event underscores Alia Wheat's commitment to an open dialogue with the public about the role of modern agriculture in environmental stewardship.

For more information about the Community Collaboration Event, please email z-w60@li.website or call 0800 11 79.

We look forward to welcoming you to a day filled with learning, fun, and community building.

**About Alia Wheat:**  
Alia Wheat is dedicated to advancing sustainable and innovative agricultural practices. With numerous projects aimed at reducing environmental impact, Alia Wheat actively collaborates with local communities and global partners to create a more sustainable and food-secure future.
