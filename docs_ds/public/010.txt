### Sunshine Tech, Inc.  
**Annual General Meeting Minutes**

**Date:** March 24, 2023  
**Time:** 10:00 AM  
**Location:** Sunshine Tech Headquarters, 123 Solar Ave, Sunnyville, CA

**Attendees:**  
- CEO: John Smith  
- CFO: Linda Gold  
- COO: Michael Johnson  
- Various shareholders and employees (List in appendix A)

**Meeting Agenda:**  

1. **Opening Remarks**  
CEO John Smith welcomed all participants and thanked them for their dedication to the company over the previous fiscal year.

2. **Review of Annual Financial Report**  
CFO Linda Gold presented the financial report, highlighting an increase in revenue by 20% and a net profit growth of 15%. Detailed financial statements were provided in the handouts. 

3. **Strategic Plan for Upcoming Year**  
COO Michael Johnson outlined the strategic plan, which includes expanding into the European market, launching two new products, and increasing R&D investment by 30%.

4. **Election of Board Members**  
The following individuals were elected to serve on the Board of Directors for a term of 3 years:  
   - Emily Chang
   - Rajiv Gupta
   - Henry Rodriguez

5. **Q&A Session**  
An open question and answer session took place where shareholders and employees could address the board and executives. Key questions and responses are documented in Appendix B.

6. **Closing Remarks**  
CEO John Smith emphasized the company's commitment to sustainable growth, corporate responsibility, and shareholder value. He concluded the meeting with a positive outlook for the current fiscal year.

**Minutes were taken by:**  
Sophia Martinez, Secretary  

**Approved by:**  
John Smith, CEO  
Date of Approval: March 25, 2023
