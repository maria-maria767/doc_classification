LEGAL BRIEF

Case No.: 23-1A47B
Court: North Kingstown Civil Court
Plaintiff: Dolphin Supply Inc.
Defendant: Hickey Enterprises
Date: April 7, 2023

I. INTRODUCTION

Dolphin Supply Inc. (plaintiff), represented by Tsafaroff Legal Group, submits this brief in support of its motion for summary judgment against Hickey Enterprises (defendant), regarding the breach of contract that occurred on January 12, 2023. The contract in dispute involves the supply of marine hardware that the defendant failed to deliver in accordance with the agreed terms.

II. STATEMENT OF FACTS

On November 5, 2022, Dolphin Supply Inc., with its principal office located at 483 Forge Road North Kingstown, Rhode Island 02852, entered into a contractual agreement with Hickey Enterprises, whereby Hickey Enterprises agreed to deliver a consignment of marine hardware no later than December 30, 2022. However, Hickey Enterprises failed to deliver the goods by the stipulated date or provide sufficient reason for the delay.

III. ARGUMENT

The contractual agreement between Dolphin Supply Inc. and Hickey Enterprises constitutes a valid and enforceable contract. Dolphin Supply Inc. fulfilled all its obligations under the terms of the contract. Hickey Enterprises' failure to deliver the goods by the agreed date constitutes a breach of contract. Hence, Dolphin Supply Inc. is entitled to damages as compensation for the losses sustained due to the defendant's non-performance.

IV. CONCLUSION

In light of the foregoing, Dolphin Supply Inc. respectfully requests that the Court grant the motion for summary judgment in its favor, award damages as stipulated in the contract, and cover any additional relief deemed just and proper by this honorable Court.
