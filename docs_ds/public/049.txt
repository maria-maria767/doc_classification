**Job Title:** Junior Marketing Specialist

**Department:** Marketing

**Reports To:** Marketing Manager

**Location:** 75 Abbott Street, Providence, Rhode Island 02906, US

**Organization:** Brighton Wray

**About Brighton Wray:**
Brighton Wray is an innovative company at the forefront of the marketing industry. We specialize in delivering cutting-edge marketing strategies and are committed to growing brands and driving sales. Our dynamic team consists of creative thinkers, strategic planners, and marketing enthusiasts who are passionate about delivering exceptional results for our clients.

**Position Summary:**
The Junior Marketing Specialist will join our energetic team and support various marketing initiatives to enhance brand awareness and contribute to the overall success of our client campaigns. The ideal candidate will be an adaptable team player with a keen eye for details and creativity.

**Key Responsibilities:**
- Assist in the development and execution of marketing plans and campaigns
- Collaborate with the marketing team to create and distribute content across various platforms, including social media, email, and websites
- Analyze market trends and customer insights to identify opportunities for growth
- Support event planning and execution, including trade shows, webinars, and other promotional events
- Communicate with clients, partners, and vendors to maintain strong working relationships
- Monitor and report on the performance of marketing initiatives
- Contribute to the maintenance of databases and marketing collateral
- Participate in brainstorming sessions to develop new and innovative marketing strategies
- Perform administrative tasks as required to support the marketing team

**Qualifications:**
- Bachelor’s degree in marketing, business, or a related field
- Basic understanding of marketing principles and strategies
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
- Proficiency with social media platforms and Microsoft Office Suite
- Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment
- Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
- Willingness to learn and adapt to new challenges
- Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines
