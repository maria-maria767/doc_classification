**Alia Wheat Expands Its Operations to Forlì-Cesena**

Via Antonio Vivaldi, Imola, Emilia-Romagna: Alia Wheat, a leader in sustainable agricultural product distribution is proud to announce the expansion of its operations with a new branch opening in the thriving agricultural hub of Forlì-Cesena. This move supports Alia Wheat's commitment to supporting local farmers and providing communities with high-quality, sustainably sourced agricultural products.

**About Alia Wheat**
Alia Wheat has been a trusted name in the agricultural industry for over a decade, focusing on innovation, sustainability, and community partnership. Our mission is to bring nutritious, responsibly sourced products to tables around the world while supporting the environment and the hardworking farmers who are at the heart of our business.

**New Location Details**
Address: Via Antonio Vivaldi, Imola, Emilia-Romagna
Phone: +02 21 6336-0954

The Forlì-Cesena location will serve as a strategic distribution point, allowing Alia Wheat to better connect with local farmers, streamline our supply chain, and deliver fresh products to our customers more efficiently. The new facility is slated to open in Q3 2023.

Stefanie Bozan Czajkowski Tečomir, Alia Wheat's Director of Operations for the Emilia-Romagna region, says, "We are excited about the opportunities this expansion into Forlì-Cesena presents. By being closer to our agricultural partners, we can continue our commitment to local economies and maintain the high standards our customers expect from Alia Wheat."

**Community Involvement**
Alia Wheat believes in the power of community and has a long-standing history of engaging with local initiatives and charities. As part of our expansion, we are also launching the Alia Wheat Fund for Sustainable Agriculture, which will invest in community projects and education programs to promote sustainable farming practices in Forlì-Cesena and surrounding areas.
