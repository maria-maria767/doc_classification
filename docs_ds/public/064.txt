Title: Themaverde Email Communication Policy

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Email Communication Policy is to ensure that electronic mail (email) is used in a professional, lawful, and efficient manner by all employees of Themaverde. Given the public nature of this policy, it highlights the accountability of employees within the organization to use the provided communication tools responsibly.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees of Themaverde, including full-time, part-time, temporary staff, and contractors who have been provided with an email account and access to the corporate email system.

3. Policy

A. Email Usage:
   - Email services provided by Themaverde are primarily for business purposes. Limited and reasonable personal use is permitted if it does not interfere with work performance or negatively impact the company’s resources.
   - All outgoing emails must include a professional signature with the employee’s full name, position, and contact details, including Themaverde’s address: 183 Main Street, Chatham, Massachusetts 02733, us, and the main office phone number: 0241852848.
   - Employees are responsible for managing their email accounts, which includes periodically reviewing and purging emails that are no longer necessary for business purposes.
   
B. Security and Confidentiality:
   - Confidential company information should never be sent via email to unauthorized recipients, internally or externally.
   - If confidential information needs to be shared as part of legitimate business operations, appropriate authorization and secure means of communication should be used.
   - Employees must exercise caution when opening emails and attachments from unknown sources to prevent malware and phishing attacks.

C. Acceptable Content:
   - Emails must not contain content that could be considered offensive, abusive, discriminatory, or otherwise inappropriate.
   - All communications should be respectful and reflect Themaverde’s commitment to professionalism and ethical standards.

D. Prohibited Use:
   - Employees may not use the email system for any unlawful or prohibited activities, including but not limited to:
      - Sending unsolicited commercial messages or spam.
      - Conducting private business or personal advertising.

E. Monitoring:
   - Themaverde reserves the right to monitor all email communications and to audit email content to ensure compliance with this policy. However, the company will respect the privacy of personal communications where identified and when not infringing upon business operations or when not required by law.

4. Procedures

A. Reporting Misuse:
   - Any employee who suspects misuse of the email system must report the concern to their direct supervisor or to the IT department at s-snyder1@carey-hernandez.website.
   
B. Email Retention:
   - Employees must adhere to Themaverde’s email retention procedures, which require business-related emails to be retained for a period of three years before deletion, unless specified otherwise by legal requirements.

5. Policy Enforcement

Any employee found to be in violation of this Email Communication Policy may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment. Compliance with this policy is mandatory for all employees.

6. Policy Review

This policy shall be reviewed annually or as needed to ensure that it remains comprehensive, appropriate, and compliant with all applicable laws and regulations.

Themaverde acknowledges and respects the diverse use of email as a vital communication tool and encourages all employees to adhere to this policy to maintain a professional and secure working environment.
