**Natco Transport Expands Its Operations to New Locations**

Hudson, United States of America – March 1, 2023 – Natco Transport, a recognized leader in the logistics and transportation industry, is pleased to announce its expansion of operations to new locations across America and overseas, including dynamic markets in Australia and India.

Angel Kovasev Zdravko, CEO of Natco Transport, says, "Our commitment to providing exceptional service and value to our customers remains our top priority. This expansion is a testament to our team's hard work and the trust our customers place in us." 

**New Locations Opening:**

- Raymond Street, Hudson, United States of America
- Am Treutengraben, Frankfurt am Main, Deutschland
- Key business centers in Australia
- Major economic zones in India

Each new facility is strategically placed to support the growing demand for efficient transportation services and provides a full range of logistics solutions.
