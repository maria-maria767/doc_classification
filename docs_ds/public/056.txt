Craftmatch HR Partners with Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda to Enhance Recruitment Solutions

ALBANY, OR – March 13, 2023 – Craftmatch HR, a leading human resource company, is proud to announce its strategic partnership with Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda. This collaboration is set to revolutionize the recruitment process for businesses in Albany and beyond.

Craftmatch HR, known for their innovative approach to connecting talented professionals with the companies that need them, is aligning with Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda's expertise in providing tailored business solutions. Together, they are set to offer a comprehensive service package that will streamline recruitment and human resources management.

"We are excited to combine our forces with Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda," said Argir Dajo Matej Matea Smolković Dodik, CEO of Craftmatch HR. "Our mutual dedication to excellence and innovation will allow us to enhance our offerings to our clients and provide even more efficient and effective recruitment services."

The partnership will leverage the strengths of both companies. Craftmatch HR's expansive network of qualified candidates and cutting-edge recruitment technologies will integrate with Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda's strategic planning and business acumen.

"We look forward to this partnership paving the way for shared success," commented Filimena Tunteva Dobrochna, President of Chess Consulting & Business Strategy Ltda. "Our collaborative effort will ensure that businesses have access to the best services for nurturing and growing their talent pools."
