### PRESS RELEASE

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

Buford, GA - March 23, 2023 - Uyola Inc.

**Uyola Inc. Announces Expansion of its Operations to Buford, Georgia**

Uyola Inc., a leader in innovative interior design solutions, is proud to announce the expansion of its operations with a new branch opening in Buford, Georgia. The new location, situated at the heart of the bustling city, is expected to bring fresh design concepts and accessible services to homeowners and businesses in the region.

**Grand Opening Ceremony**

Join us for the ribbon-cutting ceremony of Uyola Inc.'s newest office at 10:00 AM on April 15, 2023. The ceremony will take place at our new office located at:

1 Uyola Way
Buford, GA

Guests will be treated to a showcase of our latest interior design projects, a tour of our new facility, and the opportunity to meet our creative team, including our renowned designers, Beth Anders and Dexter Moore. Light refreshments will be served.

**About Uyola Inc.**

Founded in 2010 by visionary designer Kennedy Annemarie Swearingen, Uyola Inc. has established itself as a top-tier provider of interior design solutions. With a dedication to sustainability and creativity, our team focuses on creating spaces that reflect the unique values and needs of each client.
