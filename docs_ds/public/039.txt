**POLICY AND PROCEDURE DOCUMENT**

**Policy Number:** PP-1001
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2023
**Last Revised:** April 1, 2023

### I. PURPOSE

The purpose of this policy and procedure document is to outline the expected conduct and operational guidelines for employees of Themaverde. This document is intended to be shared publicly and provides a comprehensive overview of company operations in alignment with our commitment to transparency and excellence in service.

### II. SCOPE

This policy applies to all employees of Themaverde, including full-time, part-time staff, and contractors. It also serves as a source of information for our clients and partners to understand our organizational practices.

### III. POLICY

It is the policy of Themaverde to:

1. Uphold the highest standards of ethical conduct and integrity in all operations.
2. Ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations in operating jurisdictions, including but not limited to the United States and New Zealand.
3. Promote a culture of respect, diversity, and inclusion within the workplace.
4. Provide clear, accurate, and timely communications to all stakeholders.

### IV. PROCEDURES

1. **Code of Conduct:** All employees must adhere to the Themaverde code of conduct, which includes but is not limited to, the safeguarding of company assets, confidential information, and the respectful treatment of fellow employees and clients.

2. **Compliance with Laws:** Employees are required to understand and comply with the laws and regulations pertaining to their job functions. Any suspected violations should be reported immediately to a supervisor or via the company's anonymous hotline.

3. **Workplace Environment:** Themaverde is committed to maintaining a safe and healthy work environment. All potential safety issues should be reported to the Workplace Safety Officer, Lizuska Hrabovskyi Dzvezdan, at (07700)900940 or via email at h.schmidt@yahoo.com.

4. **Diversity and Inclusion:** We embrace diversity and strive for an inclusive work environment. Any incident of discrimination or harassment should be reported to the HR Department for immediate investigation.

5. **Privacy and Data Protection:** Employees must follow all procedures related to the protection of personal and sensitive information of clients and personnel, as detailed in the Themaverde Data Privacy Policy.

6. **Client Communications:** Employees should provide clear, accurate, and professional communications to clients. Any official correspondence can be directed to 183 Main Street, Chatham, Massachusetts 02733, US, or through our customer service email, juliusrogers769@hotmail.com.

### V. RESPONSIBILITIES

All employees are responsible for understanding and following the policies and procedures outlined in this document. Supervisors and managers are additionally responsible for enforcing these guidelines and providing clarification when needed.

### VI. ENFORCEMENT

Violations of company policies and procedures may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

### VII. REVISION HISTORY

This policy may be revised or updated periodically to reflect changes in legislation, organizational processes, or industry standards. Any such changes will be communicated to all employees accordingly.
