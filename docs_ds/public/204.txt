Job Title: Community Engagement Coordinator

Department: Communications and Public Relations

Reporting to: Director of Communications

Location: 209 Wachusett Street, Boston, Massachusetts

Company: Brighton Wray

About Brighton Wray:
Brighton Wray is a dynamic and community-oriented organization committed to enhancing public engagement and fostering strong community relationships. We pride ourselves on being an inclusive workplace, where every team member plays a vital role in our mission to serve the community effectively.

Job Summary:
The Community Engagement Coordinator is responsible for developing and implementing community outreach programs, creating engagement opportunities, and building sustainable relationships between Brighton Wray and the local community. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of community dynamics, excellent communication skills, and a passion for public service.

Key Responsibilities:
- Develop and execute strategies to enhance community awareness of Brighton Wray's initiatives and programs.
- Organize and coordinate public events, workshops, and meetings to promote community involvement.
- Build and maintain partnerships with local organizations, community leaders, and stakeholders.
- Act as the liaison between Brighton Wray and the community, ensuring effective communication and feedback loops.
- Manage social media platforms and other communication channels to maximize community engagement.
- Create informative materials such as newsletters, brochures, and press releases.
- Assist in the coordination of volunteer activities and community service projects.
- Monitor and report on the effectiveness of community engagement efforts.

Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Communication, Public Relations, Marketing, or a related field.
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in community engagement, public relations, or event management.
- Proficient in the use of social media and digital communication tools.
- Outstanding written and verbal communication skills.
- Strong organizational abilities and attention to detail.
- Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment.
- Passion for making a positive impact in the community.

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and benefits package.
- A supportive and collaborative work environment.
- Opportunities for professional growth and development.
- The fulfillment of making a tangible difference in the community.
