### 1. PURPOSE

To establish a standard of conduct and dress that promotes a professional image and is conducive to a productive and respectful work environment for all employees at [Organizations Name, e.g., Seratek Manufacturing Inc.]. This policy applies during normal business operations and at company-sponsored events.

### 2. SCOPE

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and visitors of [Organizations Name, e.g., Seratek Manufacturing Inc.] and is intended for public knowledge to ensure transparency in our workplace standards.

### 3. POLICY

#### 3.1 Office Conduct

- Employees are expected to conduct themselves in a manner that is professional, courteous, and respectful to both colleagues and clients.
- The use of company property and assets must be for legitimate business purposes and with utmost care.
- All forms of harassment, discrimination, and bullying are strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.

#### 3.2 Dress Code

- Employees are required to dress in business casual attire unless otherwise directed for specific roles or events.
- Clothing must be clean, neat, and appropriate for a professional environment.
- Safety gear must be worn at designated times or in specified areas as required for certain roles.

### 4. PROCEDURE

#### 4.1 Reporting Misconduct

- Any incidents of misconduct or policy violation should be reported to direct supervisors or Human Resources.
- Reports can be made via the provided contact channels [(e.g., Email: h.schmidt@yahoo.com) or Phone: (07700) 900940].

#### 4.2 Dress Code Exceptions

- Requests for exceptions to the dress code for religious or medical reasons should be directed to Human Resources.
- HR will evaluate the request and provide accommodations in accordance with applicable laws and company policy.

### 5. ENFORCEMENT

- Supervisors are responsible for enforcing this policy and are empowered to address violations through verbal warnings, written reprimands, or other disciplinary actions deemed appropriate.
- Repeated violations may result in further disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

### 6. DISTRIBUTION

This policy will be distributed widely to all employees and will be available on the company website and intranet for reference. Hard copies can also be obtained from the Human Resources department upon request.
