Product Specification: Eyewear Case

Product Description:
The Eyewear Case is a stylish and functional case designed to protect and store your eyewear. It is made of high-quality materials to ensure durability and long-lasting use. The case has a compact size, making it easy to carry with you wherever you go.

Features:

* Soft, velvety lining to prevent scratches and damage to your eyewear
* Magnetic closure for easy access and secure storage
* Multiple compartments to store additional accessories such as cleaning cloths, lenses, and cords
* Lightweight and portable design for on-the-go use
* Available in various colors to suit your personal style

Specifications:

* Dimensions: 12.5 x 7.5 x 2 cm
* Weight: 0.15 kg
* Material: High-quality synthetic leather and metal
* Color: Black, Brown, Blue, Red, Green, Pink

Safety Information:
This product is not intended for children under 3 years of age. Please ensure that all small parts are kept out of reach of children.

Note: The Eyewear Case is compatible with most eyewear sizes and shapes. However, please note that the case may not fit all eyewear frames due to their unique shapes or sizes.
