III. FACTS

The plaintiff, Sally, entered into a contractual agreement with the defendant, Dolphin Supply, on July 5th, 2022, for the purchase of maritime navigation equipment. The plaintiff alleges that the defendant failed to deliver the equipment that met the standards laid out in the contract, which caused significant financial losses and operational delays.

IV. LEGAL POSITION OF SALLY

The plaintiff contends that Dolphin Supply has breached the contract by not providing equipment that adheres to the agreed-upon specifications. The breach of contract has resulted in loss of business and harm to the plaintiff's reputation. Sally seeks restitution for the losses incurred and demands the fulfillment of the original contract terms, or appropriate compensation for the damages.

V. LEGAL POSITION OF DOLPHIN SUPPLY

The defendant argues that all terms of the contract were met in good faith and the equipment supplied was in accordance with the specifications set out in the contract. Dolphin Supply further asserts that any deficiencies in the equipment were due to improper use or handling by the plaintiff's employees.

VI. ISSUE TO BE DETERMINED

The legal issue to be determined is whether Dolphin Supply has breached the contract by failing to provide equipment that meets the agreed-upon specifications and if so, the extent of the damages owed to Sally.
