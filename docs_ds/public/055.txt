THK America, Inc. Announces Opening of New Facility in Forlì-Cesena

FORLÌ-CESENA, March 11, 2023 – THK America, Inc., a leader in the industrial motion control industry, is proud to announce the grand opening of its brand new manufacturing facility in the vibrant city of Forlì-Cesena. This expansion is part of THK's ongoing effort to offer the highest quality products and services to its European clientele.

The new state-of-the-art facility, located at Via delle Industrie, Forlì-Cesena, represents a significant investment in the region and is expected to create numerous job opportunities for local residents. The factory is equipped with the latest technologies that underscore THK's commitment to innovation and sustainability.

The ceremonious opening event is scheduled for April 15, 2023, at 10 AM, and will include a ribbon-cutting ceremony, a facility tour, and a reception. Local dignitaries, members of the press, and industry leaders are invited to attend and share in this momentous occasion.

"We are thrilled to open our doors in Forlì-Cesena and become an integral part of the local community," said Radik Kratosvil Mario, Regional Director of THK America, Inc. "Our investment here reinforces THK's promise to deliver excellence and to foster relationships with our European partners and customers."

For more details about the facility and the grand opening event, please contact Olga Jankovič Ellen at scann@shepard-reed.com or call (051) 8567-7487.

About THK America, Inc.
THK America, Inc. is part of the THK Group, which was founded in 1971 and is renowned for its innovative linear motion systems that support the advancement of modern industry. THK products are integral components in a wide variety of machines and systems in industries ranging from automotive to aerospace.
