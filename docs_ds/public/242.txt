Softway Medical Announces Expansion to New Location in Santa Paula, CA
  
Santa Paula, CA – April 15, 2023 – Softway Medical, a leading provider of innovative medical software solutions, is excited to announce the opening of a new office located at 299 Pines Bridge Road, Town of New Castle, New York 10562, us. This strategic expansion is in response to the company's growing client base and increased demand for its services in the United States.

The new state-of-the-art facility will serve as a hub for U.S. operations and customer support, reaffirming Softway Medical's commitment to providing exceptional service and support to its customers. The expansion also means new job opportunities in the area, and the company looks forward to becoming an active member of the Santa Paula community.

"Expanding our presence in the U.S. with a new office in Santa Paula is a thrilling step for Softway Medical," said CEO Velizar Cisek Kostya. "We're proud to continue our growth and are committed to investing in local talent to meet the needs of our customers."

The office inauguration event will take place on May 10, 2023, and will include a ribbon-cutting ceremony, office tours, and presentations from key executives, including Velizar Cisek Kostya. Members of the press, healthcare industry professionals, and the general public are invited to attend the event and learn more about Softway Medical's vision for the future.

For more information about the event and Softway Medical's services, please contact Marketing Director Malčkiankov at macthompson@jones.store or via phone at (051) 8567-7487.

About Softway Medical:
Softway Medical is a global healthcare IT company that specializes in the development and distribution of cutting-edge medical software. With its robust portfolio of products, including electronic health records, telemedicine, and data analysis tools, Softway Medical is dedicated to enhancing patient care and improving healthcare outcomes.
