Memorandum

To: All Department Heads
From: Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
Date: April 1, 2023
Subject: Revised Budget Allocations for Q3

Attached to this memo, you will find the revised budget allocations chart for the upcoming third quarter. This document contains sensitive financial information intended for internal planning and readjustment purposes only. The revised budget figures have been updated to reflect the current economic forecast and recent changes in our company's financial status, as well as to accommodate for the strategic initiatives we agreed upon during the last board meeting.

Key Changes:
1. An overall budget increase of 4.5% across all departments to account for inflation adjustments.
2. Additional funds have been allocated to the R&D department to support the accelerated timeline of the new product development cycle.
3. Marketing budget has been increased to boost the upcoming campaign for our new product launch.

Please review the attached document and prepare to discuss its impacts on your department's operations in the upcoming budget meeting scheduled for April 15, 2023. Department Heads are expected to submit a revised spending plan to the Finance Department no later than April 22, 2023, reflecting these changes.

It is imperative that the details of these budget allocations remain confidential until official announcements are made. Unauthorized disclosure of this information could negatively impact our competitive advantage and investor relations.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this important matter.
