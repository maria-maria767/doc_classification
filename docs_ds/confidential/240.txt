**Purpose:**

The purpose of this memorandum is to outline the newly revised strategy for project allocation within our organization, which is intended to streamline our operations and better match project demands with departmental expertise.

**Background:**

[A brief explanation of the previous project allocation process and its inefficiencies; to be shared only within the need-to-know base.]

**Strategy Overview:**

- **Resource Assessment**: Departments are required to submit a detailed report of available resources and expertise by [Specific Date].
- **Project Bidding Process**: Instead of direct assignments, departments will now participate in a bidding process, proposing their strategies and resource allocation plans for new projects.
- **Centralized Project Management Office (PMO)**: A new PMO will oversee the bidding process, evaluate proposals, and assign projects based on best fit and strategic value.
- **Allocation Transparency**: The PMO will provide feedback to all departments on decision-making to promote transparency while maintaining confidentiality on project specifics.
- **Realignment of Human Resources**: This includes potential inter-department transfers to optimize project outcomes.

**Immediate Actions:**

1. Each department head is responsible for conducting a full review of their department's current projects and resources.
2. Prepare and submit the aforementioned detailed resource report to the PMO.
3. Attend a compulsory briefing session on [Date & Time], at [Location], to gain complete understanding of the new bidding process and PMO functions.
