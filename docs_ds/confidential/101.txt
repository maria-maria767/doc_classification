Subject: Proposal for Partnership on Advanced Encryption Algorithms for Video Streaming Services

Dear Mr. Rostislav Perun Dimše,

I am writing this proposal on behalf of Cypher.tv to express our interest in forming a strategic partnership with Vbg Engineering - India. Our objective is to co-develop advanced encryption algorithms specifically tailored for video streaming services, thus enhancing the security of digital content in the ever-evolving cyberspace.

1. Introduction
Cypher.tv is a leading enterprise in digital security, specializing in encryption technologies for multimedia content across various platforms. Recognizing the expertise of Vbg Engineering - India in software development and encryption technology, we believe a partnership would greatly benefit both our companies and the industry.

2. Purpose
The purpose of this collaboration is to integrate our collective knowledge and resources to create a top-tier encryption solution. This will reinforce our services’ security and provide our clients with confidence in our commitment to protect their content against unauthorized access and piracy.

3. Scope of Work
The scope of the proposed partnership will include research and development of a new suite of encryption algorithms, implementation of a robust security protocol for our streaming services, and the establishment of a joint task force to oversee the continual development of our security measures.

4. Terms of Proposal
- The co-developed technology will be proprietary, with shared intellectual property rights.
- Profits garnered from licensing the encryption technology to third parties will be divided equitably.
- Cypher.tv commits to provide initial funding for the research development phase, accounting for 60% of the estimated costs.
- Vbg Engineering - India will provide technical expertise and additional resources, accounting for the remaining 40%.
