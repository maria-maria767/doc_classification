Purpose:

This memorandum outlines the proposed strategic marketing plan for the third quarter of 2023. It is intended for review and discussion during our upcoming confidential marketing strategy meeting scheduled for April 15, 2023.

Goals:

1. Expand our digital marketing efforts by 35% to target new demographics in Huesca, Pavia, and Cremona.
2. Collaborate with Eric Film Productions to launch a new ad campaign centralizing on multimedia exposure, with particular reference to viral marketing tactics.
3. Increase brand partnerships by entering into two or more agreements with organizations that have a solid presence in our target cities, such as the Downtown Development Association Of Lincolnton Inc.

Strategic Initiatives:

- Leverage social media advertising, with an emphasis on platforms that show heightened engagement rates within target demographic analyses to date.
- Develop and publish engaging multilingual content designed to resonate with local cultures in Huesca, Pavia, and Cremona, adapting to regional nuances and preferences.
- Implement SEO strategies specifically tailored to these regions in order to improve our search engine rankings on localized SERPs.
- Designate special budget allocations for researching market trends in Austria and Romania, with field studies to be based at Maurer Lange Gasse, Wien, Österreich, and Strada Ferme F, Otopeni, România, respectively.

Budget Projections:

An estimated budget of €150,000 is allocated for the aforementioned initiatives. Detailed budgeting will be refined post initial feedback and subsequent meetings.
