 Introduction:
This report provides a detailed overview of sales performance for Q1 2023 at Kent Manor Inn. The report includes key metrics such as revenue, occupancy rate, and customer feedback.

Revenue:
The revenue for Q1 2023 was $1,250,000, a 10% increase from Q1 2022. This increase can be attributed to the successful implementation of marketing campaigns and the addition of new rooms.

Occupancy Rate:
The occupancy rate for Q1 2023 was 85%, slightly lower than the target of 90%. This can be attributed to increased competition in the area and the impact of the pandemic on travel.

Customer Feedback:
Customer feedback for Q1 2023 was overwhelmingly positive, with an average rating of 4.5 out of 5. Areas of improvement identified included room service and housekeeping.

Conclusion:
Overall, Q1 2023 was a successful quarter for Kent Manor Inn, with increased revenue and positive customer feedback. The occupancy rate is an area for improvement, and steps will be taken to address this in the coming quarters.
