Product Name: EcoFresh Air Purifier

Product Description:
The EcoFresh Air Purifier is a revolutionary device designed to provide clean and fresh air in residential and commercial settings. The product is equipped with a high-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filter that captures 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns. The purifier also features a pre-filter and an activated carbon filter to remove odors and volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from the air.

Product Features:

* Three-stage filtration system (pre-filter, HEPA filter, and activated carbon filter)
* Automatic shut-off when filter needs to be replaced
* Adjustable fan speed
* Quiet operation
* Energy-efficient design

Product Specifications:

* Dimensions: 20 x 15 x 5 inches
* Weight: 10 pounds
* Filter replacement indicator
* Power source: AC adapter or batteries (not included)
* Filter type: HEPA, pre-filter, and activated carbon filter
* Filter replacement interval: Pre-filter every 3 months, HEPA filter every 6-12 months, activated carbon filter every 6 months
