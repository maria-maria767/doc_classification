**MARKETING PLAN**

**Executive Summary**

This confidential marketing plan has been prepared for Multicultural Youth of South Australia (MYSA) by Zak Nixon with inputs from Barbara Stein. The goal is to outline the strategy for the promotion of MYSA within the target demographic groups in various regions, with an emphasis on cultural integration and social awareness. The plan covers demographic targeting, promotional strategies, budget allocation, and performance metrics. 

---

**Target Audience**

Our primary audience consists of multicultural youths aged 15-25 within South Australia, with a secondary audience comprising of local authorities, potential sponsors, and community leaders. We will engage them through community events, social media outreach, and school programs designed to enhance cultural understanding and unity.

---

**Promotion Strategy**

1. **Community Engagement**
   - Partner with local schools and organizations (such as Db Tuning Tecnologia) to organize workshops and cultural exchange programs.
   - Hold bi-monthly events in community centers across South Australia to promote our mission and activities.

2. **Digital Marketing**
   - Develop a robust social media presence targeting youths using platforms where they are most engaged, including Instagram and Snapchat.
   - Launch an influencer campaign to connect with our audience through figures they trust and admire.

3. **Public Relations**
   - Leverage local news outlets and community bulletins to announce events and successes.
   - Utilize press releases to highlight key milestones and stories of community impact.

4. **Collateral**
   - Produce promotional materials such as brochures, posters, and swag to distribute at events and local businesses.

---

**Marketing Budget**

- Social Media Advertising: $10,000
- Influencer Partnerships: $5,000
- Event Production: $15,000
- Collateral Material: $3,000
- Public Relations: $2,000
- Miscellaneous and Emergency Fund: $5,000
- **Total: $40,000**

---

**Performance Metrics**

- Increase in social media followers by 50% within 6 months.
- Attendance of 500+ participants in each community event.
- 25% increase in volunteer sign-ups post events.
- At least 5 features in local media outlets per quarter.
- 10,000 brochures and flyers distributed within the first two months.
