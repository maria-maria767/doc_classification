Employee Handbook

Welcome to Ski Masters Ski School! This handbook has been prepared to provide you with important information about our company and its policies. Please take the time to read it carefully and understand its contents.

1. Introduction
Pablo Mesich Bretka is the founder and CEO of Ski Masters Ski School. Kristal, Vesna Svetleasni Velizar are the other key members of the management team. Our company is headquartered at 93 Tamarack Drive, Essex, Vermont 05452, United States of America.
2. Company Culture
At Ski Masters Ski School, we value our employees and strive to create a positive work environment. We expect all employees to maintain a professional and respectful demeanor at all times.
3. Employee Policies
3.1 Work Hours
The standard workweek is 40 hours, Monday through Friday, 9 am to 5 pm. Overtime is permitted with prior approval.

3.2 Vacation Policy
Employees are entitled to 2 weeks of paid vacation per year. Vacation time cannot be carried over from one year to the next.

3.3 Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 3 days of paid sick leave per year. Additional sick leave may be granted with a doctor's note.

3.4 Bereavement Leave
Employees may take up to 3 days of paid leave in the event of a immediate family member's death.

4. Compensation and Benefits
4.1 Salary
Salaries are reviewed annually and adjusted as necessary.

4.2 Health Insurance
Ski Masters Ski School offers health insurance to all full-time employees.

4.3 Retirement Plan
Ski Masters Ski School offers a 401(k) retirement plan to all eligible employees.

1. Employee Conduct
Employees are expected to maintain a professional and respectful demeanor at all times. Discrimination or harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.
2. Use of Company Property
Company property, including equipment and facilities, must be used only for company business purposes. Personal use is strictly prohibited.
3. Confidentiality
Employees must maintain the confidentiality of company information, including customer data, financial information, and trade secrets.
4. Conclusion
We appreciate your commitment to Ski Masters Ski School and look forward to your contribution to our team. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact HR.
