Product Overview:

The Henry Smart Thermostat V2 is an advanced climate control system designed for residential and commercial use. This device offers users the ability to control and schedule their environment's temperature through intuitive interfaces and smart connectivity. With its elegant design and efficient performance, the Henry Smart Thermostat V2 aims to provide an optimal balance between comfort, energy saving, and user-friendliness.

Key Features:

- WiFi Connectivity: Enable remote access through a user-friendly mobile application.
- Learning Mode: Adapt to user preferences and schedule to optimize energy consumption.
- Voice Control Integration: Compatible with voice-activated systems like Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant.
- Multi-Zone Function: Independent control of different heating zones within the premises.
- Energy Usage Reports: Comprehensive analysis on energy consumption and suggestions for cost savings.

Technical Specifications:

- Temperature Range: 7°C - 30°C (45°F - 86°F)
- Connectivity: 802.11 b/g/n WiFi at 2.4 GHz
- Power Requirements: 24V AC (C-Wire required)
- Sensor Accuracy: +/- 0.5°C (0.9°F)
- Display: 3.5-inch color LCD with touch screen

Regulatory Compliance:

- Complies with FCC, CE, RoHS, and ENERGY STAR certifications.
