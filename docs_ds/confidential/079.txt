II. Market Analysis Summary  
The Business Strategy Unit has completed a comprehensive market analysis highlighting the target demographic, competitive landscape, and potential barriers to market entry. Key findings include an increasing demand for health-monitoring features among the 25-45 age group, a gap in the mid-tier pricing segment, and an opportunity for leveraging our brand's reputation for quality and innovation.

III. Unique Selling Proposition (USP)  
The X200 Smartwatch offers unique features not found in current competitors' products, including a patented long-life battery, a built-in UV skin protection sensor, and seamless integration with our existing product ecosystem.

IV. Sales Targets  
Our sales target for the first year post-launch is 500,000 units, with a progressive increase to reach 2 million units by the end of year three. These targets reflect an ambitious but attainable goal, based on historical sales data and projected market growth.

V. Key Sales Channels  
The product will be distributed through the following key channels:
- Online direct-to-consumer through our website
- Online marketplaces (e.g., Amazon, eBay)
- Retail partners and specialty stores
- Corporate sales program (for bulk and customized orders)
