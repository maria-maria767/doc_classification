MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING

This Memorandum of Understanding (hereinafter referred to as "MoU") is entered into on this day, [Insert Date], by and between:

Maretti Lighting, with its principal place of business at Via Pozzo Strada 19, Torino 10139, Piemonte (hereinafter referred to as "Maretti"), represented by Mrs. Hristina Kirce Dzherov Ljupka;

Viktorija Močibob Aljinović, with its principal place of business at LV-9223, Vilanova de Segrià, España (hereinafter referred to as "Viktorija Močibob"), represented by Mr. Georgios Gjuričik Hristina;

and

Terence, with its principal place of business at Carrer de Joan Miró rd. on number 62 in Deltebre, Catalunya state (hereinafter referred to as "Terence"), represented by Ms. Sukhmani Fatima Blackwell Hall.

WHEREAS, Maretti, Viktorija Močibob, and Terence (hereinafter collectively referred to as the "Parties") have expressed an interest in participating in a collaborative project relating to the development and implementation of innovative lighting solutions (the "Project");

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to define the terms of their collaboration regarding the Project and to set forth their mutual rights and obligations.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

1. Project Description:
   The Project will consist of the development, testing, and implementation of advanced lighting systems that incorporate energy-saving technologies and intelligent control systems in the regions of Brescia, Pavia, and Sondrio.

2. Terms of Collaboration:
   a. Maretti will contribute its expertise in light fixture design and manufacture.
   b. Viktorija Močibob will provide the necessary infrastructure and logistical support.
   c. Terence will handle the marketing and distribution of the developed products within the specified regions.

3. Confidentiality:
   Each Party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of any information related to the Project that is not publicly available. This includes, but is not limited to, technical specifications, market research data, and client lists.

4. Intellectual Property:
   It is understood that any intellectual property developed during the course of the Project will be jointly owned by the Parties, and any use or licensing of such intellectual property will require the consent of all Parties.

5. Contact Information:
   Should any Party need to communicate with another, the following Individuals are designated as contact points:

   - Viktorija Močibob Aljinović: tucker-goode@gonzales.info, 1297807321

This MoU represents a preliminary understanding between the Parties and is not legally binding. It may be further elaborated upon the drafting of formal agreements related to specific aspects of the Project.

This MoU may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, but all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
