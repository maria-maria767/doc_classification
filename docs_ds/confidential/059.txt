1. Background

This Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) is entered into by and between Downtown Development Association of Lincolnton Inc, a nonprofit organization with its principal place of business located at Maurer Lange Gasse, Wien, Österreich (hereinafter referred to as "DDALI") and Persepolis Marketing Limited, a private limited company with its principal place of business at Strada Ferme F, Otopeni, România (hereinafter referred to as "PML").

2. Purpose

The purpose of this MOU is to establish a cooperative relationship between DDALI and PML for the marketing and development of the downtown area of Lincolnton. This document outlines mutual goals, recognizes the scope of each party's participation, and protects the interests and confidentiality of the participating entities.

3. Terms of Agreement

a. DDALI agrees to provide PML with access to relevant data pertaining to the downtown development project including demographics, current market studies, and potential growth sectors.

b. PML agrees to create and execute a marketing strategy which will include, but not be limited to, branding, social media presence, and print media advertisements to promote the revitalization efforts led by DDALI.

c. Both parties agree to hold regularly scheduled meetings to discuss progress and to adjust strategies as necessary.

4. Confidentiality

a. The parties agree that all information disclosed during the discussions, marketing strategies, and any documents shared between DDALI and PML will be regarded as "Confidential Information."

b. Neither party will disclose Confidential Information to third parties without prior written consent of the other party.

c. This clause shall survive the termination of this MOU and shall remain in force for a period of five years from the date of termination.
