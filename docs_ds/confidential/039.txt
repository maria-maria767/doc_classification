Training Manual: Data Protection and Privacy Compliance

1. Introduction
This training manual, issued by Dhwani Infotech Ltd, is strictly for internal use and is designated CONFIDENTIAL. Its purpose is to provide comprehensive instruction on handling sensitive information and maintaining privacy compliance within our organization. This document is meant only for the employees of Dhwani Infotech Ltd and authorized associates.

2. Purpose of the Manual
The aim of this manual is to:
- Educate our workforce on the importance of data protection.
- Train employees in identifying and handling sensitive information.
- Ensure our compliance with relevant data protection laws.

3. Scope and Applicability
This manual applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party service providers of Dhwani Infotech Ltd. It covers data received from clients, including but not limited to personal details of individuals such as Eidan Margot Larson, Aleksandrova, and Kostenceva.

4. Definition of Sensitive Information
Sensitive information includes:
- Personal identifiers (e.g., SSNs, financial records, contact details)
- Client records
- Proprietary business information

5. Legal Framework
- GDPR (for data concerning EU citizens)
- HIPAA (for health-related information)
- Local data protection laws (applicable in regions such as LA MESA, CA, COLFAX, WA, or Japan)

6. Handling Sensitive Information
Employees must follow these protocols:
- Secure storage: Physical documents marked CONFIDENTIAL must be stored in a locked cabinet at La Minerie, België - Belgique - Belgien. Digital files must be encrypted and only accessible through our secure VPN.
- Minimal disclosure: Only share sensitive data on a need-to-know basis.
- Transfer of data: Use only company-approved channels when transferring sensitive information.
