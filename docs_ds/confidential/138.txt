Subject: Merger and Acquisition Proposal

Dear Executive Team,

I am pleased to present to you our proposal for the merger and acquisition of Leepson Bounds Entertainment Inc. (LBEI) by Utilityworks. This document contains confidential information that is not to be shared with any unauthorized parties.

Background:
LBEI is a leading entertainment company with a diverse portfolio of assets, including film production, music publishing, and talent management. Utilityworks is a utility services company that has been expanding its business portfolio through strategic acquisitions in recent years.

Proposal:
We propose a merger and acquisition of LBEI by Utilityworks, which will create a new entertainment division within our company. The terms of the deal are as follows:

* Utilityworks will acquire 100% of the shares of LBEI for a total of $100 million in cash.
* The management team of LBEI will be offered positions within the new entertainment division of Utilityworks.
* The existing LBEI brand and assets will be maintained and leveraged to expand the new division.

Benefits:
The merger and acquisition of LBEI will bring the following benefits to Utilityworks:

* Diversification of our business portfolio, which will reduce our reliance on the utility services sector.
* Access to LBEI's extensive network of industry contacts and talent, which will help us expand our entertainment division.
* Potential for increased revenue and profit through the growth of our entertainment division.

Next Steps:
We will now enter into negotiations with LBEI's management team and shareholders to finalize the terms of the deal. We will keep you updated on any developments and seek your input as needed.

Confidentiality:
We request that this proposal and its contents be kept confidential and not shared with any third parties without our permission.
