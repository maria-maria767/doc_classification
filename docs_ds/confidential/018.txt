Maverick Software Solutions
Expense Reimbursement Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2023
---------------------------------------------------------------

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Expense Reimbursement Policy (“Policy”) is to outline the procedures and guidelines for employees seeking reimbursement for reasonable and necessary expenses incurred in connection with approved company business.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees of Maverick Software Solutions (“Company”) who incur business-related expenses.

3. General Guidelines

- All expenses must be approved by the employee’s immediate supervisor or department manager prior to incurring the expense.
- Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of being incurred.
- Receipts are required for all expenses, except for those allowed under the per diem rates.
- Expense reimbursement requests must be submitted using the Company's standardized expense report form.

4. Types of Reimbursable Expenses

- Travel: Airfare, mileage (at the IRS standard rate), car rental, parking, taxis, and public transportation.
- Lodging: Hotel expenses for approved business travel.
- Meals: Daily allowances are set by the Company per diem rates unless actual expenses are less.
- Supplies: Business-related supplies and materials not otherwise provided by the Company.
- Communication: Business-related telephone and internet charges.

5. Non-reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses will not be reimbursed: personal items, fines for traffic violations, alcoholic beverages, expenses for family or travel companions, and any expenses not in compliance with this Policy.

6. Reimbursement Procedure

Employees must submit an expense report, with all necessary receipts attached, to their department manager for approval. Upon approval, the report will be forwarded to the Accounts Payable department for processing. Reimbursements will be made to the employee within two (2) weeks of submission if all guidelines are met.

7. Compliance

Failure to comply with this Policy may result in a delay or denial of reimbursement and possible disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

8. Amendments and Interpretation

The Company reserves the right to amend, rescind, or otherwise change this Policy without notice. The Company’s Finance Department is responsible for the interpretation of this Policy.

Please direct any questions regarding this Policy to the Accounts Payable department or your supervisor.

Maverick Software Solutions' management is committed to fair and equitable treatment of all employees in the administration of this Policy.
