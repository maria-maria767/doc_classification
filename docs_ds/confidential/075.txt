**Performance Summary:**

Georgios has continued to excel in her role as Senior Product Designer. The creativity and innovation demonstrated in the Q1 lighting designs have been pivotal to the successful launch of our latest eco-friendly lighting series. Her proactive approach in leading the design team has not only kept projects on schedule but also under budget.

**Achievements:**

- Led the 'Green Glow' project, which has been recognized by industry leaders for its sustainability and design.
- Mentored two junior designers who are now successfully managing their own design projects.
- Initiated a partnership with the design department of the University of Pavia, to foster innovation and student collaboration.

**Areas for Improvement:**

While Georgios's creative output is highly commendable, communication with the manufacturing team has been identified as an area for improvement. Ensuring that design specifications are comprehensively conveyed can enhance implementation efficiency.

**Objectives for the Next Quarter:**

- Enhance cross-departmental communication, especially with the manufacturing and marketing teams.
- Oversee the integration of customer feedback into the design process to improve user experience.
- Complete the advanced design analytics course offered by Viktorija Močibob Aljinović Institute.

**Professional Development Plan:**

To further Georgios's growth within Maretti Lighting, the following professional development plan has been proposed:
- Attend leadership training to foster team management skills.
- Encourage the adoption of new design software to improve productivity and collaboration.

**Reviewer Comments:**

"Georgios has once again positioned Maretti Lighting at the forefront of design innovation. It's imperative that we continue to support and leverage her strengths while also addressing areas crucial for our collective growth."
