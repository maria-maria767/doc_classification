PRODUCT SPECIFICATION DOCUMENT

Product Name: HenryProX100

Product Code: HPX-2023-04

1. Overview
   - The HenryProX100 is a high-performance modular computing device designed for intensive data processing and machine learning applications.
   - This device is part of the Henry Electronics advanced series and is intended for professional use in research and enterprise settings.

2. System Specifications
   - CPU: QuantumX Hyperthread Processor, 8-Core, 3.6 GHz (up to 5.2 GHz with Turbo Boost)
   - GPU: Arcane 4K Graphene-Infused Dual GPU, with 16GB of GDDR6 memory
   - RAM: 64GB DDR6 SinišaSpeed RAM, expandable up to 128GB
   - Storage: 2TB NVMe M.2 SSD with 3D XPoint Technology, expandable up to 8TB
   - OS: HenryOS 1.2 Secure Edition (Licenced for Product Lifetime)
   - Power Supply: 1200W Platinum Efficiency with backup module

3. Physical Characteristics
   - Dimensions: 450mm x 200mm x 430mm (L x W x H)
   - Weight: 12kg
   - Chassis Material: Aircraft-grade Aluminum with Nano-Carbon Thermal Coating
   - Cooling System: Lehman Vortex Liquid Cooling with AI Temperature Regulation
   - Color Options: Onyx Black, Titanium Grey, Platinum White

4. Connectivity and Ports
   - Ethernet: 10Gb Ethernor Port, Advanced Encryption Standard (AES)
   - Wireless: Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2, and Infraredbeam Transfer System (IRTS)
   - USB Ports: 4 x USB-C (Thunderbolt 4), 6 x USB 3.1 Gen 2 
   - Display Outputs: 2 x HDMI 2.1a, 2 x DisplayPort 1.4, 1 x VirtualLink
   - Audio: 7.1 Channel HD Audio with Sebastien AudioTech Interface

5. Compliance and Standards
   - Safety: IEC 60950-1:2005+A1:2009+A2:2013, UL 60950-1
   - Electromagnetic Compatibility (EMC): EN 55022:2010/AC:2011, EN 55024:2010, FCC Part 15 Subpart B Class B
   - Environmental: WEEE, RoHS, Energy Star Certification
