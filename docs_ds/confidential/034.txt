**I. Introduction**

This document outlines the proposed changes to the employee compensation plans for calendar year 2023 designed to enhance employee satisfaction and retention. The changes are informed by comprehensive market research and internal analysis of employee feedback.

**II. Changes to Base Salary Structure**

Effective from July 1, 2023, the following percentage increases will be applied to the base salary for the listed roles:

- Junior positions: 3.5% increase.
- Mid-level positions: 4.0% increase.
- Senior positions & Management: 4.5% increase.

**Scaling:** Salary adjustments are further scaled by department performance metrics, ranging from 0.5% to 2% additional increase for departments exceeding key performance indicators (KPIs).

**III. Annual Performance Bonuses**

Annual performance bonuses will now be calculated based on individual, team, and company performance indicators. A three-tiered system will be introduced:

- Tier 1 (Outstanding Performance): Up to 20% of base salary.
- Tier 2 (Above Average Performance): Up to 10% of base salary.
- Tier 3 (Meets Expectations): Up to 5% of base salary.

**IV. Equity & Incentive Programs**

- **Stock Options:** 1% additional stocks will be offered to employees at VP level and above, subject to board approval.
- **Employee Profit Sharing:** An employee profit-sharing program will be introduced, with details to be determined based on annual profits.

**V. Benefit Enhancements**

- **Healthcare:** Upgrade to the premium healthcare package at no extra cost to employees.
- **Retirement:** Increase employer 401(k) match from 4% to 6%.
- **Education:** Expansion of the tuition reimbursement program to cover up to 75% of education expenses for courses relevant to the employee's job role.

**VI. Implementation Plan**

- **Communication:** Detailed communication to be sent to all employees by April 15, 2023.
- **Training:** HR to provide training sessions on the changes, including Q&A sessions scheduled for April 20 – April 30, 2023.
- **Feedback:** A feedback window will be open from May 1 – May 15, 2023, to gather employee responses and make adjustments as necessary before final implementation.
- **Deployment:** Full deployment of the new compensation plan will be effective on July 1, 2023.
