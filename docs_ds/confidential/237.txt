MEMORANDUM

To: All Department Heads
From: Jane Doe, Chief Operations Officer
Date: March 30, 2023
Subject: Implementation of the New Work-From-Home Policy

Purpose:
To outline the upcoming changes to our work-from-home policy and detail the actions required by each department for a smooth transition.

Background:
In light of the recent employee survey results and the company’s commitment to providing a flexible work environment, it has been decided to update our current work-from-home policy. Starting May 1, 2023, eligible employees will have the option to work from home up to three days a week.

Policy Changes:
1. Eligibility: Employees must be in good standing with a minimum of six months of service to be eligible for work-from-home arrangements.
2. Approval: Department Heads are responsible for approving individual employee requests based on performance and role suitability.
3. Equipment: The company will provide a one-time stipend for necessary home office equipment up to $500. IT department will oversee the distribution of company-proprietary software required for remote work.
4. Reporting: Employees must be available during core business hours (9 am – 5 pm) and are required to log their work hours in the company's time-tracking system daily.

Action Required:
Each Department Head must:
1. Identify eligible team members and communicate the new policy to them by April 7, 2023.
2. Coordinate with the IT department to facilitate technical requirements for your team.
3. Submit a budget request for home office stipends by April 14, 2023, to the Finance Department.
4. Monitor and ensure compliance with the new work-from-home policy.

Conclusion:
This updated policy is expected to offer greater work-life balance and maintain productivity. Should you have any questions or require further guidance, please reach out to the HR Department.

Please ensure this information is kept confidential and shared only with the appropriate members of your teams.

Thank you for your attention to this matter and your cooperation in its implementation.

[End of Memorandum]
