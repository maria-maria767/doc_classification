Product Specification:

Product Name: EcoFresh Air Purifier

Product Description:
The EcoFresh Air Purifier is a revolutionary device designed to provide clean and fresh air in residential and commercial settings. The product is equipped with a high-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filter that captures 99.97% of airborne particles as small as 0.3 microns. The device also features a pre-filter and an activated carbon filter to remove odors and volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from the air.

Product Features:

* Three-stage filtration system: pre-filter, HEPA filter, and activated carbon filter
* Automatic shut-off when filter needs to be replaced
* Adjustable fan speed
* LED display to show filter status and fan speed
* Quiet operation

Product Specifications:

* Dimensions: 14.5 x 14.5 x 5.5 inches
* Weight: 10 pounds
* Electrical requirements: 110-120V, 60Hz
* Filter replacements: Pre-filter every 3 months, HEPA filter every 6 months, activated carbon filter every 12 months
