2. Product Description
The EnviroClean Air Purification System is designed to provide superior air cleaning solutions for residential and commercial settings. This high-efficiency system uses advanced HEPA filtration coupled with an innovative ionization process to remove airborne contaminants, ensuring an environment that is not only clean but also conducive to better health and well-being.

3. Key Features
- Four-stage filtration process, including pre-filter, activated carbon filter, HEPA filter, and post-filter ionization.
- Smart sensor technology that adjusts filtration speed based on the air quality.
- Quiet operation with noise levels not exceeding 35 dB.
- Energy-efficient design with an Energy Star rating.
- Easy-to-use digital display with touch controls and remote access via Wi-Fi.
- Filter replacement indicator and economical long-life filters.

4. Technical Specifications
Dimensions: 760mm x 350mm x 350mm
Coverage Area: Up to 750 sq. ft with an air change rate of 5 times per hour.
Particle Removal Efficiency: 99.97% for particles as small as 0.3 microns.
CADR (Clean Air Delivery Rate): 500m³/h
Power Consumption: Max 60W
Wi-Fi Connectivity: IEEE 802.11b/g/n (2.4 GHz)

5. Compliance Standards
- Complies with the following international environmental standards: ISO 14001, RoHS, and Energy Star.
- Meets mechanical and electrical safety standards of UL 507 and CE marking.

6. Target Market
The EnviroClean Air Purification System is aimed at consumers who suffer from allergies, respiratory conditions, or those who are health-conscious. It is equally beneficial for professionals looking for cleaner air solutions in environments such as hospitals, schools, and offices.

7. Packaging Details
Product packaging will be eco-friendly, made from recycled materials, and fully recyclable. The product will be secured within the box using molded pulp inserts to prevent damage during shipping.
