**Our Commitment**

Del Buono Gazerwitz Limited is committed to providing a professional and safe work environment. As a valued member of our team, your contributions are essential to the overall success of the company. We pledge to treat you with respect and encourage open communication to foster a positive work atmosphere.

---

**Employment Policies**

*Equal Employment Opportunity*

Del Buono Gazerwitz Limited is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other characteristic protected by law.

*Employee Conduct*

All employees are expected to conduct themselves in a professional manner at all times. This includes maintaining confidentiality, adhering to workplace ethics, and upholding the company's standards.

*Attendance and Punctuality*

Reliable attendance is vital to our operations. Employees are expected to be punctual and adhere to their scheduled work hours. If you are unable to make it to work or will be late, you are required to notify your supervisor as soon as possible.

*Harassment and Discrimination*

The company strives to maintain a workplace free of harassment and discrimination. Any form of such behavior will not be tolerated and should be reported immediately to Human Resources.

---

**Workplace Safety**

*Safety Policies*

Your safety is our priority. All employees are expected to follow safety guidelines and report any unsafe conditions to a supervisor.

*Emergency Procedures*

In the event of an emergency, it is crucial to remain calm and follow the emergency evacuation plan. Emergency exits and procedures are posted throughout the workplace.

---

**Compensation and Benefits**

*Salary*

Your compensation package has been carefully determined to be competitive and reflective of your role within the company.

*Benefits*

Eligible employees are provided with a range of benefits including health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off. Details regarding these benefits are available in the Human Resources department.

*Performance Reviews*

Regular performance reviews will be conducted to assess your work and provide constructive feedback. These reviews are also an opportunity for you to address any concerns or discuss your career aspirations.
