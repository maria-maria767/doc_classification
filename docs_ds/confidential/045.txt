SUBJECT: Implementation of New Project Management Software

As part of our ongoing efforts to improve operational efficiency and communication within our organization, we have selected ABC Project Management Software as our new comprehensive project tracking tool. This software will be mandatory for all departments to use for the planning, execution, and evaluation of projects.

Key Implementation Dates:
- Training Session 1: April 2, 2023
- Training Session 2: April 9, 2023
- Go-Live Date: May 1, 2023

Training:
Attendance is mandatory for all departmental project managers. Two sessions are offered to accommodate varying schedules. A follow-up Q&A session will be scheduled post-training.

Access Credentials:
Individual login information will be disseminated by the IT department. Please ensure all staff involved in project management attend the scheduled IT sessions.

Data Migration:
All current project data must be migrated to the ABC Project Management Software by April 21, 2023. IT will assist with data transfer from legacy systems.

Features and Benefits:
- Real-time project tracking
- Enhanced reporting capabilities
- Improved resource allocation
- User-friendly interface
- Cloud-based access for remote teams

Departmental Responsibilities:
- Assign a software implementation lead
- Ensure all project data is up to date pre-migration
- Attend training and Q&A sessions
- Facilitate team adoption and compliance

Support:
Please direct any technical questions to the IT support team at projectsupport@ourcompany.com. For implementation and usage questions, contact the PMO at pmoffice@ourcompany.com.

This software is critical to our organization's strategic goals of improving operational efficiencies and maintaining our competitive edge. Your cooperation and commitment to a smooth transition are essential.

Please keep all information regarding the implementation of the ABC Project Management Software confidential until the official company-wide announcement.
