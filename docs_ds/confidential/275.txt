**Purpose**

This memorandum is to inform you about the revised budget allocation for the upcoming quarter, Q2 2023. **Revised Budget Summary**

The following changes have been made to the budget for your departments:

3. Human Resources (HR): An increase of 2% to facilitate new hiring initiatives and staff development programs.
   
4. Operations: A reduction of 4%, reflecting efficiency improvements in the supply chain management.
