Employee Handbook

Welcome to Ski Masters Ski School! This handbook has been prepared to provide you with important information about our company, its policies and procedures, and your rights and responsibilities as an employee.

1. Introduction
Pablo Mesich Bretka is the founder and CEO of Ski Masters Ski School. Kristal is the head instructor and Vesna Svetleasni Velizar is the assistant head instructor. Our company is based in Lugo, Italy and we operate in the winter sports industry.
2. Company Policies

2.1 Anti-Discrimination Policy
Ski Masters Ski School is committed to providing a workplace that is free from discrimination and harassment. We prohibit discrimination and harassment based on race, ethnicity, national origin, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, religion, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

2.2 Employee Conduct
Employees are expected to conduct themselves in a professional and respectful manner at all times. This includes treating colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and dignity.

2.3 Employee Conduct and Responsibilities
Employees are expected to be punctual, dress appropriately, and adhere to all safety protocols. Failure to meet these expectations may result in disciplinary action.

2.4 Employee Privacy
Ski Masters Ski School respects the privacy of its employees. We do not monitor emails or phone calls unless there is a legitimate business reason to do so.

2.5 Social Media Policy
Employees are encouraged to engage with our company on social media. However, any posts that are deemed to be offensive or inappropriate may result in disciplinary action.

3. Benefits

3.1 Health and Wellness Program
Ski Masters Ski School offers a comprehensive health and wellness program to all employees. This includes access to a gym, yoga classes, and wellness workshops.

3.2 Employee Assistance Program
We also offer an employee assistance program that provides confidential counseling and support to employees dealing with personal or work-related issues.

3.3 Paid Time Off
Employees are eligible for paid time off after one year of employment. The amount of paid time off increases with length of service.

4. Conclusion
We hope that this handbook has provided you with a comprehensive overview of our company policies and procedures. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to reach out to your supervisor or HR representative.

Thank you for your dedication and hard work as a member of the Ski Masters Ski School team.

Sincerely,
Pablo Mesich Bretka
CEO, Ski Masters Ski School
