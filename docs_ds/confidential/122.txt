2. Product Overview
The ProSync 3000X is an advanced synchronization module designed for high-performance computing environments. It facilitates seamless system integration by providing precise time alignment across different computing platforms and peripheral devices.

3. Technical Specifications
- Clock Accuracy: ±500 picoseconds typical
- Network Interfaces: Dual redundant Gigabit Ethernet
- Input Voltage: 100-240V AC, 47-63Hz
- Power Consumption: 40 Watts typical
- Operating Temperature: 0°C to 50°C (32°F to 122°F)
- Storage Temperature: -40°C to 70°C (-40°F to 158°F)
- Relative Humidity: 5% to 95% non-condensing
- Time Protocol Support: NTP, PTP (IEEE 1588), and Custom Protocols
- Enclosure: Standard 19" rack mount, 1U height
