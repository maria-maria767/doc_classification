1. Introduction
This document outlines the technical specifications and safety guidelines for the SmartStream 5000X Water Filtration System. The purpose of this product is to provide high-quality, potable water through an advanced five-stage filtration process. It is designed for use in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.

2. Product Specifications
2.1. Filtration Performance
- Five-stage filtration process including pre-filter, activated carbon, reverse osmosis, UV sterilization, and post-carbon filter.
- Removes 99.99% of contaminants including bacteria, viruses, parasites, heavy metals, and chemical pollutants.
- Flow rate: 500 gallons per day at 60 psi

2.2. Physical Dimensions
- Height: 140 cm
- Width: 60 cm
- Depth: 45 cm
- Weight: 50 kg (dry)

2.3. Operating Parameters
- Minimum Inlet Water Pressure: 35 psi
- Maximum Inlet Water Pressure: 80 psi
- Optimal Temperature Range for Operation: 10°C - 35°C

2.4. Materials and Components
- Food-grade stainless steel housing
- BPA-free plastic filtration components
- UV-resistant silicone seals

2.5. Electrical Requirements
- Power Input: 110-240V AC, 50/60 Hz
- Power Consumption: 60W

2.6. Installation
- To be installed at the primary water inlet
- Requires a minimum clearance of 1 meter around the unit for maintenance

3. Compliance
- Product meets all applicable standards and regulations for water filtration systems.
- Certification: NSF/ANSI 58 for reverse osmosis systems

4. Safety Information
- To be operated by trained personnel only
- Avoid exposure to UV light from the sterilization unit
- Ensure system is depressurized before servicing
