Subject: Implementation of New Security Protocols

I. Introduction
As part of our ongoing commitment to safeguarding our company’s sensitive information and the privacy of our clients, this memorandum outlines the newly developed security protocols. All employees are required to adhere to these guidelines with immediate effect.

II. New Security Measures
We have updated our digital security infrastructure to include the following enhancements:

1. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for all company accounts.
2. End-to-end encryption for internal and client communications.
3. Regular cybersecurity training for all employees.
4. Implementation of secure VPN access for remote work.

III. Physical Security Upgrades
The physical security at our main office, located at 6 Burwell Street, Saylesville, Rhode Island 02865, us, will also be heightened:

1. Installation of biometric access control at all entry points.
2. Introduction of visitor management system with photo-ID badges.
3. Reinforcement of surveillance systems including the placement of new cameras in critical areas.

IV. Compliance and Enforcement
Staff must ensure strict compliance with these security measures. The Security Department, headed by Cvitko Jankovič Shaw, will conduct random checks to enforce compliance. Any breaches or non-compliance issues should be reported to the Security Department immediately.

V. Training Schedule
An initial training session on these protocols will be held on April 25, 2023, at our Mantova Office. Attendance is mandatory for all employees. Please confirm your availability with your department coordinator by April 18, 2023.
